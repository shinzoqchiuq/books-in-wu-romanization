## S-DU PAO-LO SIA PEH HYI-PAH-LÆ NYING GO SHÜ-SING. 使徒<u>保罗</u>写拨<u>希伯来</u>人个书信

### H. I.

DZONG-ZIN Jing-ming dzæ-ü keh-sing sin-cü-nying yüong hyü-to fông-fah le-tsʽ wô hyiang tsu-tsong dao; <sup>2</sup> tao keh-sing kyih-sah-go nyih-ts dzæ-ü *Gyi-go* Ng-ts wô hyiang ah-lah dao: *Jing-ming* z shih-lih Gyi hao tsih-djôh væn-veh tso tsʽæn-nyih, yi z tʽôh Gyi tsʽông-zao tʽin-di. <sup>3</sup> Gyi z *Jing-ming-go* yüong-wô sô tʽeo-cʽih-go kwông-tsʽæ, Jing-ming-go tʽi-tsih sô yin-cʽih weh-tʽeh-ko-go siang-mao; yiu djün-neng-go ʽao-ling tsih-công væn-veh; kyi-jün sô-cʽih Zi-go sing-ming gyiang-diao ah-lah-go ze, ziu zo læ *ting* kao *u-dông*, w̆e-nyin *Cü*-go jing-siu-pin: <sup>4</sup> tseng-jü keh-sing tʽin-s, ing-we tsih-djôh keng-kô tseng-djong-go ngæn-deo.

从前神明在于箇星先知人用许多方法屡次话向祖宗道；<sup>2</sup> 到箇星结煞个日子，在于其个儿子话向阿拉道。神明是设立其好接着万物做产业，又是托其创造天地。<sup>3</sup> 其是神明个荣华所透出个光彩，神明个体质所现出活脱过个相貌；有全能个号令执掌万物；既然舍出自个性命滰掉阿拉个罪，就坐来顶高乌荡，威严主个顺手边。<sup>4</sup> 尊如箇星天使，因为接着更加尊重个衔头。

<sup>5</sup> *Jing-ming* ʽo-dzeng teng ʽah-li ih-go tʽin-s yiu ka wô, “Ng z Ngô-go Ng-ts, Ngô kyih-mih sang Ng,” \[S. 2. 7.\] wa-yiu, “Ngô we tso Gyi Ah-tia, Gyi we tso Ngô-go Ng-ts” \[2S. 7. 14.\] ma? <sup>6</sup> <sup>\[1\]</sup>Wa-yiu *Jing-ming* ta-leh *Gyi* Tsiang-ts tseo-tsing shü-kæn-zông læ z-ʽeo, yiu ka wô, “Jing-ming-go cong tʽin-s tu kæ pa Gyi.” \[S. 97. 7.\]

<sup>5</sup> 神明何曾等何里一个天使有介话，「尔是我个儿子，我今末生尔」，\[S. 2. 7.\] 还有，「我会做其阿爹，其会做我个儿子」\[2S. 7. 14.\] 吗？<sup>6</sup> 还有神明带勒<sup>\[1\]</sup>其长子走进世间上来时候，有介话，「神明个众天使都该拜其。」\[S. 97. 7.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Yiu-teh* *fæn*, *Jing-ming* tsæ ta-læ. 有得翻，神明再带来。（原注）</sup>

<sup>7</sup> Kông-tao keh-sing tʽin-s z-ka wô, “Gyi shih-lih Gyi-go tʽin-s *kʽo-pi* *ziang* fong, *Gyi* *shih-lih* Gyi-go tsʽa-s *kʽo-pi* *ziang* ho-kwông.” \[S. 104. 4.\] <sup>8</sup> Kông-tao Ng-ts *yiu* *ka* *wô*, “Jing-ming,” Ng-go zo-we z tao shü-shü-dæ-dæ; Ng *sô* *tsih-công* koh-kô-go gyün-ping z <sup>\[1\]</sup>tsing-dzih-go gyün-ping. <sup>9</sup> Ng hwun-hyi kong-yi, u-su fi-li; keh-lah Jing-ming, *ziu-z* Ng-go Jing-ming, pô hwun-lôh-go hyiang-yiu fu Ng ko-jü Ng-go *cong* de-ho.” \[S. 45. 6, 7.\] <sup>10</sup> Yi *wô*, “Ng, Cü, z kyʽi-tsʽu lih-ding di-go keng-kyi; keh-sing tʽin yia z Ng tsʽing-siu cü-zao-go. <sup>11</sup> Gyi-lah we mih-diao, tsih-yiu Ng üong-yün dzeng-tong: gyi-lah tu we ziang i-voh ka gyiu-kyʽi-læ; <sup>12</sup> Ng we tsih gyi long ziang bao-tʽao ka, gyi ziu we pin-yih: tsih-yiu Ng z üong feh keng-kæ, Ng-go ziu-shü m-neh dzing-gyi.” \[S. 102. 25–27.\]

<sup>7</sup> 讲到箇星天使是介话，「其设立其个天使可比像风，其设立其个差使可比像火光。」\[S. 104. 4.\] <sup>8</sup> 讲到儿子又介话，「神明，尔个座位是到世世代代；尔所执掌国家个权柄是正直<sup>\[1\]</sup>个权柄。<sup>9</sup> 尔欢喜公义，恶愫非礼；箇拉神明，就是尔个神明，把欢乐个香油傅尔过如尔个众队伙。」\[S. 45. 6, 7.\] <sup>10</sup> 又话，「尔，主，是起初立定地个根基；箇星天也是尔亲手制造个。<sup>11</sup> 其拉会灭掉，只有尔永远存东；其拉都会像衣服介旧起来；<sup>12</sup> 尔会折其拢像袍套介，其就会变易。只有尔是永弗更改，尔个寿岁呒得尽其。」\[S. 102. 25–27.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng* *wô*, dzih-go. 本文话，直个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>13</sup> *Jing-ming* ʽo-dzeng teng ʽah-li ih-go tʽin-s wô, “Hao zo læ Ngô jing-siu-pin, teng-tao Ngô pô Ng-go dziu-dih tso Ng-go dah-kyiah-teng?” \[S. 110. 1.\] <sup>14</sup> Cong *tʽin-s* kyʽi feh-z voh-z *Jing-ming* go ling, *coh-kwun* be tsʽa-kyʽi we-leh keh-sing tsiang-læ iao tsih-djôh kyiu-sing go nying hao tông tsʽa-s ma?

<sup>13</sup> 神明何曾等何里一个天使话，「好坐来我顺手边，等到我把尔个仇敌做尔个踏脚凳？」\[S. 110. 1.\] <sup>14</sup> 众天使岂弗是服侍神明个灵，只管被差去为了箇星将来要接着救星个人好当差使吗？

### H. II.

Sô-yi ah-lah kæ yü-kô liu-sing sô tʽing-meng-go z-ken, kʽong-pʽô ʽôh-tsia <sup>\[1\]</sup>shih-lôh *gyi*. <sup>2</sup> Ing-we *Jing-ming* tʽôh-leh tʽin-s kông-go shih-wô kyi-jün z ih-ding-peh-yih-go, væn-pah we-nyih feh i-jing z-ken tu iao teh-djôh tsing-dzih-go pao-ing; <sup>3</sup> *hwông-tsʽia* ka do-go kyiu-sing, ah-lah ziah feh kyʽi tsʽæ gyi, dza neng-keo bi-leh-ko ni? Keh-go *kyiu-sing* z tʽôh-leh Cü kông kyʽi-deo go, keh-sing tʽing-meng cü-kwu kyʽüôh-kyʽüôh-jih-jih wô hyiang ah-lah dao; <sup>4</sup> Jing-ming tsiao Gyi-zi-go ts-i yüong gyi-z, ziao-deo, yiu-ho ʽao-su jing-tsih, teng <sup>\[2\]</sup>Sing-Ling-go eng-s, dô-kô tso te-tsing.

所以阿拉该愈加留心所听闻个事干，恐怕或者失落其<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>2</sup> 因为神明托勒天使讲个说话既然是一定不易个，凡百违逆弗依顺事干都要得着正直个报应；<sup>3</sup> 况且介大个救星，阿拉若弗去睬其，咋能够避勒过呢？箇个救星是托了主讲起头个，箇星听闻主顾确确实实话向阿拉道；<sup>4</sup> 神明照其自个旨意用奇事，兆头，有伙号数神迹，等圣灵个恩赐<sup>\[2\]</sup>，大家做对证。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, feh kyʽi tsʽæ *gyi*; *ʽôh*, *teng* *gyi* tsʽô-kʽæ. 或话，弗去睬其；或，等其岔开。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *Peng-veng*, *ʽôh* *kông*, pô Sing-Ling feng peh *nying*; *ʽôh*, Sing-Ling sô feng peh *nying* go. 本文，或讲，把圣灵分拨人；或圣灵所分拨人个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>5</sup> AH-LAH sô kông keh tsiang-læ-go z-shü, *Jing-ming* bing feh-z peh gyi kwe-voh keh-sing tʽin-s: <sup>6</sup> dæn-z yiu ih-go nying læ ih-pʽin shü-li tso te-tsing, z-ka wô, “Nying z soh-si, Ng we kyi-nyiæn gyi? nying-go ng-ts *z* *soh-si*, Ng we tsiao-kwu gyi? <sup>7</sup> Ng z peh gyi pi tʽin-s <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzæn-z siao-tin, *yi* we peh gyi ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun, peh gyi công-kwun Ng siu sô zao-go tong-si: <sup>8</sup> Ng we s-teh væn-veh tu kwe-voh gyi-go kyiah-ʽô.” \[S. 8. 4–6.\] Kyi-jün “s-teh væn-veh kwe-voh gyi,” tön m-yiu ih-yiang feh kwe-voh gyi: dæn-z tao jü-kying wa feh-zing kʽen-kyin væn-veh kwe-voh gyi. <sup>9</sup> <sup>\[2\]</sup>Tsih-yiu keh-we “pi tʽin-s dzæn-z siao-tin-go” Yiæ-su, ah-lah kʽen-kyin Gyi we-leh si-go kwʽu-deo “ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun,” s-teh Gyi kʽao-djôh Jing-ming-go eng-we hao tʽi cong-nying kyʽüoh si-go *kwʽu-deo*.

<sup>5</sup> 阿拉所讲箇将来个时世，神明并弗是拨其归服箇星天使。<sup>6</sup> 但是有一个人来一片书里做对证，是介话，「人是啥西，尔会记念其？人个儿子是啥西，尔会照顾其？<sup>7</sup> 尔是拨其比天使暂时小点<sup>\[1\]</sup>，又会拨其戴上荣华体面个平天冠，拨其掌管尔手所造个东西。<sup>8</sup> 尔会使得万物都归服其个脚下。」\[S. 8. 4–6.\] 既然「使得万物归服其」，断呒有一样弗归服其。但是到如今还弗曾看见万物归服其。<sup>9</sup> 只有箇位「比天使暂时小点个」<u>耶稣</u><sup>\[2\]</sup>，阿拉看见其为了死个苦头「戴上荣华体面个平天冠」，使得其靠着神明个恩惠好替众人吃死个苦头。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, shü-vi siao-tin. 或话，势微小点。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, Tsih kʽen-kyin Yiæ-su ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun: Gyi we-leh si-go kwʽu-deo dzæn-z pi tʽin-s siao-tin-go. 或话，只看见<u>耶稣</u>带上荣华体面个平天冠。其为了死个苦头暂时比天使小点个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>10</sup> Væn-veh tu z we-leh Gyi, kʽao-djôh Gyi go, keh-we *Jing-ming*, iao ta-ling hyü-to ng-ts teh-djôh yüong-wô, ziu s-teh gyi-lah teh-kyiu-go Kyüing-wông we-leh kwʽu-næn neng-keo djün-be, z ing-kæ-go. <sup>11</sup> Keh-we tseng-kyʽi *gyi-lah* go, teng keh-sing be tseng-kyʽi-go, tu z cʽih-ü Ih-we: sô-yi tsʽing-hwu gyi-lah z hyüong-di yia feh we wông-kʽong, z-ka wô, <sup>12</sup> “Ngô we pô Ng-go ming-deo tʽong-pao Ngô-go hyüong-di; læ kyiao-we cong-nyiang Ngô we tsʽông s tsæn-me Ng.” \[S. 22. 22.\] <sup>13</sup> Yi *wô*, “Ngô we siang-sing kʽao-djôh Gyi.” \[2S. 22. 3. S-pʽin 18. 2.\] Wa-yiu *wô*, “Ngô teng Jing-ming sô s-peh Ngô go siao-nying tu læ-tong.” \[Y. 8. 18.\]

<sup>10</sup> 万物都是为了其，靠着其个，箇位神明，要带领许多儿子得着荣华，就使得其拉得救个君王为了苦难能够全备，是应该个。<sup>11</sup> 箇位尊起其拉个，等箇星被尊起个，都是出于一位。所以称呼其拉是兄弟也弗会惶恐，是介话，<sup>12</sup> 「我会把尔个名头通报我个兄弟；来教会中央我会唱诗赞美尔。」\[S. 22. 22.\]<sup>13</sup> 又话，「我会相信靠着其。」还有话，「我等神明所赐拨我个小人都来东。」\[Y. 8. 18.\]

<sup>14</sup> Keh-sing siao-nying kyi-jün joh-ü nyüoh-sing teng hyüih-kyʽi, Gyi-zi yia z-ka dzing we nyüoh-sing teng hyüih-kyʽi; iao we-leh *jih-z-kô-go* si hao ba-diao công-kwun si go cü-kwu, ziu-z mo-kwe; <sup>15</sup> yi hao sih-fông keh-sing, we-leh pʽô si, ih-seng tso nu-boh go.

<sup>14</sup> 箇星小人既然属于肉身等血气，其自也是介成为肉身等血气；要为了十字架个死好败掉掌管死个主顾，就是魔鬼；<sup>15</sup> 又好释放箇星，为了怕死，一生做奴仆个。

<sup>16</sup> Gyi bing feh-z *læ* vu-dzu tʽin-s, z *læ* vu-dzu Üô-pah-lah-hen-go ʽeo-dæ. <sup>17</sup> Keh-lah kông-tao Jing-ming z-ken iao tso ih-go dz-pe cong-sing-go Tsi-s-deo, hao w̆æn-we pah-sing-go ze, li-kʽæ teng *Gyi* hyüong-di yiang-yiang siang-dong. <sup>18</sup> Gyi-zi kyi-jün ziu-ko s-lin-go kwʽu-næn, sô-yi neng-keo pông-dzu keh-sing be s-lin-go cü-kwu.

<sup>16</sup> 其并弗是来扶助天使，是来扶助<u>亚伯拉罕</u>个后代。<sup>17</sup> 箇拉讲到神明事干要做一个慈悲忠信个祭司头，好挽回百姓个罪，离开等其兄弟样样相同。<sup>18</sup> 其自既然受过试炼个苦难，所以能够帮助箇星被试炼个主顾。

### H. III.

KA-NI, be tʽin-zông sô dziao-go sing-jün hyüong-di, yüong ts-si s-tsʽeng ah-lah sô tsiao-jing *dao-li* go Tsʽa-s teng Tsi-s-deo, keh-we Kyi-toh Yiæ-su: <sup>2</sup> Gyi yiu cong-sing *voh-z* sô shih-lih Gyi go *Cü*, tsing-ziang Mo-si “læ Jing-ming-go ʽeh-kô li-hyiang *yiu* *cong-sing*” ka. \[Ms. 12. 7.\] <sup>3</sup> Hwông-tsʽia keh-we *Yiæ-su* sön z kʽo-yi teh-djôh yüong-wô ko-jü Mo-si, yiu-ts-wu dzing-kô-go cü-kwu pi *gyi* ih-kô-go *nying* z keng-kô kwe-djong. <sup>4</sup> Væn-pah nying-kô tsong yiu ih-go dzing-kô-go cü: ts-ü dzing-dziu væn-veh go, z Jing-ming. <sup>5</sup> Næn-kæn Mo-si tso “nu-boh,” hao te-tsing keh-sing tsiang-læ iao kông go shih-wô, “læ Jing-ming-go ʽeh-kô yiu cong-sing;” <sup>6</sup> dæn-z Kyi-toh tso Ng-ts djü Gyi-zi-go ih-kô *yiu* *cong-sing*: ah-lah ziah neng-keo fông-tæn *siang-sing*, kao-hying siang-vông, ih-dzih tao ti kyin-kwu siu-djü, ziu-z Gyi ih-kô-go *nying*.

介呢，被天上所召个圣善兄弟，用仔细思忖阿拉所招认道理个差使等祭司头，箇位<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>。<sup>2</sup> 其有忠信服侍所设立其个主，正像<u>摩西</u>「来神明个合家里向有忠信」介。\[Ms. 12. 7.\] <sup>3</sup> 况且箇位<u>耶稣</u>算是可以得着荣华过如<u>摩西</u>，犹之乎成家个主顾比其一家个人是更加贵重。<sup>4</sup> 凡百人家总有一个成家个主。至于成就万物个，是神明。<sup>5</sup> 难间<u>摩西</u>做「奴仆」，好对证箇星将来要讲个说话，「来神明个合家有忠信。」<sup>6</sup> 但是<u>基督</u>做儿子治其自个一家有忠信。阿拉若能够放胆相信，高兴想望，一直到底坚固守住，就是其一家个人。

<sup>7</sup> Keh-lah, ziang Sing-Ling sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, <sup>8</sup> feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing, ziang *zin-deo* kyih *Ngô* ô-wông, læ kwʽông-iæ di-fông s-s *Ngô* z-ʽeo ka: <sup>9</sup> ziu-z ng-lah tsu-tsong s-s Ngô, tʽen-tʽen Ngô, s-jih nyin kong-fu kʽen-kyin Ngô sô tso-go z-ken. <sup>10</sup> Sô-yi Ngô kʽo-u keh-dæ-go nying, ziu wô, Gyi-lah ih-hyiang yiu zia-pʽih-go sing; gyi yia feh sih-teh Ngô-go lu-deo. <sup>11</sup> Ngô ziu dong-nu vah-tsiu, *wô*, Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô en-sih li-hyiang.” \[S. 95. 8–11.\] <sup>12</sup> Hyüong-di, yüong kwu-djôh, kʽong-pʽô ng-lah cong-nyiang yiu nying dzeng feh siang-sing hyüong-ôh-go sing, ziu li-kʽæ keh-we weh-go Jing-ming: <sup>13</sup> “kyih-mih nyih-ts wa læ-tong, kæ nyih-tang-nyih dô-kô siang-kyʽün; sæn-leh ng-lah li-hyiang yiu nying, we-leh ze-nyih-go hong-pʽin, pin tso ngang *sing-dziang*. <sup>14</sup> Ing-we ah-lah ziah neng-keo fông-tæn *siang-sing*, s-cong-jü-ih kyin-kwu siu-djü, ziu teng Kyi-toh dô-kô yiu-veng.

<sup>7</sup> 箇拉，像圣灵所话，「今末若听其个说话，<sup>8</sup> 弗可硬自个心，像前头激我𢛟惶，来旷野地方试试我时候介。<sup>9</sup> 就是尔拉祖宗试试我，探探我，四十年工夫看见我所做个事干。<sup>10</sup> 所以我可恶箇代个人，就话，其拉一向有邪僻个心；其也弗识得我个路头。<sup>11</sup> 我就动怒罚咒，话，其拉总弗能够走进我安息里向。」\[S. 95. 8–11.\] <sup>12</sup> 兄弟，用顾着，恐怕尔拉中央有人存弗相信凶恶个心，就离开箇位活个神明。<sup>13</sup> 「今末日子还来东，该日打日大家相劝；省勒尔拉里向有人，为了罪孽个哄骗，变做硬心肠。<sup>14</sup> 因为阿拉若能够放胆相信，始终如一坚固守住，就等<u>基督</u>大家有份。

<sup>15</sup> *Zông-veng* sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing, ziang *zin-deo* kyih Ngô ô-wông ka.” \[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>16</sup> Ka-ni, tʽing-meng ts-ʽeo kyih *Gyi* ô-wông cü-kwu z jü ni? kyʽi feh-z keng-leh Mo-si tseo-cʽih Yiæ-gyih go cong-nying ma? <sup>17</sup> *Jing-ming* s-jih nyin kong-fu kʽo-u jü ni? kyʽi feh-z keh-sing væn-leh ze, gyi s-siu tʽæn læ kwʽông-iæ-li go ma? <sup>18</sup> *Jing-ming* vah-tsiu feh hyü tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go, z jü ni? Ziu-z keh-pæn feh i-jing-go nying. Kʽo kyin-teh, gyi-lah sô-yi feh neng-keo tseo-tsing z we-leh feh siang-sing-go yün-kwu.

<sup>15</sup> 上文所话，「今末若听其个说话，弗可硬自个心，像前头激我𢛟惶介。」\[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>16</sup> 介呢，听闻之后激其𢛟惶主顾是谁呢？岂弗是跟勒<u>摩西</u>走出<u>埃及</u>个众人吗？<sup>17</sup> 神明四十年工夫可恶谁呢？岂弗是箇星犯了罪，其尸首摊来旷野里个吗？<sup>18</sup> 神明罚咒弗许走进其安息里向个，是谁呢？就是箇班弗依顺个人。可见得，其拉所以弗能够走进是为了弗相信个缘故。

### H. IV.

Ka-ni, tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go ing-hyü kyi-jün yi-lôh-tong, ah-lah kæ pʽô-gyü, vi-min ng-lah li-hyiang yiu nying sön z tsʽo-lôh-go. <sup>2</sup> Yiu hao sing-sih djün peh ah-lah, tsing-ziang peh gyi-lah ka: dæn-z sô <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-go *en-sih* shih-wô z vu-ih ü gyi-lah, ing-we <sup>\[2\]</sup>keh-sing tʽing-meng-go cü-kwu m-neh siang-sing-go sing dzeng-tih. <sup>3</sup> Keh-go en-sih li-hyiang *tsih-yiu* ah-lah siang-sing-go nying hao tseo-tsing-kyʽi; ziang *Jing-ming* sô wô, “Ngô ziu dong-nu vah-tsiu, *wô*, Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô en-sih li-hyiang:” \[S. 95. 11.\] jün-r tʽin-di zao-dzing ts-ʽeo *Jing-ming-go* z-ken yi-kying wun-kong-de. Kông-tao di-tsʽih *nyih* yiu ih-pʽin shü z-ka wô, “Jing-ming læ di-tsʽih nyih Gyi væn-pah z-ken tu wun-kong, ziu en-sih-de:” \[Ts. 2. 2.\] <sup>5</sup> wa-yiu zông-veng *wô*, “Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô-go en-sih li-hyiang.”

介呢，走进其安息里向个应许既然遗落东，阿拉该怕惧，未免尔拉里向有人算是错落个。<sup>2</sup> 有好信息传拨阿拉，正像拨其拉介。但是所传个<sup>\[1\]</sup>安息说话是无益于其拉，因为箇星听闻个主顾呒得相信个心存的<sup>\[2\]</sup>。<sup>3</sup> 箇个安息里向只有阿拉相信个人好走进去；像神明所话，「我就动怒罚咒，话，其拉总弗能够走进我安息里向。」\[S. 95. 11.\] 然而天地造成之后神明个事干已经完工了。讲到第七日有一片书是介话，「神明来第七日其凡百事干都完工，就安息了。」\[Ts. 2. 2.\] <sup>5</sup> 还有上文话，「其拉总弗能够走进我个安息里向。」

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng*, tʽing-ming-go. 本文，听闻个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kông-tao siang-sing-go sing, gyi-lah feh neng-keo teng keh-sing tʽing-meng cü-kwu dô-kô ʽeh-long. 或话，讲到相信个心，其拉弗能够等箇星听闻主顾大家合拢。（原注）</sup>

<sup>6</sup> Ka-ni, keh-go en-sih li-hyiang tsong yiu nying wa hao tseo-tsing-kyʽi; keh-sing sin tʽing-meng keh hao sing-sih cü-kwu we-leh feh i-jing yi feh neng-keo-tseo-tsing: <sup>7</sup> keh-lah læ hyü-to nyin-su ts-ʽeo læ Da-bih-go *shü*-li yi ʽæn-ding ih-go nyih-ts, wô z “kyih-mih;” ziang *zông-veng* sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing.” \[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>8</sup> Kyüô-jü <sup>\[1\]</sup>Iah-shü-üô yi-kying peh pah-sing en-sih, ʽeo-deo *Jing-ming* feh we di-kyʽi bih-go nyih-ts;—— <sup>9</sup> ka kʽen-kyʽi-læ, Jing-ming-go pah-sing tsong wa yiu ih-go <sup>\[2\]</sup>en-sih. <sup>10</sup> Ing-we tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go cü-kwu, gyi z zi-go z-ken wun-kong ts-ʽeo ziu en-sih-go, ziang Jing-ming Zi-go z-ken zao-dzing ts-ʽeo *ziu* *en-sih* ih-yiang-go.

<sup>6</sup> 介呢，箇个安息里向总有人还好走进去；箇星先听闻箇好信息主顾为了弗依顺又弗能够走进。<sup>7</sup> 箇拉来许多年数之后来<u>大辟</u>个书里又限定一个日子，话是「今末；」像上文所话，「今末若听其个说话，弗可硬自个心。」\[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>8</sup> 假如<u>约书亚</u><sup>\[1\]</sup>已经拨百姓安息，后头神明弗会提起别个日子。<sup>9</sup> 介看起来，神明个百姓总还有一个安息。<sup>10</sup> 因为走进其安息里向个主顾，其是自个事干完工之后就安息个<sup>\[2\]</sup>，像神明自个事干造成之后就安息一样个。

<sup>\[1\] Iah-shü-üô, YIÆ-SU, *keh* *liang* *ming-z* *peng-veng* *tsôh* *tso* *z-ngæn*. <u>约书亚</u>，<u>耶稣</u>，箇两名字本文作做字眼。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *Peng-veng* *wô*, siu en-sih-*nyih* go z-ken. 本文话，守安息日个事干。（原注）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah yüong zông-kying gying-lih tseo-tsing keh en-sih li-hyiang, kʽong-pʽô yiu nying kʽen keh-cong-ka <sup>\[1\]</sup>feh i-jing-go pông-yiang ziu iao tih-tao. <sup>12</sup> Jing-ming-go shih-wô z ling-weh, yiu neng-ken, kwʽa-jü ting fong-li-go pao-kyin, we cʽoh-tsing pʽo-kʽæ weh-ling teng ling-sing, gao-kwu teng kweh-si, yi we bin-cʽih sing-li-go s-nyiæn teng ts-hyiang. <sup>13</sup> Gyi sô zao-go, yia m-yiu ih-yiang læ Gyi min-zin neng-keo ing-mun: væn-veh læ Gyi ngæn-zin tu cʽih-lu-lu, ming-ming pa-tong; Gyi ziu-z kyih-sön ah-lah tsiang go *Cü*.

<sup>11</sup> 所以阿拉用上紧勤力走进箇安息里向，恐怕有人看箇种介弗依顺个<sup>\[1\]</sup>榜样就要跌倒。<sup>12</sup> 神明个说话是灵活，有能干，快如顶锋利个宝剑，会戳进破开活灵等灵性，绞股等骨髓，又会辨出心里个思念等志向。<sup>13</sup> 其所造个，也呒有一样来其面前能够隐瞒。万物来其眼前都出露露，明明摆东。其就是结算阿拉账个主。

<sup>\[1\] Feh i-jing-go; *yiu-teh* *fæn*, feh siang-sing-go. 弗依顺个；有得翻，弗相信个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>14</sup> KA kʽen-kyʽi-læ, ah-lah kyi-jün yiu ih-go do Tsi-s-deo, yi-kying kying-ko keh-sing tʽin, ziu-z Jing-ming-go Ng-ts Yiæ-su, kæ kyin-kwu siu-djü *ah-lah* sô tsiao-jing-go dao-li. <sup>15</sup> Hwông-tsʽia ah-lah sô yiu-go Tsi-s-deo tsæ-ve feh neng-keo tʽi-liang æ-lin ah-lah-go no-ziah; Gyi yiang-yiang z-ken be s-lin, ziang ah-lah ka, *tsih-z* m-neh tsao-djôh ze-kyʽin. <sup>16</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah yüong fông-tæn tseo-long *ʽong*-eng zo-we min-zin, hao teh-djôh æ-lin, tsin-djôh eng-we ze-z pông-dzu *ah-lah*.

<sup>14</sup> 介看起来，阿拉既然有一个大祭司头，已经经过箇星天，就是神明个儿子<u>耶稣</u>，该坚固守住阿拉所招认个道理。<sup>15</sup> 况且阿拉所有个祭司头再𫧃弗能够体谅哀怜阿拉个懦弱；其样样事干被试炼，像阿拉介，只是呒得遭着罪愆。<sup>16</sup> 所以阿拉用放胆走拢鸿恩座位面前，好得着哀怜，占着恩惠随时帮助阿拉。

### H. V.

Væn-pah tsi-s-deo tu z dzong nying-go cong-nyiang kæn-shün-cʽih-læ, yi z we-leh tʽi nying *voh-z* Jing-ming sô shih-lih-go, hao tʽi ze-ôh hyin-zông li-veh teng tsi-z; <sup>2</sup> gyi neng-keo nyün-liang keh-sing feh ming-bah tseo tsʽô-lu go nying, ing-we gyi-zi yia ʽo-jün z no-ziah-go. <sup>3</sup> Sô-yi we-leh zi, tsing-ziang we-leh pah-sing, tông-kæ hyin joh-ze-go tsi-veh.

凡百祭司头都是从人个中央拣选出来，又是为了替人服侍神明所设立个，好替罪恶献上礼物等祭祀；<sup>2</sup> 其能够原谅箇星弗明白走岔路个人，因为其自也和然是懦弱个。<sup>3</sup> 所以为了自，正像为了百姓，当该献赎罪个祭物。

<sup>4</sup> Wa-yiu keh kwe-djong-go tsih-veng m-nying ken tông, tsih-yiu mong Jing-ming sô dziao-læ cü-kwu, ziang Üô-leng ka. <sup>5</sup> Z-ka Kyi-toh yia m̆-teh Zi tʽe-tseng tso tsi-s-deo; z *Jing-ming* *sô* *tʽe-tseng-go*, teng Gyi wô, “Ng z Ngô-go Ng-ts, Ngô kyih-mih sang Ng:” \[S. 2. 7.\] <sup>6</sup> wa-yiu bih *u-sen* z-ka wô, “Ng üong-yün *tso* Tsi-s, tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.” \[S.110. 4.\]

<sup>4</sup> 还有箇贵重个职份呒人敢当，只有蒙神明所召来主顾，像<u>亚伦</u>介。<sup>5</sup> 是介<u>基督</u>也呒得自推尊做祭司头；是神明所推尊个，等其话，「尔是我个儿子，我今末生尔。」\[S. 2. 7.\] <sup>6</sup> 还有别乌碎是介话，「尔永远做祭司，照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次。」 \[S.110. 4.\]

<sup>7</sup> Gyi dzing we nyüoh-sing go z-ʽeo, tsʽæn-nyiæn gyiu-kʽeng, ngæn-li beh-cʽih do-do sing-hyiang eo keh-we neng-keo kyiu Gyi min-diao si go Cü-kwu; <sup>\[1\]</sup>we-leh Gyi tseng-kying pʽô-gyü-go sing, *Jing-ming* ziu tʽing Gyi: <sup>8</sup> se-tsih? z-go Ng-ts, Gyi neng-keo i-jing, yia z dzong kwʽu-næn li-hyiang jih-lin-ko-læ-go. <sup>9</sup> Tao-leh djün-be, *ziu* tso væn-pah i-jing Gyi cü-kwu üong-yün teh-kyiu-go Cü: <sup>10</sup> z Jing-ming sô tsʽing-hwu z Tsi-s-deo “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.”

<sup>7</sup> 其成为肉身个时候，忏念求恳，眼泪浡出大大声响讴箇位能够救其免掉死个主顾；为了其尊敬怕惧个心，神明就听其<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>8</sup> 虽即是个儿子，其能够依顺，也是从苦难里向习练过来个。<sup>9</sup> 到了全备，就做凡百依顺其主顾永远得救个主。<sup>10</sup> 是神明所称呼是祭司头「照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次。」

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, *Jing-ming* ziu tʽing Gyi, *s-teh* *Gyi* tʽeh-cʽih pʽô-gyü-go sing: *ʽôh*, *Jing-ming* ziu tʽing Gyi, *kyiu* *Gyi* min-diao Gyi sô pʽô-gyü-go z-ken. *Kʽen* Mt. 26. 37. Mk. 14. 33. Iʽe. 12. 27. 或话，或话，神明就听其，使得其脱出怕惧个心。或，神明就听其，救其免掉其所怕惧个事干。看 Mt. 26. 37. Mk. 14. 33. Iʽe. 12. 27.（原注）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> KÔNG-TAO Gyi, ah-lah yiu hyü-to næn ka-shih go shih-wô, ing-we ng-lah tʽing-ts z mông-vu-deo-jü-de. <sup>13</sup> Ziah leng z-ʽeo-go *dziang-kyiu*, ng-lah *nyün* kæ tso sin-sang; næn-kæn wa kyʽiah-leh pô Jing-ming tsiao-shü-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li tsæ kao ng; ng-lah yia kyʽiah-leh *kyʽüoh* na, væn-zih z kyʽüoh-feh-læ-de. <sup>13</sup> Væn-pah kyʽüoh-na-go cü-kwu feh tʽong-dah kong-yi-go dao-li; gyi z-go siao-nying.” <sup>14</sup> Tsih-yiu <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzing-nying-tsiang-da-go hao *kyʽüoh* væn-zih; gyi kyin-sih we-leh <sup>\[2\]</sup>jih-lin neng-keo-bin-pih hao-tæ.

<sup>11</sup> 讲到其，阿拉有许多难解说个说话，因为尔拉听仔是茫无头绪了。<sup>12</sup> 若论时候个长久，尔拉原该做先生；难间还却勒把神明诏书个初学道理再教尔；尔拉也却勒吃奶，饭食是吃弗来了。<sup>13</sup> 凡百吃奶个主顾弗通达公义个道理；其是个小人。<sup>14</sup> 只有成人长大个<sup>\[1\]</sup>好吃饭食；其见识为了习练<sup>\[2\]</sup>能够辨别好歹。

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, jih-kwæn. 或话，习惯。（原注）</sup>

### HI. VI.

Sô-yi <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzong Kyi-toh-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li ah-lah yüong *dzin-dzin* tsing-ih tao djün-be-go di-bu: hao-vong tsæ tang ti-ts, *ziu-z* hwe-kæ *gyi* si-go ʽang-we, siang-sing Jing-ming, <sup>2</sup> *ling-ziu* keh-sing si-li, en-siu-go *li-tsih*, si-nying tsæ weh, üong-yün pʽun-tön, keh-sing dao-li. <sup>3</sup> Ziah-z Jing-ming kʽeng ing-hyü, ah-lah pih-ding z-ka tso.

所以从<u>基督</u>个初学道理阿拉用渐渐进益到全备个地步<sup>\[1\]</sup>。好甮再打底子，就是悔改其死个行为，相信神明，<sup>2</sup> 领受箇星洗礼，按手个礼节，死人再活，永远判断，箇星道理。<sup>3</sup> 若是神明肯应许，阿拉必定是介做。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, ah-lah hao-vong kông Kyi-toh-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li; hao kyʽi kông keh djün-be-go dao-li. 或话，阿拉好甮讲<u>基督</u>个初学道理；好去讲箇全备个道理。（原注）</sup>

<sup>4</sup> Tʽông-jün yiu nying ih-tsao teh-djôh kwông-ming, zông-djôh keh tʽin-zông eng-s-go mi-dao, tsih-ziu Sing-Ling-go *ken-dong*, <sup>5</sup> zông-djôh Jing-ming-go hao-hao dao-li teng tsiang-læ z-shü-go neng-ken, <sup>6</sup> *ʽeo-deo* we shih-diao *keh-go* *dao-li*, tön feh neng-keo tsæ we-sing-cün-i ih-li tso-nying; *keh* *z* gyi-zi djong-sin ting-sah Jing-ming-go Ng-ts, ming-ming tsao-tʽah *Gyi*. <sup>7</sup> Pʽi-jü din-di peh-djông yiu yü ts-jing gyi; ziah fah-cʽih tʽu-tsʽæn, ʽeh-djôh <sup>\[1\]</sup>nong-fu-go yüong-dziang, z teh-djôh Jing-ming-go tô-hwô: <sup>8</sup> ziah sang-cʽih tsʽ-bang yia-tsʽao, *ziu* *iao* be kyʽi-diao, gying-wu tsiu-mô; gyi-go kwe-keng z iao me-diao.

<sup>4</sup> 倘然有人一遭得着光明，尝着箇天上恩赐个味道，接受圣灵个感动，<sup>5</sup> 尝着神明个好好道理等将来时世个能干，<sup>6</sup> 后头会失掉箇个道理，断弗能够再回心转意一礼做人；箇是其自重先钉煞神明个儿子，明明糟蹋其。<sup>7</sup> 譬如田地不常有雨滋润其；若发出土产，合着农夫个<sup>\[1\]</sup>用场，是得着神明个朵化。<sup>8</sup> 若生出刺棚野草，就要被弃掉，近乎咒骂；其个归根是要𤈦掉。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kang-cong cü-nying-kô-go. 或话，耕种主人家个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>9</sup> Tsʽing-æ-go *hyüong-di*, ah-lah se-tsih ka kông, yia jih-we siang-sing ng-lah z kôh-lu-go, tsong hao teh-djôh kyiu. <sup>10</sup> Jing-ming tsæ-ve feh kong-dao, mông-kyi ng-lah sô tso-go z-ken, teng ng jing-æ-go lao-loh; ziu-z ng-lah we-leh Gyi-go ming-deo sô hyin-cʽih-go *jing-æ*, su-djông we kong-ing sing-du, *yin-dzæ* dzing-gyiu z-ka tso. <sup>11</sup> Ah-lah pô-feh-neng-keo ng-lah kôh-nying tu we hyin-cʽih tso-yiang nyih-sing, ih-dzih tao ti toh-jih siang-vông: <sup>12</sup> feh-kʽo yiæ-dæ, *iao* kʽen keh-sing we-leh siang-sing jing-næ we tsih-djôh sô ing-hyü cü-kwu-go yiang.

<sup>9</sup> 亲爱个兄弟，阿拉虽即介讲，也实惠相信尔拉是各路个，总好得着救。<sup>10</sup> 神明再𫧃弗公道，忘记尔拉所做个事干，等尔仁爱个劳碌；就是尔拉为了其个名头所显出个仁爱，素常会供应圣徒，现在仍旧是介做。<sup>11</sup> 阿拉巴弗能够尔拉各人都会显出做样热心，一直到抵笃实想望。<sup>12</sup> 弗可懈怠，要看箇星为了相信忍耐会接着所应许主顾个样。

<sup>13</sup> Jing-ming ing-hyü Üô-pah-lah-hen z-ʽeo, ing-we m-yiu keng-kô do-go hao ts-tin gyi vah-tsiu, ziu ts-tin Zi vah-tsiu, wô, <sup>14</sup> “Ngô tsong we do-nyiang coh-foh ng, do-nyiang cʽông-zing ng-go *ts-seng*:” \[Ts. 22. 16, 17.\] <sup>15</sup> z-ka *Üô-pah-lah-hen* ziu næ-sing teng-dæ, teh-djôh sô ing-hyü-go.

<sup>13</sup> 神明应许<u>亚伯拉罕</u>时候，因为呒有更加大个好指点其罚咒，就指点自罚咒，话，<sup>14</sup> 「我总会大样祝福尔，大样昌盛尔个子孙。」<sup>15</sup> 是介<u>亚伯拉罕</u>就耐心等待，得着所应许个。

<sup>16</sup> *Væn-pah* nying vah-tsiu, tsong z ts-tin do-jü *gyi-zi* go: vah-tsiu z kʽao-jih gyi *shih-wô*, ʽeo-deo m-kao hao cün-pin. <sup>17</sup> Næn-kæn Jing-ming pô-feh-neng-keo keh-sing tsih-djôh *Gyi* ing-hyü cü-kwu hao ming-ming hyiao-teh Gyi-go ts-i z keng-kæ-feh-læ, *sô-yi* <sup>\[1\]</sup>yüong vah-tsiu shih-wô tso bing-kyü: <sup>18</sup> z-ka, yiu liang-yiang keng-kæ-feh-læ-go z-ken, Jing-ming sô feh neng-keo zih-yin go; s-teh ah-lah dao-næn kyʽi tsih-ziu sô ts-tin ah-lah go siang-vông cü-kwu tu hao en-en-sing-sing. <sup>19</sup> Keh-go *siang-vông* kʽo-pi ling-weng-go mao, dziah-jih lao-kʽao, pʽao læ mun-tsiang li-hyiang: <sup>20</sup> keh-go di-fông Yiæ-su tso sin-fong tʽi ah-lah *sin* tseo-tsing-kyʽi, “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ” üong-yün tso Tsi-s-deo.

<sup>16</sup> 凡百人罚咒，总是指点大如其自个。罚咒是靠实其说话，后头呒告好转变。<sup>17</sup> 难间神明巴弗能够箇星接着其应许主顾好明明晓得其个旨意是更改弗来，所以用罚咒说话做凭据<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>18</sup> 是介，有两样更改弗来个事干，神明所弗能够食言个；使得阿拉逃难去接受所指点阿拉个想望主顾都好安安心心。<sup>19</sup> 箇个想望可比灵魂个锚，着实牢靠，抛来幔帐里向。<sup>20</sup> 箇个地方<u>耶稣</u>做先锋替阿拉先走进去，「照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次」永远做祭司头。

### H. VII.

KEH Mah-kyi-si-teh, Sah-leng-go wông, ting kao Jing-ming-go tsi-s, zin-deo pʽong-djôh Üô-pah-lah-hen sah-ba-leh keh-sing koh-wông cün-læ, ziu coh-foh gyi; <sup>2</sup> Üô-pah-lah-hen pô ih-tsʽih-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih feng peh gyi; *Mah-kyi-si-teh-go* *ming-z*, di-ih, z ‘kong-yi wông’ ka ka-shih, wa-yiu *kyiao-leh* Sah-leng wông, ziu-z, ‘bing-en-go wông’; <sup>3</sup> gyi m̆-teh ah-tia, m̆-teh ah-nyiang, m̆-teh kô-pu, yia m̆-teh sang-nyih, yia m̆-teh si-gyi, z ziang Jing-ming Ng-ts ka;—— keh-go nying dziang-tʽong tso tsi-s.

箇<u>麦基洗德</u>，<u>撒冷</u>个王，顶高神明个祭司，前头碰着<u>亚伯拉罕</u>杀败了箇星国王转来，就祝福其；<sup>2</sup> <u>亚伯拉罕</u>把一切个东西十股得一分拨其。<u>麦基洗德</u>个名字，第一，是「公义王」介解说，还有叫勒<u>撒冷</u>王，就是，「平安个王」；<sup>3</sup> 其呒得阿爹，呒得阿娘，呒得家谱，也呒得生日，也呒得死期，是像神明儿子介；——箇个人长通做祭司。

<sup>4</sup> Ng-lah tsʽeng-tsʽeng-kʽen, tsu-tsong-deo Üô-pah-lah-hen we pô lo-liah-læ-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih peh gyi, keh-go *nying* z dza-nyiang tseng-kwe. <sup>5</sup> Næn-kæn Li-vi ts-seng tsih-ziu tsi-s tsih-veng go cü-kwu, keh-sing pah-sing se-tsih z gyi hyüong-di, cʽih-ü Üô-pah-lah-hen-go kweh-joh, jün-r gyi vong-ts tsiao lih-fah hao do pah-sing-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih: <sup>6</sup> dæn-z Mah-kyi-si-teh, z teng gyi-lah kôh <sup>\[1\]</sup>ts-pʽa go, gyi we do Üô-pah-lah-hen-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih, yi coh-foh keh yiu ing-hyü go cü-kwu. <sup>7</sup> Keh ʽô-pe teh-djôh zông-pe-go coh-foh, z m-pæn-poh-go dao-li. <sup>8</sup> Wa-yiu, *dziu* dông-pin *kʽen-læ*, iao si go nying z do-kyü jih-kwu-teh-ih go; *dziu* keh-pin *kʽen-læ*, *keh* *do-kyü-go* *cü-kwu* yiu te-tsing gyi z weh-go. <sup>9</sup> Ping-tsʽia Li-vi, *se-tsih* z do-kyü jih-kwu-teh-ih go cü-kwu, yia hao wô gyi dzong Üô-pah-lah-hen-go siu-li yi-kying yiu jih-kwu-teh-ih do-cʽih-de: <sup>10</sup> ing-we Mah-kyi-si-teh nying-tsih Üô-pah-lah-hen z-ʽeo, Li-vi wa læ gyi tsu-tsong-go sing-zông.

<sup>4</sup> 尔拉忖忖看，祖宗头<u>亚伯拉罕</u>会把掳掠来个东西十股得一拨其，箇个人是咋样尊贵。<sup>5</sup> 难间<u>利未</u>子孙接受祭司职份个主顾，箇星百姓虽即是其兄弟，出于<u>亚伯拉罕</u>个骨肉，然而其奉旨照律法好驮百姓个东西十股得一。<sup>6</sup> 但是<u>麦基洗德</u>，是等其拉各支派<sup>\[1\]</sup>个，其会驮<u>亚伯拉罕</u>个东西十股得一，又祝福箇有应许个主顾。<sup>7</sup> 箇下辈得着上辈个祝福，是呒扳驳个道理。<sup>8</sup> 还有，就荡边看来，要死个人是驮归十股得一个；就箇边看来，箇驮归个主顾有对证其是活个。<sup>9</sup> 并且<u>利未</u>，虽即是驮归十股得一个主顾，也好话其从<u>亚伯拉罕</u>个手里已经有十股得一驮出了。<sup>10</sup> 因为<u>麦基洗德</u>迎接<u>亚伯拉罕</u>时候，<u>利未</u>还来其祖宗个身上。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kô-pu. 或话，家谱。（原注）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Li-vi tsi-s-go tsih-veng ziah neng-keo s-teh *nying* djün-be, (pah-sing z yi-kying læ gyi joh-ʽô teh-djôh lih-fah,) ka-ni ʽo-yüong bih-go tsi-s “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ” ka cʽih-læ, feh eo gyi tsiao. Üô-leng-go pæn-tsʽ ni? <sup>12</sup> Tsi-s-go tsih-veng kyi-jün keng-kæ, lih-fah z-jün yia iao keng-kæ. <sup>13</sup> Keh-go shih-wô sô ts-tin-go z joh-ü bih-go ts-pʽa, bing m-yiu nying z-dzih tsi-dæn ko: <sup>14</sup> ah-lah-go Cü z ming-ming cʽih-ü Yiu-da; keh-go ts-pʽa Mo-si bing m-yiu kông-djôh tsi-s tsih-veng ko.

<sup>11</sup> <u>利未</u>祭司个职份若能够使得人全备，（百姓是已经来其属下得着律法，）介呢何用别个祭司「照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次」介出来，弗讴其照<u>亚伦</u>个班次呢？<sup>12</sup> 祭司个职份既然更改，律法自然也要更改。<sup>13</sup> 箇个说话所指点个是属于别个支派，并呒有人侍值祭坛过。<sup>14</sup> 阿拉个主是明明出于<u>犹大</u>；箇个支派<u>摩西</u>并呒有讲着祭司职份过。

<sup>15</sup> Keh-go z-ken z keng-kô hyin-jün; ing-we yiu bih-go Tsi-s ziang Mah-kyi-si-teh ka iao cʽih-læ, <sup>16</sup> Gyi feh-z tsiao nyüoh-sing ʽao-ling-go lih-fah, z tsiao m-gyüong-dzing weh-ming-go neng-ken sô shih-lih-go: <sup>17</sup> ing-we *Jing-ming* te-tsing *Gyi*, wô, “Ng üong-yün tso Tsi-s tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.” \[S. 110. 4.\] <sup>18</sup> Keh zin-deo-go ʽao-ling, we-leh gyi no-ziah vu-yüong, z fi-diao-de; <sup>19</sup> (lih-fah ih-ngæn feh neng-keo s-teh nying djün-be;) <sup>\[1\]</sup>næn-kæn tsæ shih-cʽih keng-kô hao-go siang-vông, s-teh ah-lah kʽo-yi tsʽing-gying Jing-ming.

<sup>15</sup> 箇个事干是更加显然；因为有别个祭司像<u>麦基洗德</u>介要出来，<sup>16</sup> 其弗是照肉身号令个律法，是照呒穷尽活命个能干所设立个。<sup>17</sup> 因为神明对证其，话，「尔永远做祭司照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次。」<sup>18</sup> 箇前头个号令，为了其懦弱无用，是废掉了。<sup>19</sup> （律法一眼弗能够使得人全备；）难间再说出更加好个想望，使得阿拉可以亲近神明<sup>\[1\]</sup>。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, *lih-fah* peh-ko s-teh keh keng-kô hao-go siang-vông læ; *kʽen* Ky. 3. 24: *ʽôh* *wô*, tsih-yiu sô shih-lih keh keng-kô hao-go siang-vông *we* *s-teh* *nying* *djün-be*. 或话，律法不过使得箇更加好个想望来；看 Ky. 3. 24。或话，只有所设立箇更加好个想望会使得人全备。（原注）</sup>

<sup>20</sup> Tsæ-wô, *shih-lih* *Yiæ-su* feh-z m̆-teh vah-tsiu;—— <sup>21</sup> shih-lih gyi-lah tso tsi-s z m̆-teh vah-tsiu; tsih-yiu *Jing-ming* *shih-lih* Yiæ-su, vah-tsiu teng Gyi wô, “CÜ yi-kying vah-tsiu, tsæ-ve fæn-hwe; Ng üong-yün tso Tsi-s tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ:”—— <sup>22</sup> z-ka Yiæ-su *yia* z keng hao iah-go Pao-nying.

<sup>20</sup> 再话，设立<u>耶稣</u>弗是呒得罚咒；——<sup>21</sup> 设立其拉做祭司是呒得罚咒；只有神明设立<u>耶稣</u>，罚咒等其话，「主已经罚咒，再𫧃反悔；尔永远做祭司照<u>麦基洗德</u>个班次。」——<sup>22</sup> 是介<u>耶稣</u>也是更好约个保人。

<sup>23</sup> Wa-yiu gyi-lah, we-leh iao si, feh neng-keo dziang læ-tong; sô-yi yiu hyü-to tsi-s: <sup>24</sup> tsih-yiu Yiæ-su, we-leh Gyi üong-yün læ-tong, z yiu <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-ziu-feh-læ-go tsi-s tsih-veng. <sup>25</sup> Sô-yi cong-ü neng-keo kyiu keh-sing kʽao-djôh Gyi tseo-long Jing-ming u-sen læ-go cü-kwu; ing-we Gyi z-djông weh-tong, hao tʽi gyi-lah dæ-gyiu.

<sup>23</sup> 还有其拉，为了要死，弗能够长来东；所以有许多祭司。<sup>24</sup> 只有<u>耶稣</u>，为了其永远来东，是有传授弗来个<sup>\[1\]</sup>祭司职份。<sup>25</sup> 所以终于能够救箇星靠着其走拢神明乌碎来个主顾；因为其时常活东，好替其拉代求。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, feh we ko-kyʽi go. 或话，弗会过去个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>26</sup> Keh-cong-ka Tsi-s-deo kʽeo-kʽeo teng ah-lah siang-nyi-go; *Gyi* *z* sing-jün, lao-jih, m-yüô-tin, li-yün ze-nying, yi-kying kao-jü keh-sing tʽin go; <sup>27</sup> Gyi feh ziang *bih-go* tsi-s-deo, sin we-leh zi-go ze, ʽeo we-leh pah-sing-go *ze*, pih iao me-nyih hyin tsi-veh: Gyi z hyin-zông Zi-go *kyi-sing* tso joh-ze-go tsi-veh, tsih-siao ih-tsao *ziu* *keo-de*. <sup>28</sup> Keh lih-fah z shih-lih no-ziah-go nying tso tsi-s-deo: tsih-yiu lih-fah ts-ʽeo sô vah-tsiu shih-wô z *shih-lih* *keh-go* Ng-ts; Gyi yi-kying üong-yün <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-be-de.

<sup>26</sup> 箇种介祭司头扣扣等阿拉相宜个；其是圣善，老实，呒瑕点，离远罪人，已经高如箇星天个。<sup>27</sup> 其弗像别个祭司头，先为了自个罪，后为了百姓个罪，必要每日献祭物。其是献上自个肌身做赎罪个祭物，只消一遭就够了。<sup>28</sup> 箇律法是设立懦弱个人做祭司头。只有律法之后所罚咒说话是设立箇个儿子；其已经永远全备了<sup>\[1\]</sup>。

<sup>\[1\] *Yiu-teh* *fæn*, tseng-kyʽi-liao. 有得翻，尊起了。（原注）</sup>

### H. VIII.

NÆN-KÆN sô kông-go do-kwe-mo z ah-lah yiu ka-go Tsi-s-deo, yi-kying zo læ tʽin-zông, w̆e-nyin *Cü*-go zo-we jing-siu-pin; <sup>2</sup> tso keh sing-jün <sup>\[1\]</sup>*di-fông* teng tsing tsiang-bong li-hyiang go Bæn-z-nying: keh-go tsiang-bong z Cü sô tah, feh-z nying *tah-go*.

难间所讲个大规模是阿拉有介个祭司头，已经坐来天上，威严主个座位顺手边；<sup>2</sup> 做箇圣善地方<sup>\[1\]</sup>等真帐篷里向个办事人。箇个帐篷是主所搭，弗是人搭个。

<sup>\[1\] *Di-fông*; *yiu-teh* *wô*, *tong-si*. 地方；有得话，东西。（原注）</sup>

<sup>3</sup> Væn-pah tsi-s-deo z we-leh hyin li-veh teng tsi-z ka shih-lih; keh-lah keh-we *Tsi-s-deo* yia pih yiu soh-go *tsi-veh* hao hyin. <sup>4</sup> Gyi ziah læ di-ʽô læ-tong, m-neh hao sön tsi-s, ing-we keh-sing tsiao lih-fah hyin li-veh go tsi-s læ-tong: <sup>5</sup> gyi-lah z læ keh tʽin-zông *tong-si-go* siang-mao teng ing-tsong li-hyiang vong-z *Jing-ming*; ziang Mo-si tang-tsiang zao tsiang-bong z-ʽeo teh-djôh meh-z, wô, “Ng yüong siao-sing, væn-pah tong-si tu iao tsiao sæn-zông sô ts-tin ng go yiang-shih ka tso.” \[C. 25. 4O.\] <sup>6</sup> Næn-kæn *Yiæ-su* yi-kying teh-djôh keng kwe-djong-go tsih-veng, ing-we Gyi tso keng hao iah-go Cong-pao, z kʽao-djôh keng hao ing-hyü sô shih-lih-go.

<sup>3</sup> 凡百祭司头是为了献礼物等祭祀介设立；箇拉箇位祭司头也必有啥个祭物好献。<sup>4</sup> 其若来地下来东，呒得好算祭司，因为箇星照律法献礼物个祭司来东。<sup>5</sup> 其拉是来箇天上东西个相貌等影踪里向奉侍神明；像<u>摩西</u>打账造帐篷时候得着默示，话，「尔用小心，凡百东西都要照山上所指点尔个样式介做。」\[C. 25. 4O.\] <sup>6</sup> 难间<u>耶稣</u>已经得着更贵重个职份，因为其做更好约个中保，是靠着更好应许所设立个。

<sup>7</sup> Keh zin-deo-go *iah* ziah-z m-kao hao pʽi-bing, hao-vong tsæ tang-sön ʽeo-deo-go *iah*. <sup>8</sup> Næn-kæn Jing-ming pʽi-bing gyi-lah, wô, “CÜ z-ka wô, ‘Nyih-ts læ-gyi, Ngô teng Yi-seh-lih keh-kô lin Yiu-da keh-kô iao shih-lih ih-go sing iah; <sup>9</sup> *keh* feh ziang Ngô teng gyi-lah tsu-tsong sô lih-go iah, *ziu-z* te gyi siu iao ling gyi cʽih Yiæ-gyih z-ʽeo *sô* *shih-lih-go*; ing-we gyi-lah feh siu-djü Ngô-go iah, Ngô yia feh kyʽi tsʽæ gyi:’ *keh* *z* CÜ *sô* kông-go. <sup>10</sup> CÜ *yi* wô, ‘Ko-leh keh-sing nyih-ts Ngô teng Yi-seh-lih keh-kô iao lih go iah, ziu-z, pô Ngô-go lih-fah <sup>\[1\]</sup>fông læ gyi hyüong-cong, sia læ gyi sing-li: Ngô we tso gyi-go Jing-ming, gyi we tso Ngô-go pah-sing: <sup>11</sup> hao-vong ling-kyü kao gyi ling-kyü, hyüong-di *kao* gyi hyüong-di, wô, Ng yüong sih-teh CÜ; ing-we væn-pah; feh-leng tseng-kwe pe-zin, tu pih-ding nying-djôh Ngô: <sup>12</sup> ing-we Ngô we kwʽun-shü gyi-go fi-li, feh tsæ kyi-nyiæn gyi-go ze-ôh teng gyi-go ko-væn.’” \[Yl. 31. 31–34.\]

<sup>7</sup> 箇前头个约若是呒告好批评，好甮再打算后头个约。<sup>8</sup> 难间神明批评其拉，话，「主是介话，『日子来其，我等<u>以色列</u>箇家连<u>犹大</u>箇家要设立一个新约。<sup>9</sup> 箇弗像我等其拉祖宗所立个约，就是对其手要领其出<u>埃及</u>时候所设立个；因为其拉弗守住我个约，我也弗去睬其。』箇是主所讲个。<sup>10</sup> 主又话，『过了箇星日子我等<u>以色列</u>箇家要立个约，就是，把我个律法放来其胸中<sup>\[1\]</sup>，写来其心里。我会做其个神明，其会做我个百姓。<sup>11</sup> 好甮邻居教其邻居，兄弟教其兄弟，话，尔用识得主；因为凡百，弗论尊贵卑贱，都必定认着我。<sup>12</sup> 因为我会宽恕其个非礼，弗再记念其个罪恶等其个过犯。』」 \[Yl. 31. 31–34.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng*, s-peh gyi. 本文，赐拨其。（原注）</sup>

### H. IX.

Keh zin-deo-go *iah* yia yiu voh-z *Jing-ming* go <sup>\[1\]</sup>li-tsih, teng joh-ü shü-kæn-zông sing-jün-go *di-fông*. <sup>2</sup> Yiu zin-pun-gyüih tsiang-bong be-hao-kæn; li-hyiang yiu teng-dæ, en-coh, teng pa-kæn-go ping: keh tsʽing we “sing-jün *di-fông*.” <sup>3</sup> Wa-yiu di-nyi-dao mun-tsiang ʽeo-go tsiang-bong, tsʽing we “ting sing-jün *di-fông*:” <sup>4</sup> yiu tin-hyiang-go kying-lu, teng dön-kyʽün tʽiah-kying-go iah-siang; *iah-siang* li-hyiang *yiu* tsi mô-nô go kying-bing, Üô-leng tsʽiu-ngô-go bông, teng *lih*-iah-go pe-ba; <sup>5</sup> zông-deo *yiu* yüong-wô-go Kyi-lu-ping tæ-djü eng-zo: *ngô* yin-dzæ tsʽia vong dziang-si kông gyi.

### H. X.

Lih-fah *peh-ko* yiu tsiang-læ hao-cʽü-go ing-tsong, m̆-teh keh-sing tong-si-go jih-we ying-ziang, sô-yi ih-ngæn feh neng-keo we-leh me-nyin dziang-tʽong sô hyin-zông-go tso-yiang tsi-veh s-teh keh-sing tseo-long-go cü-kwu djün-be.” <sup>2</sup> Ziah *neng-keo* z-ka, keh-sing voh-z-go cü-kwu ih-tsao long kyih-zing, gyi-go liang-sing yi-ʽeo ih-ngæn feh we kôh-djôh ze, keh hyin-tsi-go z-ken <sup>\[1\]</sup>kyʽi feh hao hyih-tsôh? <sup>3</sup> Jün-r keh-sing tsi-veh z *deh-we* nyin-nyin læ-tih di-sing *gyi-go* ze. <sup>4</sup> Keh-sing yüong-ngeo yüong-sæn-yiang-go hyüih tön feh neng-keo djü-diao ze.

律法不过有将来好处个影踪，呒得箇星东西个实惠形象，所以一眼弗能够为了每年长通所献上个做样祭物使得箇星走拢个主顾全备。<sup>2</sup> 若能够是介，箇星服侍个主顾一遭拢洁净，其个良心以后一眼弗会觉着罪，箇献祭个事干岂弗好歇作<sup>\[1\]</sup>？<sup>3</sup> 然而箇星祭物是特为年年来的提醒其个罪。<sup>4</sup> 箇星雄牛雄山羊个血断弗能够除掉罪。

### H. XII.

Sô-yi ah-lah kyi-jün yiu ka hyü-to kyin-tsing *ziang* yüing *ka* dön-kyʽün we-djü, ziu kæ sia-diao ih-tsʽih-go djong-tæn, teng yüong-yi dzin-ziao go ze-nyih; sô ts-ying ah-lah go lu-dzing, kæ næ-sing peng-zông-kyʽi; <sup>2</sup> *kæ* *z-djông* nyiang-vông Yiæ-su, *ziu-z* keh-we <sup>\[1\]</sup>shih-lih wun-djün *ah-lah-go* siang-sing Cü-kwu: Gyi, we-leh sô ts-tin Gyi go kwʽa-lôh, we jing-næ jih-z-kô-go *kwʽu-deo*, feh kwu tao-me; *yin-dzæ* zo læ Jing-ming zo-we-go jing-siu-pin. <sup>3</sup> Gyi yi-kying jing-næ ze-nying keh-cü-ka w̆ang-dzin, ng yüong s-tsʽeng Gyi; sæn-leh ng-lah bi-gyün, sing-li tʽeh-lih.

所以阿拉既然有介许多见证像云介团圈围住，就该卸掉一切个重担，等容易缠扰个罪孽；所指引阿拉个路程，该耐心奔上去；<sup>2</sup> 该时常仰望<u>耶稣</u>，就是箇位设立<sup>\[1\]</sup>完全阿拉个相信主顾。其，为了所指点其个快乐，会忍耐十字架个苦头，弗顾倒霉；现在坐来神明座位个顺手边。<sup>3</sup> 其已经忍耐罪人箇株介横缠，尔用思忖其；省勒尔拉疲倦，心里脱力。

### H. XIII.

KÆ z-djông tsʽing-æ hyüong-di. <sup>2</sup> Iao ing-gying tsih-dæ cʽih-meng-nying, feh-kʽo mông-kyi: ing-we *zin-deo* yiu nying z-ka tso-fah, feh-cü-feh-kôh we tsih-dæ tʽin-s. <sup>3</sup> Yüong kyi-nyiæn keh-sing be lin-diao so-djü-kæn-go, ziang ng-zi *teng* *gyi* dô-kô so-djü ka: *kyi-nyiæn* keh-sing ziu-næn-go, ing-we hyiao-teh ng-zi yia yiu *ziu-næn-go* kyi-sing. <sup>4</sup> Hweng-ing z-ken, cong-*nying* tu kæ kʽen-djong; gyi min-zông yia *kæ* m-yüô-tin-go: ziah-z ying-lön teng kæn-ying-go cü-kwu, Jing-ming we pʽun-tön gyi. <sup>5</sup> Tso-nying feh-kʽo dzeng tʽen-dzæ-go sing; sô yiu-go tong-si, kæ cü-tsoh; ing-we Jing-ming yiu ka wô, “Ngô feh we li-kʽæ ng, yia feh we kyʽi-diao ng:” \[Is. l. 5.\] <sup>6</sup> sô-yi ah-lah hao fông-tæn wô, “CÜ z ngô-go pông-siu; ngô feh pʽô nying-kô dza dæ ngô.” \[S. 118. 6.\]

该时常亲爱兄弟。<sup>2</sup> 要殷勤接待出门人，弗可忘记。因为前头有人是介做法，弗知弗觉会接待天使。<sup>3</sup> 用记念箇星被链条锁住间个，像尔自等其大家锁住介。记念箇星受难个，因为晓得尔自也有受难个肌身。<sup>4</sup> 婚姻事干，众人都该看重；其眠床也该呒瑕点个。若是淫乱等奸淫个主顾，神明会判断其。<sup>5</sup> 做人弗可存贪财个心；所有个东西，该知足。因为神明有介话，「我弗会离开尔，也弗会弃掉尔。」\[Is. l. 5.\] <sup>6</sup> 所以阿拉好放胆话，「主是我个帮手；我弗怕人家咋待我。」\[S. 118. 6.\]

<sup>10</sup> Ah-lah yiu ih-go tsi-dæn, gyi-go *li-veh* keh-sing tsiang-bong-li vong-z-go cü-kwu feh neng-keo kyʽüoh gyi. <sup>11</sup> Keh-sing sang-kʽeo gyi-go hyüih z tsi-s-deo sô ta-tsing sing-jün di-fông iao joh *nying-go* ze, gyi-go sing-tʽi z læ ying-za-nga be *ho* me-diao: <sup>12</sup> sô-yi Yiæ-su, iao pô Zi-go hyüih tseng-kyʽi pah-sing, yia læ dzing-meng-nga ziu kwʽu. <sup>13</sup> Ka-ni, ah-lah *yia* kæ tseo-cʽih ying-za-nga tao Gyi u-sen kyʽi, *dzing-nyün* tæn-tông Gyi-go zôh-mô. <sup>14</sup> Ah-lah dông-deo m̆-teh dziang læ-tong-go dzing-ts, z tæn gyiu keh-zo tsiang-læ-go.

<sup>10</sup> 阿拉有一个祭坛，其个礼物箇星帐篷里奉侍个主顾弗能够吃其。<sup>11</sup> 箇星牲口其个血是祭司头所带进圣善地方要赎人个罪，其个身体是来营寨外被火𤈦掉。<sup>12</sup> 所以<u>耶稣</u>，要把自个血尊起百姓，也来城门外受苦。<sup>13</sup> 介呢，阿拉也该走出营寨外到其乌碎去，情愿担当其个𧮙骂。<sup>14</sup> 阿拉荡头呒得长来东个城子，是单求箇座将来个。

<sup>15</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah kæ kʽao-djôh Yiæ-su pô tsæn-me-go tsi-veh z-kʽeh hyin-zông peh Jing-ming, ziu-z *ah-lah* kʽeo-li ken-zia Gyi ming-deo go ko-ts. <sup>16</sup> Ping-tsʽia kyʽi ʽang hao-z tsiu-tsi *nying-kô*, feh-kʽo mông-kyi: keh-cong-ka tsi-veh z Jing-ming sô hwun-hyi-go.
