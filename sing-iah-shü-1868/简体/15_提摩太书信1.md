## S-DU PAO-LO SIA PEH DI-MO-TʽA DI-IH-FONG SHÜ-SING. 使徒<u>保罗</u>写拨<u>提摩太</u>第一封书信

### 1D. I.

VONG ah-lah Kyiu-cü Jing-ming teng ah-lah sô nyiang-vông-go Cü Yiæ-su Kyi-toh-go ʽao-ling tso Yiæ-su Kyi-toh-go s-du, Pao-lo, <sup>2</sup> *mông-mông* Di-mo-tʽa, z we-leh siang-sing tso *ngô* tsing ng-ts go: Dæn-nyün Jing-ming ah-lah *Tʽin*-Vu, teng Kyi-toh Yiæ-su ah-lah Cü, s-lôh eng-we, æ-lin, bing-en *peh* ng.

奉阿拉救主神明等阿拉所仰望个主<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>个号令做<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>个使徒，<u>保罗</u>，<sup>2</sup> 望望<u>提摩太</u>，是为了相信做我真儿子个。但愿神明阿拉天父，等<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>阿拉主，赐落恩惠，哀怜，平安拨尔。

<sup>3</sup> NGÔ *zin-deo* tao Mô-gyi-teng kyʽi z-ʽeo tsʽing ng deng læ Yi-feh-sô, iao ng ting-coh keh kyi-go nying feh-kʽo kông bih-nyiang-kao dao-li, <sup>4</sup> yia feh-kʽo liu-sing m-ing-tsong-go shih-wô, teng m-gyüong-dzing ts-pʽa-go læ-lih: *ngô* *yin-dzæ* *iao* *ng* *yia* *z-ka*. Ing-we keh-sing z-ken tsih we kô-tsʽeo nyi-leng; feh neng-keo *s-teh* *nying* *we* *ʽeh-djôh* Jing-ming-go *ts-i* ka dzing-dziu teh-kyʽi, keh z siang-sing tso-cü.

<sup>3</sup> 我前头到<u>马其顿</u>去时候请尔庉来<u>以弗所</u>，要尔叮嘱箇几个人弗可讲别样告道理，<sup>4</sup> 也弗可留心呒影踪个说话，等呒穷尽支派个来历。我现在要尔也是介。因为箇星事干只会加凑议论；弗能够使得人会合着神明个旨意介成就德气，箇是相信做主。

<sup>5</sup> Næn-kæn sô ting-coh-go z-ken, gyi-go peng-i ziu-z jing-æ dzong kyih-zing-go sing-dziang, teng hao-go liang-sing, teng dzing-jih-go siang-sing ka cʽih-læ-go. <sup>6</sup> Yiu-sing nying <sup>\[1\]</sup>teng keh-sing z-ken tsʽô-kʽæ, fæn-cün hyiang-djôh hyü-wô; <sup>7</sup> iao-siang tso lih-fah-go sin-sang; gyi-lah sô kông-go shih-wô, sô kʽao-jih-go z-ken, zi tu feh ming-bah. <sup>8</sup> Ah-lah hyiao-teh lih-fah z hao-go, tsih iao nying yüong-leh teh fah; <sup>9</sup> iao hyiao-teh lih-fah feh-z we-leh tsing-dzih nying shih-lih-go, z we-leh vu-fah-vu-tʽin, w̆ang-tsʽih-dzin-pah-go, feh gyin-sing, yiu ze-ôh go, feh tsʽing-kyih-go, vông-tsôh-vông-we-go, tang tia-nyiang go, sah nying-kô go, <sup>10</sup> væn kæn-ying go, æ nen-fong go, fæn ma nying go, kông hwông-wô go, vah-tsiu feh sön-su go, ʽôh-tsia yiu soh-go bih-nyiang-kao teng tsing-kying dao-li tsôh-te-go z-ken,—— <sup>11</sup> *ziu-z* tsiao keh-we ʽong-foh Jing-ming yüong-wô-go foh-ing; keh-go foh-ing yia z yi-kying tʽôh-fu ngô go.

<sup>5</sup> 难间所叮嘱个事干，其个本意就是仁爱从洁净个心肠，等好个良心，等诚实个相信介出来个。<sup>6</sup> 有星人等箇星事干岔开<sup>\[1\]</sup>，反转向着虚话；<sup>7</sup> 要想做律法个先生；其拉所讲个说话，所靠实个事干，自都弗明白。<sup>8</sup> 阿拉晓得律法是好个，只要人用勒得法；<sup>9</sup> 要晓得律法弗是为了正直人设立个，是为了无法无天，横七缠八个，弗虔心，有罪恶个，弗清洁个，妄作妄为个，打爹娘个，杀人家个，<sup>10</sup> 犯奸淫个，爱男风个，贩卖人个，讲谎话个，罚咒弗算数个，或者有啥个别样告等正经道理作对个事干，——<sup>11</sup> 就是照箇位鸿福神明荣华个福音；箇个福音也是已经托付我个。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, tsʽô-kʽæ keh-da lu. 或话，岔开箇埭路。（原注）</sup>

<sup>12</sup> Næn-kæn ngô ken-zia keh-we công ngô-go tæn, Kyi-toh Yiæ-su ah-lah Cü, ing-we sön ngô yiu cong-sing, s-peh ngô bæn-z-go tsih-veng, <sup>13</sup> yi-zin z pông-hwe, pih-næn, bao-nyiah-go cü-kwu: dæn-z ngô we-leh feh hyiao-teh feh siang-sing ka tso-go, sô-yi *wa* neng-keo teh-djôh æ-lin. <sup>14</sup> Ping-tsʽia ah-lah Cü-go eng-we z kah-nga mun-tsoh, s-teh *ngô* yi we dzæ-ü Kyi-toh Yiæ-su dzeng siang-sing jing-æ-go sing. <sup>15</sup> Keh-kyü shih-wô z kyʽüôh-jih, kæ djün-jün tsih-ziu go, ziu-z, Kyi-toh Yiæ-su lôh-væn læ kyiu ze-nying; gyi-cong ngô z ze-kwʽe. <sup>16</sup> Ngô neng-keo teh-djôh æ-lin, z we-leh Yiæ-su Kyi-toh iao læ ngô-go sing-zông sin hyin-cʽih *Gyi* ih-tsʽih-go jing-næ, hao peh keh-sing tsiang-læ siang-sing Gyi teh-djôh üong-yün weh-ming go cü-kwu tso pông-yiang. <sup>17</sup> Dæn-nyün tʽi-min yüong-wô shü-shü-dæ-dæ kwe peh keh üong-yün-go Wông-ti, *ziu-z* ve wæ-diao, kʽen-feh-kyin, doh-ih-we tsʽong-ming-go Jing-ming. Üô-meng.

<sup>12</sup> 难间我感谢箇位壮我个胆，<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>阿拉主，因为算我有忠信，赐拨我办事个职份，<sup>13</sup> 以前是谤毁，逼难，暴虐个主顾。但是我为了弗晓得弗相信介做个，所以还能够得着哀怜。<sup>14</sup> 并且阿拉主个恩惠是格外满足，使得我又会在于<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>存相信仁爱个心。<sup>15</sup> 箇句说话是确实，该全然接受个，就是，<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>落凡来救罪人；其中我是罪魁。<sup>16</sup> 我能够得着哀怜，是为了<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>要来我个身上先显出其一切个忍耐，好拨箇星将来相信其得着永远活命个主顾做榜样。<sup>17</sup> 但愿体面荣华世世代代归拨箇永远个皇帝，就是𫧃坏掉，看弗见，独一位聪明个神明。亚们。

<sup>18</sup> *Ngô-go* ng-ts Di-mo-tʽa, keh ting-coh-go z-ken ngô tsiao zin-deo sô ts-tin ng go sin-cü shih-wô tʽôh-fu peh ng; iao ng kʽao-djôh keh-sing sin-cü shih-wô kyʽi jih-lin keh hao-hao-go kao-tsin; <sup>19</sup> *iao* dzeng siang-sing-go sing, teng hao-go liang-sing: ing-we yiu nying sông-diao keh-go liang-sing, lin gyi siang-sing tu tao-meh-de. <sup>20</sup> Hyü-mi-næ teng Üô-lih-sæn-da z keh-cü-ka nying: ngô yi-kying kao-fu gyi peh Sah-dæn, s-teh gyi we-leh ziu kao-hyüing feh ken kông sih-doh-go shih-wô.

<sup>18</sup> 我个儿子<u>提摩太</u>，箇叮嘱个事干我照前头所指点尔个先知说话托付拨尔；要尔靠着箇星先知说话去习练箇好好个交战；<sup>19</sup> 要存相信个心，等好个良心。因为有人丧掉箇个良心，连其相信都倒没了。<sup>20</sup> <u>许米乃</u>等<u>亚力山大</u>是箇株介人。我已经交付其拨<u>撒但</u>，使得其为了受教训弗敢讲亵渎个说话。

### 1D. II.

NÆN-KÆN, ting iao-kying, ngô kyʽün *ng* yüong we-leh cong-nying gyiu-gyiu, tao-kao, tsʽæn-nyiæn, coh-zia: <sup>2</sup> we-leh keh-sing kyüing-wông, teng væn-pah <sup>\[1\]</sup>yiu tsiah-we go, *iao* *gyiu-gyiu*; s-teh ah-lah kʽo-yi tʽa-tʽa-bing-bing en-en-tæn-tæn yiu ih-tsʽih-go gyin-dzing teng tön-tsing ko nyih-ts. <sup>3</sup> Keh z hao-go, z ah-lah Kyiu-cü Jing-ming sô hwun-hyi-go; <sup>4</sup> Gyi z iao cong-nying teh-kyiu, ming-ming sih-teh tsing dao-li. <sup>5</sup> Ing-we Jing-ming tsih-yiu ih-we; Jing-ming teng nying-go cong-nyiang yia tsih-yiu ih-we Cong-pao, *ziu-z* Kyi-toh Yiæ-su *keh-go* Nying: <sup>6</sup> Gyi z sô-cʽih Zi-go *sing-ming* tso cong-nying-go tʽi-dæ; z-ʽeo ih tao, <sup>\[2\]</sup>keh-go te-tsing *ziu* *iao* *djün-kʽæ*. <sup>7</sup> Ngô we-leh keh-go te-tsing be shih-lih tso djün-dao-go, tso s-du, tso bih-koh-nying-go sin-sang, kao gyi siang-sing *Cü*, *hyiao-teh* tsing dao-li: ngô z dzæ-ü Kyi-toh kông tsing-wô, bing m-neh shih-hwông.

难间，顶要紧，我劝尔用为了众人求求，祷告，忏念，祝谢。<sup>2</sup> 为了箇星君王，等凡百有爵位个<sup>\[1\]</sup>，要求求；使得阿拉可以太太平平安安耽耽有一切个虔诚等端正过日子。<sup>3</sup> 箇是好个，是阿拉救主神明所欢喜个；<sup>4</sup> 其是要众人得救，明明识得真道理。<sup>5</sup> 因为神明只有一位；神明等人个中央也只有一位中保，就是<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>箇个人。<sup>6</sup> 其是舍出自个性命做众人个替代；时候一到，箇个对证就要传开<sup>\[2\]</sup>。<sup>7</sup> 我为了箇个对证被设立做传道个，做使徒，做别国人个先生，教其相信主，晓得真道理。我是在于<u>基督</u>讲真话，并呒得说谎。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, dzæ-zông yiu gyün-ping-go. 或话，在上有权柄个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, *ziu* *yiu* *ming-tông-tông-go* te-tsing. 或话，就有明当当个对证。（原注）</sup>

<sup>8</sup> Ka-ni, ngô iao keh-sing nen-nyüing kong-kyʽi kyih-zing-go siu, m-neh ün-ʽeng, m-neh nyi-sing, cʽü-cʽü tso tao-kao. <sup>9</sup> Wa-yiu keh-sing nyü-nying *iao* *gyi* w̆eng-tông, æ lin-cʽü, yiu tön-tsing i-voh go tsông-sih; feh-kʽo pin deo-fah, ta kying-cü pao-pe, cʽün kyü-kyü i-zông; <sup>10</sup> z *iao* ʽang hao-z: keh z tsiao-jing kying-djong Jing-ming go nyü-nying sô siang-nyi-go *tsông-sih*.

<sup>8</sup> 介呢，我要箇星男𭑸拱起洁净个手，呒得怨恨，呒得疑心，处处做祷告。<sup>9</sup> 还有箇星女人要其稳当，爱廉耻，有端正衣服个装饰；弗可编头发，戴金珠宝贝，穿贵贵衣裳；<sup>10</sup> 是要行好事。箇是招认敬重神明个女人所相宜个装饰。

<sup>11</sup> Nyü-nying kæ en-en-zing-zing ʽôh *dao-li*, yiang-yiang *z-ken* i-jing kwe-voh. <sup>12</sup> Ngô feh hyü nyü-nying kao-hyüing, yia *feh* *hyü* *gyi* ah-voh nen-nyüing; z *iao* *gyi* en-en-zing-zing. <sup>13</sup> Ing-we z Üô-tông sin zao, Yüô-w̆ô dzæ-ʽeo. <sup>14</sup> Wa-yiu feh-z Üô-tông be yiu-ʽôh; z nyü-nying be yiu-ʽôh, ʽæn-lôh ze-li go. <sup>15</sup> Dæn-z *nyü-nying* we-leh sang ng-nô kʽo-yi teh-kyiu; tsih iao gyi-lah dziang dzeng siang-sing, jing-æ, sing-jün, teng w̆eng-tông-go sing.

<sup>11</sup> 女人该安安静静学道理，样样事干依顺归服。<sup>12</sup> 我弗许女人教训，也弗许其压服男𭑸；是要其安安静静。<sup>13</sup> 因为是<u>亚当</u>先造，夏娃在后。<sup>14</sup> 还有弗是<u>亚当</u>被诱惑；是女人被诱惑，陷落罪里个。<sup>15</sup> 但是女人为了生儿囡可以得救；只要其拉长存相信，仁爱，圣善，等稳当个心。

### 1D. III.

ZIAH yiu nying iao-siang tso kyin-toh, z kyʽi-mo ih-yiang hao-hao z-ken: keh z ih-kyü kyʽüôh-jih-go shih-wô. <sup>2</sup> Tso kyin-toh go tông-kæ m-pʽi-bing, tsih yiu ih-go lao-nyüing, *tso-nying* kying-jing, w̆eng-tông, <sup>\[1\]</sup>tön-tsing, ing-gying dæ kʽah, jün-ü kao-hyüing; <sup>3</sup> feh-kʽo <sup>\[2\]</sup>tsiu-tse-wu-du, feh-kʽo zao-nyih ôh-cü, feh-kʽo tʽen fi-li-ts-dzæ; z iao w̆eng-ziu, ʽo-kyʽi, feh dzeng tʽen-sing. <sup>4</sup> *Pih* *iao* hao-hao djü gyi-zi-go ih-kô, *iao* gyi ng-nô tön-tön-tsing-tsing voh gyi kæ-kwun: <sup>5</sup> (ih-go nying ziah feh hyiao-teh djü gyi-zi-go ih-kô, dza neng-keo tsiao-kwun Jing-ming-go kyiao-we ni?) <sup>6</sup> *yi* feh-z tsʽu jih-kyiao cü-kwu, kʽong-pʽô sang kyiao-ngao-wu-du-go sing, ziu ʽæn-lôh læ mo-kwe-go ying-vah li-hyiang. <sup>7</sup> Ping-tsʽia pih iao læ kyiao-nga-nying u-sen yiu hao ming-sing, kʽong-pʽô be *nying-kô* tsao-tʽah, tih-lôh mo-kwe-go kyʽün-tʽao.

若有人要想做监督，是企慕一样好好事干。箇是一句确实个说话。<sup>2</sup> 做监督个当该呒批评，只有一个老𭑸，做人谨慎，稳当，端正<sup>\[1\]</sup>，殷勤待客，擅于教训；<sup>3</sup> 弗可酒醉糊涂<sup>\[2\]</sup>，弗可造孽恶嘴，弗可贪非礼之财；是要温柔，和气，弗存贪心。<sup>4</sup> 必要好好治其自个一家，要其儿囡端端正正服其该管。<sup>5</sup> （一个人若弗晓得治其自个一家，咋能够照管神明个教会呢？）<sup>6</sup> 又弗是初入教主顾，恐怕生骄傲糊涂个心，就陷落来魔鬼个刑罚里向。<sup>7</sup> 并且必要来教外人乌碎有好名声，恐怕被人家糟蹋，跌落魔鬼个圈套。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kwe-kyü. 或话，规矩。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, seng-z cʽông-ʽo, ziang tse-nying ka. 或话，生事闯祸，像醉人介。（原注）</sup>

<sup>8</sup> Keh-sing bæn-z-nying yia *tông-kæ* tön-tsông, vu-r-yin, feh-kʽo to kyʽüoh-tsiu, feh-kʽo tʽen fi-li-ts-dzæ; <sup>9</sup> keh sô siang-sing-go ao-miao *foh-ing* iao dzeng læ kyih-zing-go liang-sing-li. <sup>10</sup> Keh-sing nying, yia kæ sin s-s gyi; ziah ko-jün z m-pʽi-bing, ziu hao peh gyi kyʽi bæn-z. <sup>11</sup> Keh-sing *bæn-z-go* nyü-nying yia *tông-kæ* tön-tsông, feh-kʽo pun-cü, iao kying-jing, væn-pah z-ken yiu cong-sing. <sup>12</sup> Bæn-z-nying pih iao tsih yiu ih-go lao-nyüing, hao-hao djü ng-nô teng gyi-zi ih-kô-go nying. <sup>13</sup> Ing-we keh-sing jün-ü <sup>\[1\]</sup>bæn-z cü-kwu *z-jün* we teh-djôh me-kyʽüih; kông-tao siang-sing Kyi-toh Yiæ-su, yia we do-nyiang fông-tæn.

<sup>8</sup> 箇星办事人也当该端庄，无二言，弗可多吃酒，弗可贪非礼之财；<sup>9</sup> 箇所相信个奥妙福音要存来洁净个良心里。<sup>10</sup> 箇星人，也该先试试其；若果然是呒批评，就好拨其去办事。<sup>11</sup> 箇星办事个女人也当该端庄，弗可搬嘴，要谨慎，凡百事干有忠信。<sup>12</sup> 办事人必要只有一个老𭑸，好好治儿囡等其自一家个人。<sup>13</sup> 因为箇星擅于办事<sup>\[1\]</sup>主顾自然会得着美缺；讲到相信<u>基督</u> <u>耶稣</u>，也会大样放胆。

<sup>\[1\] *Keh* *liang* *tsih* *wô* bæn-z, *ʽôh-tsia* *fæn*, tso tsʽa-s; *ʽôh-tsia*, kong-ing; *ʽôh-tsia*, voh-z. 箇两节话办事，或者翻，做差使；或者，供应；或者，服侍。（原注）</sup>

<sup>14</sup> NGÔ sia keh-sing z-ken peh ng, *nyün* tang-tsiang ziu tao ng u-sen læ; <sup>15</sup> tsih-z kʽong-pʽô dzi-ngwu, iao peh ng hyiao-teh læ Jing-ming-go oh-lô kæ dza-go tso-shih: keh-go oh-lô ziu-z weh Jing-ming-go kyiao-we, tsing dao-li-go oh-djü teng <sup>\[1\]</sup>di-kyi. <sup>16</sup> Keh gyin-dzing-go dao-li kyʽüôh-kyʽüôh-jih-jih z ao-miao fi-væn: Jing-ming dzing we nyüoh-sing hyin-cʽih-læ, dzæ-ü *Sing*-Ling te-tsing z tsing-dzih-go, peh tʽin-s kʽen-kyin-go, læ bih-koh djün-kʽæ-go, læ tʽin-ʽô siang-sing-go, læ yüong-wô li-hyiang sing-zông-go.

<sup>14</sup> 我写箇星事干拨尔，原打账就到尔乌碎来；<sup>15</sup> 只是恐怕迟误，要拨尔晓得来神明个屋落该咋个做说。箇个屋落就是活神明个教会，真道理个屋柱等地基<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>16</sup> 箇虔诚个道理确确实实是奥妙非凡。神明成为肉身显出来，在于圣灵对证是正直个，拨天使看见个，来别国传开个，来天下相信个，来荣华里向升上个。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, sông-bun-zah. 或话，磉盘石。（原注）</sup>

### 1D. IV.

Jün-r *Sing*-Ling z ming-ming wô, Tsiang-læ yi-ʽeo yiu nying we tʽing-sing tsʽô-lu-go *zia*-ling, ôh-kyü-go yi-tön, kyʽi li-kʽæ sô siang-sing-go dao-li; <sup>2</sup> *ziu-z* we-leh shih-hwông cü-kwu-go kô-hao z-ken: *keh-sing* *shih-hwông-go* *nying* zi-go liang-sing yi-kying siao-mih-de, <sup>3</sup> feh hyü cʽü-tsing kô-cʽih, iao ka-diao *hyü-to* kyʽüoh-zih, ziu-z Jing-ming sô zao peh cong siang-sing hyiao-teh tsing dao-li cü-kwu hao zia-zia *Gyi* do-læ kyʽüoh go. <sup>4</sup> Ing-we Jing-ming ih-tsʽih sô zao tu z hao-go, m-yiu ih-yiang hao tʽih-cʽih, tsih-siao zia-zia *Jing-ming* ka tsih-ziu gyi: <sup>5</sup> we-leh Jing-ming-go dao-li teng tsʽæn-nyiæn-go shih-wô, *tu* z be tseng-kyʽi-go.

然而圣灵是明明话，将来以后有人会听信岔路个邪灵，恶鬼个异端，去离开所相信个道理；<sup>2</sup> 就是为了说谎主顾个假好事干。箇星说谎个人自个良心已经消灭了，<sup>3</sup> 弗许娶进嫁出，要戒掉许多吃食，就是神明所造拨众相信晓得真道理主顾好谢谢其驮来吃个。<sup>4</sup> 因为神明一切所造都是好个，呒有一样好踢出，只消谢谢神明介接受其。<sup>5</sup> 为了神明个道理等忏念个说话，都是被尊起个。

<sup>6</sup> Ng tsiang keh-sing z-ken di-sing hyüong-di, ziu hao tso Yiæ-su Kyi-toh-go hao-hao tsʽa-s, z ng siang-sing-go *tsing* dao-li, ʽôh-jih-go hao kao-hyüing, keh-sing shih-wô sô dzing-dziu-go. <sup>7</sup> Yüong kyʽi-diao keh-sing feh tseng-kyʽi m-ing-tsong-go shih-wô, z ziang lao-tʽa-bo *vu-kyi-ts* *dæn* ka: yüong jih-lin zi-go gyin-dzing. <sup>8</sup> Jih-lin kyi-sing go ih-cʽü <sup>\[1\]</sup>læ-leh siao; tsih-yiu gyin-dzing ü væn-pah z-ken tu yiu ih-cʽü, z ing-hyü gyi yiu kying-si teng ʽô-si-go weh-ming. <sup>9</sup> Keh z ih-kyü kyʽüôh-jih-go shih-wô, kæ djün-jün tsih-ziu go. <sup>10</sup> Ah-lah we-leh ka yia lao-loh, be tsao-siao, ing-we z nyiang-vông keh-we weh-go Jing-ming; Gyi z cong-nying-go Kyiu-cü, yü-kô *we* *kyiu* siang-sing-go cü-kwu.

<sup>6</sup> 尔将箇星事干提醒兄弟，就好做<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>个好好差使，是尔相信个真道理，学习个好教训，箇星说话所成就个。<sup>7</sup> 用弃掉箇星弗尊起呒影踪个说话，是像老太婆无稽之谈介；用习练自个虔诚。<sup>8</sup> 习练肌身个益处来勒小<sup>\[1\]</sup>；只有虔诚于凡百事干都有益处，是应许其有今世等下世个活命。<sup>9</sup> 箇是一句确实个说话，该全然接受个。<sup>10</sup> 阿拉为了介也劳碌，被嘲笑，因为是仰望箇位活个神明；其是众人个救主，愈加会救相信个主顾。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, z dzæn-z-go. 或话，是暂时个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Keh-sing z-ken ng yüong ting-coh *gyi*, kao-hyüing gyi. <sup>12</sup> M-nao peh nying-kô kʽen-kyʽing ng-go nyin-kyi siao: tsong iao s-teh yin-nyü, ʽang-ts, jing-æ, siang-sing, tsʽing-kyih, hao peh keh-sing siang-sing cü-kwu tso pông-yiang. <sup>13</sup> Yüong liu-sing kyʽi doh *shü*, kyʽün-hwô, kao-hyüing, ih-dzih teng-tao ngô læ. <sup>14</sup> Ng sô yiu-go eng-s, ziu-z yüong sin-cü shih-wô teng tsiang-lao en-siu go *li-tsih* sô s-peh ng go, m-nao feh kwu-djôh gyi. <sup>15</sup> Ng yüong jih-lin keh-sing z-ken, ih-sing hyiang-djôh gyi; <sup>\[1\]</sup>hao peh cong-nying ming-ming kʽen-kyin ng kong-fu-go tsing-ih. <sup>16</sup> Ng yüong kwu-djôh zi, yia *kwu-djôh* sô kao-hyüing-go dao-li; z-kʽeh liu-sing keh-sing z-ken: z-ka tso-shih, ziu we kyiu zi, teng keh-sing tʽing ng go cü-kwu.

<sup>11</sup> 箇星事干尔用叮嘱其，教训其。<sup>12</sup> 呒得好拨人家看轻尔个年纪小；总要使得言语，行止，仁爱，相信，清洁，好拨箇星相信主顾做榜样。<sup>13</sup> 用留心去读书，劝化，教训，一直等到我来。<sup>14</sup> 尔所有个恩赐，就是用先知说话等长老按手个礼节所赐拨尔个，呒得好弗顾着其。<sup>15</sup> 尔用习练箇星事干，一心向着其；好拨众人明明看见尔工夫个进益<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>16</sup> 尔用顾着自，也顾着所教训个道理；时刻留心箇星事干。是介做说，就会救自，等箇星听尔个主顾。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, s-teh nying-kô læ yiang-yiang z-ken hao kʽen-kyin. 或话，使得人家来样样事干好看见。（原注）</sup>

### 1D. V.

LAO-DZING-NYING, feh-kʽo heng-hô *gyi*, iao kyʽün *gyi* ziang ah-tia ka; siao-nyin-nying *iao* ziang hyüong-di ka; <sup>2</sup> lao-dzing nyü-nying, iao ziang ah-nyiang ka; siao-nyin nyü-nying, *iao* ts-ts-dzing-dzing ziang tsi-me ka *kyʽün* *gyi*.

老成人，弗可狠呵其，要劝其像阿爹介；少年人要像兄弟介；<sup>2</sup> 老成女人，要像阿娘介；少年女人，要致致诚诚像姊妹介劝其。

<sup>3</sup> Keh-sing kwu-sông lao-nyüing li-hyiang tsing-tsing z kwu-sông go, yüong tseng-kying gyi. <sup>4</sup> Ziah-z kwu-sông lao-nyüing yiu ng-ts yiu seng-ts, tông-kæ kao gyi sin iao <sup>\[1\]</sup>gyin-gyin-sing-sing dæ gyi-zi-go ih-kô, pao do-nying-go eng: keh z hao-go, z Jing-ming sô hwun-hyi-go. <sup>5</sup> Keh tsing-tsing tso kwu-sông doh-zi dzing-lôh-tong-go, tsih-yiu nyiang-vông Jing-ming, nyih-yia dziang læ-tih gyiu-gyiu tao-kao: <sup>6</sup> ziah-z <sup>\[2\]</sup>æ shæ-wô tsôh-lôh go, gyi *se-tsih* weh-tong, *jih-dzæ* z si. <sup>7</sup> Keh-sing z-ken ng yüong ting-coh *gyi-lah*, hao s-teh gyi tso-nying m-pʽi-bing. <sup>8</sup> Tʽông-jün yiu nying feh kwu-djôh zi-go nying, z kyʽi-diao sô siang-sing-go dao-li, pi feh siang-sing-go cü-kwu keng-kô w̆a: hwông-tsʽia *feh* *kwu-djôh* gyi-zi ih-kô-go nying ma?

<sup>3</sup> 箇星孤孀老𭑸里向真正是孤孀个，用尊敬其。<sup>4</sup> 若是孤孀老𭑸有儿子有孙子，当该教其先要虔虔心心待其自个一家，报大人个恩<sup>\[1\]</sup>。箇是好个，是神明所欢喜个。<sup>5</sup> 箇真正做孤孀独自剩落东个，只有仰望神明，日夜长来的求求祷告。<sup>6</sup> 若是爱奢华作乐<sup>\[2\]</sup>个，其虽即活东，实在是死。<sup>7</sup> 箇星事干尔用叮嘱其拉，好使得其做人呒批评。<sup>8</sup> 倘然有人弗顾着自个人，是弃掉所相信个道理，比弗相信个主顾更加坏。况且弗顾着其自一家个人吗？

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, hyiao-jing zi-kô-nying. *Keh-tsih* *shü* *sô* *wô*, *ʽôh* *kông* *keh-tsih* *kwu-sông* *kæ* *gyin-sing* *dæ* *zi-go* *ts-seng*; *ʽôh* *kông* *ts-seng* *kæ* *hyiao-jing* *zi* *oh-li-go* *kwu-sông*. 或话，孝顺自家人。箇节书所话，或讲箇节孤孀该虔心待自个子孙；或讲子孙该孝顺自屋里个孤孀。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, yiu-hyi-lông-dông. 或话，游戏浪荡。（原注）</sup>

<sup>9</sup> <sup>\[1\]</sup>Kwu-sông lao-nyüing zông tsʽah-ts, ting siao pih iao loh-jih shü, tsih pʽe ih-go nen-nyüing, <sup>10</sup> yiu hao ʽang-we go ming-sing, ziang fu-yiang siao-nying, siu-liu cʽih-meng-nying, gyiang sing-du-go kyiah, pông-dzu lôh-næn-go nying, teng liu-sing ʽang ih-tsʽih hao-z go. <sup>11</sup> Ziah-z siao-nyin kwu-sông, yüong we-foh gyi: kʽong-pʽô yiu z-ʽeo dzeng s-sing we-nyih Kyi-toh, iao-siang kô nying. <sup>12</sup> (Keh z yiu ze, ing-we we-be gyi yi-zin-go sing-iah.) <sup>13</sup> Gyi yia dön-kyʽün gyiah nying-kô, kwæn tʽen læn-do; fi-dæn tʽen læn, wa iao kyʽing-cü-boh-zih, tæn kwun ʽæn-tsiang, kông feh siang-nyi-go shih-wô. <sup>14</sup> Sô-yi ngô iao siao-nyin *kwu-sông* neng-s kô nying, sang ng-nô, kwun kô-z, ih-ngæn m-nao s-teh dziu-dih yiu tsao-siao-go kyi-we: <sup>15</sup> ing-we yiu-sing z yi-kying cün-w̆æn hyiang-djôh Sah-dæn de.

<sup>9</sup> 孤孀老𭑸上册子<sup>\[1\]</sup>，顶小必要六十岁，只配一个男𭑸，<sup>10</sup> 有好行为个名声，像抚养小人，收留出门人，滰圣徒个脚，帮助落难个人，等留心行一切好事个。<sup>11</sup> 若是少年孤孀，用回复其。恐怕有时候存私心违逆<u>基督</u>，要想嫁人。<sup>12</sup> （箇是有罪，因为违背其以前个信约。）<sup>13</sup> 其也团圈𧾵人家，惯贪懒惰；非但贪懒，还要轻嘴薄舌，单管闲账，讲弗相宜个说话。<sup>14</sup> 所以我要少年孤孀能使嫁人，生儿囡，管家事，一眼呒得好使得仇敌有嘲笑个机会。<sup>15</sup> 因为有星是已经转弯向着<u>撒但</u>了。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, zông kwu-sông tsʽah-ts. 或话，上孤孀册子。（原注）</sup>

<sup>16</sup> Ziah yiu siang-sing-go nen, ʽôh-tsia siang-sing-go nyü, *gyi-zi* *oh-li* yiu kwu-sông lao-nyüing, *zi* yüong kyʽi pông-dzu gyi, m-nao siang-væn kyiao-we; s-teh *kyiao-we* hao pông-dzu keh-sing tsing-tsing kwu-sông-go lao-nyüing.

<sup>16</sup> 若有相信个男，或者相信个女，其自屋里有孤孀老𭑸，自用去帮助其，呒得好相烦教会；使得教会好帮助箇星真正孤孀个老𭑸。

<sup>17</sup> Keh-sing jün-ü tsiao-kwun go tsiang-lao, kæ kô-be hyiao-kying gyi; lao-lao-loh-loh kông-kying djün-kyiao-go, *kæ* yü-kô *z-ka* *dæ* *gyi*: <sup>18</sup> ing-we Sing-shü yiu ka wô, “Nyin-koh-go ngeo, feh-kʽo tʽao gyi-go cü-pô;” \[Sm. 25. 4.\] yi wô, “Kong-nying teh-djôh gyi kong-din, z ing-kæ-go.” \[Mt. 10. 10. Lk. 10. 7.\] <sup>19</sup> Ziah yiu nying læ kao tsiang-lao zông, m̆-teh liang-sæn-go nying tso te-tsing, feh-kʽo cing gyi. <sup>20</sup> Yiu væn-ze-go cü-kwu, iao læ cong-nying-go min-zin pʽi-bing gyi; s-teh bih-nying we pʽô-gyü.

<sup>17</sup> 箇星擅于照管个长老，该加倍孝敬其；劳劳碌碌讲经传教个，该愈加是介待其。<sup>18</sup> 因为圣书有介话，「碾谷个牛，弗可套其个嘴巴；」\[Sm. 25. 4.\] 又话，「工人得着其工钿，是应该个。」\[Mt. 10. 10. Lk. 10. 7.\] <sup>19</sup> 若有人来告长老状，呒得两三个人做对证，弗可准其。<sup>20</sup> 有犯罪个主顾，要来众人个面前批评其；使得别人会怕惧。

<sup>21</sup> Ngô læ Jing-ming teng Cü Yiæ-su Kyi-toh lin tʽiao-shün-go tʽin-s min-zin hyüih-sing kyʽün ng yüong pao-siu keh-sing z-ken, *ih-ngæn* m-nao <sup>\[1\]</sup>tsih dzing-kyin, ih-ngæn m-nao cing min-dzing.

<sup>21</sup> 我来神明等主<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>连挑选个天使面前血心劝尔用保守箇星事干，一眼呒得好执成见<sup>\[1\]</sup>，一眼呒得好准面情。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kyʽing-yi tön nying-kô-go en-gyin: *kʽen* Sm. 17. 4: 19. 18. 或话，轻易断人家个案件。看 Sm. 17. 4: 19. 18。（原注）</sup>

<sup>22</sup> M-nao kyʽing-yi pô siu en-en nying-kô; yia m-nao tsao-djôh bih-nying-go ze. Zi yüong pao-siu tsʽing-kyih. <sup>23</sup> Ng yi-ʽeo m-nao tæn kyʽüoh lang shü, neng-s shü-vi kyʽüoh-tin tsiu, we-leh ng-go bi-we teng ng le-tsʽ-go bing-tʽong.

<sup>22</sup> 呒得好轻易把手按按人家；也呒得好遭着别人个罪。自用保守清洁。<sup>23</sup> 尔以后呒得好单吃冷水，能使势微吃点酒，为了尔个脾胃等尔屡次个病痛。

<sup>24</sup> Yiu-sing nying-go ze ming-ming pa-cʽih-tong, z sin-jü *gyi* tao sing-pʽun *di-fông*; yiu-sing *nying-go* ze z dzong ʽeo-deo ka keng-leh-læ-go. <sup>25</sup> *Yiu-sing* *nying-go* hao ʽang-we yia z ming-ming pa-cʽih-tong: tsih-s feh-z ka, yia *cong-ü* feh neng-keo ing-dzông.

<sup>24</sup> 有星人个罪明明摆出东，是先如其到审判地方；有星人个罪是从后头介跟勒来个。<sup>25</sup> 有星人个好行为也是明明摆出东。即使弗是介，也终于弗能够隐藏。

### 1D. VI.

Væn-pah zông-ah-liao tso nu-boh go cü-kwu, tu kæ jih-feng kying-djong zi-go cü-nying-kô, sæn-leh yiu nying pông-hwe Jing-ming-go ming-deo teng *Gyi-go* kao-hyüing. <sup>2</sup> Wa-yiu gyi cü-nying-kô ziah-z siang-sing-go, feh-kʽo we-leh gyi tso hyüong-di ziu kyʽing-kʽen *gyi*; z kæ yü-kô voh-z *gyi*; ing-we teh-djôh keh ziu-yüong go cü-kwu z <sup>\[1\]</sup>cong-sing tsʽing-æ-go. Keh-sing z-ken ng yüong hyüing-hwe *gyi*, kyʽün-kyʽün *gyi*.

凡百上轭了做奴仆个主顾，都该十分敬重自个主人家，省勒有人谤毁神明个名头等其个教训。<sup>2</sup> 还有其主人家若是相信个，弗可为了其做兄弟就轻看其；是该愈加服侍其；因为得着箇受用个主顾是忠信亲爱个<sup>\[1\]</sup>。箇星事干尔用训诲其，劝劝其。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, siang-sing-go. 或话，相信个。（原注）</sup>

<sup>3</sup> Ziah yiu nying kông bih-nyiang-kao dao-li, feh i ah-lah Cü Yiæ-su Kyi-toh tsing-kying-go shih-wô teng keh gyin-dzing-go kao-hyüing; <sup>4</sup> gyi z <sup>\[1\]</sup>kyiao-ngao wu-du, ih-ngæn feh ming-bah, gyi sing cün-pin iao hyiang-djôh keh-sing nyi-leng teng kʽong-deo-go gyiang-bin: sô-yi sang-cʽih tu-gyi, tsang-zao, pông-hwe, ôh-i, <sup>\[2\]</sup>dzin-wô, <sup>5</sup> *ziu-z* keh-sing w̆ang sing-dziang ʽao-vu tsing dao-li cü-kwu-go <sup>\[2\]</sup>*dzin-wô*; gyi-lah pô gyin-dzing sön z teh-li-go fông-fah: keh-cü-ka nying yüong yün-kʽæ gyi.

<sup>3</sup> 若有人讲别样告道理，弗依阿拉主<u>耶稣</u> <u>基督</u>正经个说话等箇虔诚个教训；<sup>4</sup> 其是骄傲糊涂<sup>\[1\]</sup>，一眼弗明白，其心转变要向着箇星议论等空头个强辩。所以生出妒忌，争造，谤毁，恶意，缠话<sup>\[2\]</sup>，<sup>5</sup> 就是箇星横心肠毫无真道理主顾个缠话<sup>\[2\]</sup>；其拉把虔诚算是得利个方法。箇株介人用远开其。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, zi tsʽing zi hyüing. 或话，自称自勋。（原注）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, w̆ang-dzin-go bin-leng. 或话，横缠个辩论。（原注）</sup>

<sup>6</sup> Jün-r gyin-dzing teng cü-tsoh *jih-dzæ* z do li-sih. <sup>7</sup> Ah-lah cʽih-si, m-kao soh-go ta-læ; *kyʽü-shü*, yia ming-ming m-kao soh-go hao ta-kyʽi. <sup>8</sup> Yiu-leh kyʽüoh-zih i-cʽün, ah-lah ziu yüong cü-tsoh. <sup>9</sup> Ziah-z siang fah-dzæ go cü-kwu, z ʽæn-lôh mi-ʽôh, teng kyʽün-tʽao, teng hyü-to nyü-cʽing yiu-ʽæ-go s-yüôh li-hyiang; keh-sing *s-yüôh* tsih we s-teh nying dzing-meh, peh gyi tsao-djôh ʽo-se kwe-ü mih-vông. <sup>10</sup> Tʽen-dzæ-go sing z væn ôh-go keng-deo: yiu nying kʽen-siang gyi, ziu <sup>\[1\]</sup>teng siang-sing-go dao-li tsʽô-kʽæ, pô hyü-to tʽong-kwʽu zi cʽoh-tsing Zi-go *sing-li*.

<sup>6</sup> 然而虔诚等知足实在是大利息。<sup>7</sup> 阿拉出世，呒告啥个带来；去世，也明明呒告啥个好带去。<sup>8</sup> 有了吃食衣穿，阿拉就用知足。<sup>9</sup> 若是想发财个主顾，是陷落迷惑，等圈套，等许多愚蠢有害个私欲里向；箇星私欲只会使得人沉没，拨其遭着祸祟归于灭亡。<sup>10</sup> 贪财个心是万恶个根头。有人看相其，就等相信个道理岔开<sup>\[1\]</sup>，把许多痛苦自戳进自个心里。
