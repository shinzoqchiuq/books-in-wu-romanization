## S-DU PAO-LO SIA PEH HYI-PAH-LÆ NYING GO SHÜ-SING. 使徒<u>保羅</u>寫撥<u>希伯來</u>人個書信

### H. I.

DZONG-ZIN Jing-ming dzæ-ü keh-sing sin-cü-nying yüong hyü-to fông-fah le-tsʽ wô hyiang tsu-tsong dao; <sup>2</sup> tao keh-sing kyih-sah-go nyih-ts dzæ-ü *Gyi-go* Ng-ts wô hyiang ah-lah dao: *Jing-ming* z shih-lih Gyi hao tsih-djôh væn-veh tso tsʽæn-nyih, yi z tʽôh Gyi tsʽông-zao tʽin-di. <sup>3</sup> Gyi z *Jing-ming-go* yüong-wô sô tʽeo-cʽih-go kwông-tsʽæ, Jing-ming-go tʽi-tsih sô yin-cʽih weh-tʽeh-ko-go siang-mao; yiu djün-neng-go ʽao-ling tsih-công væn-veh; kyi-jün sô-cʽih Zi-go sing-ming gyiang-diao ah-lah-go ze, ziu zo læ *ting* kao *u-dông*, w̆e-nyin *Cü*-go jing-siu-pin: <sup>4</sup> tseng-jü keh-sing tʽin-s, ing-we tsih-djôh keng-kô tseng-djong-go ngæn-deo.

從前神明在於箇星先知人用許多方法屢次話向祖宗道；<sup>2</sup> 到箇星結煞個日子，在於其個兒子話向阿拉道。神明是設立其好接着萬物做產業，又是託其創造天地。<sup>3</sup> 其是神明個榮華所透出個光彩，神明個體質所現出活脫過個相貌；有全能個號令執掌萬物；既然捨出自個性命滰掉阿拉個罪，就坐來頂高烏蕩，威嚴主個順手邊。<sup>4</sup> 尊如箇星天使，因爲接着更加尊重個銜頭。

<sup>5</sup> *Jing-ming* ʽo-dzeng teng ʽah-li ih-go tʽin-s yiu ka wô, “Ng z Ngô-go Ng-ts, Ngô kyih-mih sang Ng,” \[S. 2. 7.\] wa-yiu, “Ngô we tso Gyi Ah-tia, Gyi we tso Ngô-go Ng-ts” \[2S. 7. 14.\] ma? <sup>6</sup> <sup>\[1\]</sup>Wa-yiu *Jing-ming* ta-leh *Gyi* Tsiang-ts tseo-tsing shü-kæn-zông læ z-ʽeo, yiu ka wô, “Jing-ming-go cong tʽin-s tu kæ pa Gyi.” \[S. 97. 7.\]

<sup>5</sup> 神明何曾等何裏一個天使有介話，「爾是我個兒子，我今末生爾」，\[S. 2. 7.\] 還有，「我會做其阿爹，其會做我個兒子」\[2S. 7. 14.\] 嗎？<sup>6</sup> 還有神明帶勒<sup>\[1\]</sup>其長子走進世間上來時候，有介話，「神明個衆天使都該拜其。」\[S. 97. 7.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Yiu-teh* *fæn*, *Jing-ming* tsæ ta-læ. 有得翻，神明再帶來。（原註）</sup>

<sup>7</sup> Kông-tao keh-sing tʽin-s z-ka wô, “Gyi shih-lih Gyi-go tʽin-s *kʽo-pi* *ziang* fong, *Gyi* *shih-lih* Gyi-go tsʽa-s *kʽo-pi* *ziang* ho-kwông.” \[S. 104. 4.\] <sup>8</sup> Kông-tao Ng-ts *yiu* *ka* *wô*, “Jing-ming,” Ng-go zo-we z tao shü-shü-dæ-dæ; Ng *sô* *tsih-công* koh-kô-go gyün-ping z <sup>\[1\]</sup>tsing-dzih-go gyün-ping. <sup>9</sup> Ng hwun-hyi kong-yi, u-su fi-li; keh-lah Jing-ming, *ziu-z* Ng-go Jing-ming, pô hwun-lôh-go hyiang-yiu fu Ng ko-jü Ng-go *cong* de-ho.” \[S. 45. 6, 7.\] <sup>10</sup> Yi *wô*, “Ng, Cü, z kyʽi-tsʽu lih-ding di-go keng-kyi; keh-sing tʽin yia z Ng tsʽing-siu cü-zao-go. <sup>11</sup> Gyi-lah we mih-diao, tsih-yiu Ng üong-yün dzeng-tong: gyi-lah tu we ziang i-voh ka gyiu-kyʽi-læ; <sup>12</sup> Ng we tsih gyi long ziang bao-tʽao ka, gyi ziu we pin-yih: tsih-yiu Ng z üong feh keng-kæ, Ng-go ziu-shü m-neh dzing-gyi.” \[S. 102. 25–27.\]

<sup>7</sup> 講到箇星天使是介話，「其設立其個天使可比像風，其設立其個差使可比像火光。」\[S. 104. 4.\] <sup>8</sup> 講到兒子又介話，「神明，爾個座位是到世世代代；爾所執掌國家個權柄是正直<sup>\[1\]</sup>個權柄。<sup>9</sup> 爾歡喜公義，惡愫非禮；箇拉神明，就是爾個神明，把歡樂個香油傅爾過如爾個衆隊夥。」\[S. 45. 6, 7.\] <sup>10</sup> 又話，「爾，主，是起初立定地個根基；箇星天也是爾親手製造個。<sup>11</sup> 其拉會滅掉，只有爾永遠存東；其拉都會像衣服介舊起來；<sup>12</sup> 爾會摺其攏像袍套介，其就會變易。只有爾是永弗更改，爾個壽歲嘸得盡其。」\[S. 102. 25–27.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng* *wô*, dzih-go. 本文話，直個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>13</sup> *Jing-ming* ʽo-dzeng teng ʽah-li ih-go tʽin-s wô, “Hao zo læ Ngô jing-siu-pin, teng-tao Ngô pô Ng-go dziu-dih tso Ng-go dah-kyiah-teng?” \[S. 110. 1.\] <sup>14</sup> Cong *tʽin-s* kyʽi feh-z voh-z *Jing-ming* go ling, *coh-kwun* be tsʽa-kyʽi we-leh keh-sing tsiang-læ iao tsih-djôh kyiu-sing go nying hao tông tsʽa-s ma?

<sup>13</sup> 神明何曾等何裏一個天使話，「好坐來我順手邊，等到我把爾個仇敵做爾個踏腳凳？」\[S. 110. 1.\] <sup>14</sup> 衆天使豈弗是服侍神明個靈，只管被差去爲了箇星將來要接着救星個人好當差使嗎？

### H. II.

Sô-yi ah-lah kæ yü-kô liu-sing sô tʽing-meng-go z-ken, kʽong-pʽô ʽôh-tsia <sup>\[1\]</sup>shih-lôh *gyi*. <sup>2</sup> Ing-we *Jing-ming* tʽôh-leh tʽin-s kông-go shih-wô kyi-jün z ih-ding-peh-yih-go, væn-pah we-nyih feh i-jing z-ken tu iao teh-djôh tsing-dzih-go pao-ing; <sup>3</sup> *hwông-tsʽia* ka do-go kyiu-sing, ah-lah ziah feh kyʽi tsʽæ gyi, dza neng-keo bi-leh-ko ni? Keh-go *kyiu-sing* z tʽôh-leh Cü kông kyʽi-deo go, keh-sing tʽing-meng cü-kwu kyʽüôh-kyʽüôh-jih-jih wô hyiang ah-lah dao; <sup>4</sup> Jing-ming tsiao Gyi-zi-go ts-i yüong gyi-z, ziao-deo, yiu-ho ʽao-su jing-tsih, teng <sup>\[2\]</sup>Sing-Ling-go eng-s, dô-kô tso te-tsing.

所以阿拉該愈加留心所聽聞個事幹，恐怕或者失落其<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>2</sup> 因爲神明託勒天使講個說話既然是一定不易個，凡百違逆弗依順事幹都要得着正直個報應；<sup>3</sup> 況且介大個救星，阿拉若弗去睬其，咋能夠避勒過呢？箇個救星是託了主講起頭個，箇星聽聞主顧確確實實話向阿拉道；<sup>4</sup> 神明照其自個旨意用奇事，兆頭，有夥號數神蹟，等聖靈個恩賜<sup>\[2\]</sup>，大家做對證。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, feh kyʽi tsʽæ *gyi*; *ʽôh*, *teng* *gyi* tsʽô-kʽæ. 或話，弗去睬其；或，等其岔開。（原註）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *Peng-veng*, *ʽôh* *kông*, pô Sing-Ling feng peh *nying*; *ʽôh*, Sing-Ling sô feng peh *nying* go. 本文，或講，把聖靈分撥人；或聖靈所分撥人個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>5</sup> AH-LAH sô kông keh tsiang-læ-go z-shü, *Jing-ming* bing feh-z peh gyi kwe-voh keh-sing tʽin-s: <sup>6</sup> dæn-z yiu ih-go nying læ ih-pʽin shü-li tso te-tsing, z-ka wô, “Nying z soh-si, Ng we kyi-nyiæn gyi? nying-go ng-ts *z* *soh-si*, Ng we tsiao-kwu gyi? <sup>7</sup> Ng z peh gyi pi tʽin-s <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzæn-z siao-tin, *yi* we peh gyi ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun, peh gyi công-kwun Ng siu sô zao-go tong-si: <sup>8</sup> Ng we s-teh væn-veh tu kwe-voh gyi-go kyiah-ʽô.” \[S. 8. 4–6.\] Kyi-jün “s-teh væn-veh kwe-voh gyi,” tön m-yiu ih-yiang feh kwe-voh gyi: dæn-z tao jü-kying wa feh-zing kʽen-kyin væn-veh kwe-voh gyi. <sup>9</sup> <sup>\[2\]</sup>Tsih-yiu keh-we “pi tʽin-s dzæn-z siao-tin-go” Yiæ-su, ah-lah kʽen-kyin Gyi we-leh si-go kwʽu-deo “ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun,” s-teh Gyi kʽao-djôh Jing-ming-go eng-we hao tʽi cong-nying kyʽüoh si-go *kwʽu-deo*.

<sup>5</sup> 阿拉所講箇將來個時世，神明並弗是撥其歸服箇星天使。<sup>6</sup> 但是有一個人來一片書裏做對證，是介話，「人是啥西，爾會記念其？人個兒子是啥西，爾會照顧其？<sup>7</sup> 爾是撥其比天使暫時小點<sup>\[1\]</sup>，又會撥其戴上榮華體面個平天冠，撥其掌管爾手所造個東西。<sup>8</sup> 爾會使得萬物都歸服其個腳下。」\[S. 8. 4–6.\] 既然「使得萬物歸服其」，斷嘸有一樣弗歸服其。但是到如今還弗曾看見萬物歸服其。<sup>9</sup> 只有箇位「比天使暫時小點個」<u>耶穌</u><sup>\[2\]</sup>，阿拉看見其爲了死個苦頭「戴上榮華體面個平天冠」，使得其靠着神明個恩惠好替衆人喫死個苦頭。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, shü-vi siao-tin. 或話，勢微小點。（原註）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, Tsih kʽen-kyin Yiæ-su ta-zông yüong-wô tʽi-min-go bing-tʽin-kwun: Gyi we-leh si-go kwʽu-deo dzæn-z pi tʽin-s siao-tin-go. 或話，只看見<u>耶穌</u>帶上榮華體面個平天冠。其爲了死個苦頭暫時比天使小點個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>10</sup> Væn-veh tu z we-leh Gyi, kʽao-djôh Gyi go, keh-we *Jing-ming*, iao ta-ling hyü-to ng-ts teh-djôh yüong-wô, ziu s-teh gyi-lah teh-kyiu-go Kyüing-wông we-leh kwʽu-næn neng-keo djün-be, z ing-kæ-go. <sup>11</sup> Keh-we tseng-kyʽi *gyi-lah* go, teng keh-sing be tseng-kyʽi-go, tu z cʽih-ü Ih-we: sô-yi tsʽing-hwu gyi-lah z hyüong-di yia feh we wông-kʽong, z-ka wô, <sup>12</sup> “Ngô we pô Ng-go ming-deo tʽong-pao Ngô-go hyüong-di; læ kyiao-we cong-nyiang Ngô we tsʽông s tsæn-me Ng.” \[S. 22. 22.\] <sup>13</sup> Yi *wô*, “Ngô we siang-sing kʽao-djôh Gyi.” \[2S. 22. 3. S-pʽin 18. 2.\] Wa-yiu *wô*, “Ngô teng Jing-ming sô s-peh Ngô go siao-nying tu læ-tong.” \[Y. 8. 18.\]

<sup>10</sup> 萬物都是爲了其，靠着其個，箇位神明，要帶領許多兒子得着榮華，就使得其拉得救個君王爲了苦難能夠全備，是應該個。<sup>11</sup> 箇位尊起其拉個，等箇星被尊起個，都是出於一位。所以稱呼其拉是兄弟也弗會惶恐，是介話，<sup>12</sup> 「我會把爾個名頭通報我個兄弟；來教會中央我會唱詩讚美爾。」\[S. 22. 22.\]<sup>13</sup> 又話，「我會相信靠着其。」還有話，「我等神明所賜撥我個小人都來東。」\[Y. 8. 18.\]

<sup>14</sup> Keh-sing siao-nying kyi-jün joh-ü nyüoh-sing teng hyüih-kyʽi, Gyi-zi yia z-ka dzing we nyüoh-sing teng hyüih-kyʽi; iao we-leh *jih-z-kô-go* si hao ba-diao công-kwun si go cü-kwu, ziu-z mo-kwe; <sup>15</sup> yi hao sih-fông keh-sing, we-leh pʽô si, ih-seng tso nu-boh go.

<sup>14</sup> 箇星小人既然屬於肉身等血氣，其自也是介成爲肉身等血氣；要爲了十字架個死好敗掉掌管死個主顧，就是魔鬼；<sup>15</sup> 又好釋放箇星，爲了怕死，一生做奴僕個。

<sup>16</sup> Gyi bing feh-z *læ* vu-dzu tʽin-s, z *læ* vu-dzu Üô-pah-lah-hen-go ʽeo-dæ. <sup>17</sup> Keh-lah kông-tao Jing-ming z-ken iao tso ih-go dz-pe cong-sing-go Tsi-s-deo, hao w̆æn-we pah-sing-go ze, li-kʽæ teng *Gyi* hyüong-di yiang-yiang siang-dong. <sup>18</sup> Gyi-zi kyi-jün ziu-ko s-lin-go kwʽu-næn, sô-yi neng-keo pông-dzu keh-sing be s-lin-go cü-kwu.

<sup>16</sup> 其並弗是來扶助天使，是來扶助<u>亞伯拉罕</u>個後代。<sup>17</sup> 箇拉講到神明事幹要做一個慈悲忠信個祭司頭，好挽回百姓個罪，離開等其兄弟樣樣相同。<sup>18</sup> 其自既然受過試煉個苦難，所以能夠幫助箇星被試煉個主顧。

### H. III.

KA-NI, be tʽin-zông sô dziao-go sing-jün hyüong-di, yüong ts-si s-tsʽeng ah-lah sô tsiao-jing *dao-li* go Tsʽa-s teng Tsi-s-deo, keh-we Kyi-toh Yiæ-su: <sup>2</sup> Gyi yiu cong-sing *voh-z* sô shih-lih Gyi go *Cü*, tsing-ziang Mo-si “læ Jing-ming-go ʽeh-kô li-hyiang *yiu* *cong-sing*” ka. \[Ms. 12. 7.\] <sup>3</sup> Hwông-tsʽia keh-we *Yiæ-su* sön z kʽo-yi teh-djôh yüong-wô ko-jü Mo-si, yiu-ts-wu dzing-kô-go cü-kwu pi *gyi* ih-kô-go *nying* z keng-kô kwe-djong. <sup>4</sup> Væn-pah nying-kô tsong yiu ih-go dzing-kô-go cü: ts-ü dzing-dziu væn-veh go, z Jing-ming. <sup>5</sup> Næn-kæn Mo-si tso “nu-boh,” hao te-tsing keh-sing tsiang-læ iao kông go shih-wô, “læ Jing-ming-go ʽeh-kô yiu cong-sing;” <sup>6</sup> dæn-z Kyi-toh tso Ng-ts djü Gyi-zi-go ih-kô *yiu* *cong-sing*: ah-lah ziah neng-keo fông-tæn *siang-sing*, kao-hying siang-vông, ih-dzih tao ti kyin-kwu siu-djü, ziu-z Gyi ih-kô-go *nying*.

介呢，被天上所召個聖善兄弟，用仔細思忖阿拉所招認道理個差使等祭司頭，箇位<u>基督</u> <u>耶穌</u>。<sup>2</sup> 其有忠信服侍所設立其個主，正像<u>摩西</u>「來神明個合家裏向有忠信」介。\[Ms. 12. 7.\] <sup>3</sup> 況且箇位<u>耶穌</u>算是可以得着榮華過如<u>摩西</u>，猶之乎成家個主顧比其一家個人是更加貴重。<sup>4</sup> 凡百人家總有一個成家個主。至於成就萬物個，是神明。<sup>5</sup> 難間<u>摩西</u>做「奴僕」，好對證箇星將來要講個說話，「來神明個合家有忠信。」<sup>6</sup> 但是<u>基督</u>做兒子治其自個一家有忠信。阿拉若能夠放膽相信，高興想望，一直到底堅固守住，就是其一家個人。

<sup>7</sup> Keh-lah, ziang Sing-Ling sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, <sup>8</sup> feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing, ziang *zin-deo* kyih *Ngô* ô-wông, læ kwʽông-iæ di-fông s-s *Ngô* z-ʽeo ka: <sup>9</sup> ziu-z ng-lah tsu-tsong s-s Ngô, tʽen-tʽen Ngô, s-jih nyin kong-fu kʽen-kyin Ngô sô tso-go z-ken. <sup>10</sup> Sô-yi Ngô kʽo-u keh-dæ-go nying, ziu wô, Gyi-lah ih-hyiang yiu zia-pʽih-go sing; gyi yia feh sih-teh Ngô-go lu-deo. <sup>11</sup> Ngô ziu dong-nu vah-tsiu, *wô*, Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô en-sih li-hyiang.” \[S. 95. 8–11.\] <sup>12</sup> Hyüong-di, yüong kwu-djôh, kʽong-pʽô ng-lah cong-nyiang yiu nying dzeng feh siang-sing hyüong-ôh-go sing, ziu li-kʽæ keh-we weh-go Jing-ming: <sup>13</sup> “kyih-mih nyih-ts wa læ-tong, kæ nyih-tang-nyih dô-kô siang-kyʽün; sæn-leh ng-lah li-hyiang yiu nying, we-leh ze-nyih-go hong-pʽin, pin tso ngang *sing-dziang*. <sup>14</sup> Ing-we ah-lah ziah neng-keo fông-tæn *siang-sing*, s-cong-jü-ih kyin-kwu siu-djü, ziu teng Kyi-toh dô-kô yiu-veng.

<sup>7</sup> 箇拉，像聖靈所話，「今末若聽其個說話，<sup>8</sup> 弗可硬自個心，像前頭激我𢛟惶，來曠野地方試試我時候介。<sup>9</sup> 就是爾拉祖宗試試我，探探我，四十年工夫看見我所做個事幹。<sup>10</sup> 所以我可惡箇代個人，就話，其拉一向有邪僻個心；其也弗識得我個路頭。<sup>11</sup> 我就動怒罰咒，話，其拉總弗能夠走進我安息裏向。」\[S. 95. 8–11.\] <sup>12</sup> 兄弟，用顧着，恐怕爾拉中央有人存弗相信兇惡個心，就離開箇位活個神明。<sup>13</sup> 「今末日子還來東，該日打日大家相勸；省勒爾拉裏向有人，爲了罪孽個哄騙，變做硬心腸。<sup>14</sup> 因爲阿拉若能夠放膽相信，始終如一堅固守住，就等<u>基督</u>大家有份。

<sup>15</sup> *Zông-veng* sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing, ziang *zin-deo* kyih Ngô ô-wông ka.” \[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>16</sup> Ka-ni, tʽing-meng ts-ʽeo kyih *Gyi* ô-wông cü-kwu z jü ni? kyʽi feh-z keng-leh Mo-si tseo-cʽih Yiæ-gyih go cong-nying ma? <sup>17</sup> *Jing-ming* s-jih nyin kong-fu kʽo-u jü ni? kyʽi feh-z keh-sing væn-leh ze, gyi s-siu tʽæn læ kwʽông-iæ-li go ma? <sup>18</sup> *Jing-ming* vah-tsiu feh hyü tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go, z jü ni? Ziu-z keh-pæn feh i-jing-go nying. Kʽo kyin-teh, gyi-lah sô-yi feh neng-keo tseo-tsing z we-leh feh siang-sing-go yün-kwu.

<sup>15</sup> 上文所話，「今末若聽其個說話，弗可硬自個心，像前頭激我𢛟惶介。」\[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>16</sup> 介呢，聽聞之後激其𢛟惶主顧是誰呢？豈弗是跟勒<u>摩西</u>走出<u>埃及</u>個衆人嗎？<sup>17</sup> 神明四十年工夫可惡誰呢？豈弗是箇星犯了罪，其屍首攤來曠野裏個嗎？<sup>18</sup> 神明罰咒弗許走進其安息裏向個，是誰呢？就是箇班弗依順個人。可見得，其拉所以弗能夠走進是爲了弗相信個緣故。

### H. IV.

Ka-ni, tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go ing-hyü kyi-jün yi-lôh-tong, ah-lah kæ pʽô-gyü, vi-min ng-lah li-hyiang yiu nying sön z tsʽo-lôh-go. <sup>2</sup> Yiu hao sing-sih djün peh ah-lah, tsing-ziang peh gyi-lah ka: dæn-z sô <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-go *en-sih* shih-wô z vu-ih ü gyi-lah, ing-we <sup>\[2\]</sup>keh-sing tʽing-meng-go cü-kwu m-neh siang-sing-go sing dzeng-tih. <sup>3</sup> Keh-go en-sih li-hyiang *tsih-yiu* ah-lah siang-sing-go nying hao tseo-tsing-kyʽi; ziang *Jing-ming* sô wô, “Ngô ziu dong-nu vah-tsiu, *wô*, Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô en-sih li-hyiang:” \[S. 95. 11.\] jün-r tʽin-di zao-dzing ts-ʽeo *Jing-ming-go* z-ken yi-kying wun-kong-de. Kông-tao di-tsʽih *nyih* yiu ih-pʽin shü z-ka wô, “Jing-ming læ di-tsʽih nyih Gyi væn-pah z-ken tu wun-kong, ziu en-sih-de:” \[Ts. 2. 2.\] <sup>5</sup> wa-yiu zông-veng *wô*, “Gyi-lah tsong feh neng-keo tseo-tsing Ngô-go en-sih li-hyiang.”

介呢，走進其安息裏向個應許既然遺落東，阿拉該怕懼，未免爾拉裏向有人算是錯落個。<sup>2</sup> 有好信息傳撥阿拉，正像撥其拉介。但是所傳個<sup>\[1\]</sup>安息說話是無益於其拉，因爲箇星聽聞個主顧嘸得相信個心存的<sup>\[2\]</sup>。<sup>3</sup> 箇個安息裏向只有阿拉相信個人好走進去；像神明所話，「我就動怒罰咒，話，其拉總弗能夠走進我安息裏向。」\[S. 95. 11.\] 然而天地造成之後神明個事幹已經完工了。講到第七日有一片書是介話，「神明來第七日其凡百事幹都完工，就安息了。」\[Ts. 2. 2.\] <sup>5</sup> 還有上文話，「其拉總弗能夠走進我個安息裏向。」

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng*, tʽing-ming-go. 本文，聽聞個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kông-tao siang-sing-go sing, gyi-lah feh neng-keo teng keh-sing tʽing-meng cü-kwu dô-kô ʽeh-long. 或話，講到相信個心，其拉弗能夠等箇星聽聞主顧大家合攏。（原註）</sup>

<sup>6</sup> Ka-ni, keh-go en-sih li-hyiang tsong yiu nying wa hao tseo-tsing-kyʽi; keh-sing sin tʽing-meng keh hao sing-sih cü-kwu we-leh feh i-jing yi feh neng-keo-tseo-tsing: <sup>7</sup> keh-lah læ hyü-to nyin-su ts-ʽeo læ Da-bih-go *shü*-li yi ʽæn-ding ih-go nyih-ts, wô z “kyih-mih;” ziang *zông-veng* sô wô, “Kyih-mih ziah tʽing Gyi-go shih-wô, feh-kʽo ngang zi-go sing.” \[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>8</sup> Kyüô-jü <sup>\[1\]</sup>Iah-shü-üô yi-kying peh pah-sing en-sih, ʽeo-deo *Jing-ming* feh we di-kyʽi bih-go nyih-ts;—— <sup>9</sup> ka kʽen-kyʽi-læ, Jing-ming-go pah-sing tsong wa yiu ih-go <sup>\[2\]</sup>en-sih. <sup>10</sup> Ing-we tseo-tsing Gyi en-sih li-hyiang go cü-kwu, gyi z zi-go z-ken wun-kong ts-ʽeo ziu en-sih-go, ziang Jing-ming Zi-go z-ken zao-dzing ts-ʽeo *ziu* *en-sih* ih-yiang-go.

<sup>6</sup> 介呢，箇個安息裏向總有人還好走進去；箇星先聽聞箇好信息主顧爲了弗依順又弗能夠走進。<sup>7</sup> 箇拉來許多年數之後來<u>大闢</u>個書裏又限定一個日子，話是「今末；」像上文所話，「今末若聽其個說話，弗可硬自個心。」\[S. 95. 8.\] <sup>8</sup> 假如<u>約書亞</u><sup>\[1\]</sup>已經撥百姓安息，後頭神明弗會提起別個日子。<sup>9</sup> 介看起來，神明個百姓總還有一個安息。<sup>10</sup> 因爲走進其安息裏向個主顧，其是自個事幹完工之後就安息個<sup>\[2\]</sup>，像神明自個事幹造成之後就安息一樣個。

<sup>\[1\] Iah-shü-üô, YIÆ-SU, *keh* *liang* *ming-z* *peng-veng* *tsôh* *tso* *z-ngæn*. <u>約書亞</u>，<u>耶穌</u>，箇兩名字本文作做字眼。（原註）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *Peng-veng* *wô*, siu en-sih-*nyih* go z-ken. 本文話，守安息日個事幹。（原註）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah yüong zông-kying gying-lih tseo-tsing keh en-sih li-hyiang, kʽong-pʽô yiu nying kʽen keh-cong-ka <sup>\[1\]</sup>feh i-jing-go pông-yiang ziu iao tih-tao. <sup>12</sup> Jing-ming-go shih-wô z ling-weh, yiu neng-ken, kwʽa-jü ting fong-li-go pao-kyin, we cʽoh-tsing pʽo-kʽæ weh-ling teng ling-sing, gao-kwu teng kweh-si, yi we bin-cʽih sing-li-go s-nyiæn teng ts-hyiang. <sup>13</sup> Gyi sô zao-go, yia m-yiu ih-yiang læ Gyi min-zin neng-keo ing-mun: væn-veh læ Gyi ngæn-zin tu cʽih-lu-lu, ming-ming pa-tong; Gyi ziu-z kyih-sön ah-lah tsiang go *Cü*.

<sup>11</sup> 所以阿拉用上緊勤力走進箇安息裏向，恐怕有人看箇種介弗依順個<sup>\[1\]</sup>榜樣就要跌倒。<sup>12</sup> 神明個說話是靈活，有能幹，快如頂鋒利個寶劍，會戳進破開活靈等靈性，絞股等骨髓，又會辨出心裏個思念等志向。<sup>13</sup> 其所造個，也嘸有一樣來其面前能夠隱瞞。萬物來其眼前都出露露，明明擺東。其就是結算阿拉賬個主。

<sup>\[1\] Feh i-jing-go; *yiu-teh* *fæn*, feh siang-sing-go. 弗依順個；有得翻，弗相信個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>14</sup> KA kʽen-kyʽi-læ, ah-lah kyi-jün yiu ih-go do Tsi-s-deo, yi-kying kying-ko keh-sing tʽin, ziu-z Jing-ming-go Ng-ts Yiæ-su, kæ kyin-kwu siu-djü *ah-lah* sô tsiao-jing-go dao-li. <sup>15</sup> Hwông-tsʽia ah-lah sô yiu-go Tsi-s-deo tsæ-ve feh neng-keo tʽi-liang æ-lin ah-lah-go no-ziah; Gyi yiang-yiang z-ken be s-lin, ziang ah-lah ka, *tsih-z* m-neh tsao-djôh ze-kyʽin. <sup>16</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah yüong fông-tæn tseo-long *ʽong*-eng zo-we min-zin, hao teh-djôh æ-lin, tsin-djôh eng-we ze-z pông-dzu *ah-lah*.

<sup>14</sup> 介看起來，阿拉既然有一個大祭司頭，已經經過箇星天，就是神明個兒子<u>耶穌</u>，該堅固守住阿拉所招認個道理。<sup>15</sup> 況且阿拉所有個祭司頭再𣍐弗能夠體諒哀憐阿拉個懦弱；其樣樣事幹被試煉，像阿拉介，只是嘸得遭着罪愆。<sup>16</sup> 所以阿拉用放膽走攏鴻恩座位面前，好得着哀憐，佔着恩惠隨時幫助阿拉。

### H. V.

Væn-pah tsi-s-deo tu z dzong nying-go cong-nyiang kæn-shün-cʽih-læ, yi z we-leh tʽi nying *voh-z* Jing-ming sô shih-lih-go, hao tʽi ze-ôh hyin-zông li-veh teng tsi-z; <sup>2</sup> gyi neng-keo nyün-liang keh-sing feh ming-bah tseo tsʽô-lu go nying, ing-we gyi-zi yia ʽo-jün z no-ziah-go. <sup>3</sup> Sô-yi we-leh zi, tsing-ziang we-leh pah-sing, tông-kæ hyin joh-ze-go tsi-veh.

凡百祭司頭都是從人個中央揀選出來，又是爲了替人服侍神明所設立個，好替罪惡獻上禮物等祭祀；<sup>2</sup> 其能夠原諒箇星弗明白走岔路個人，因爲其自也和然是懦弱個。<sup>3</sup> 所以爲了自，正像爲了百姓，當該獻贖罪個祭物。

<sup>4</sup> Wa-yiu keh kwe-djong-go tsih-veng m-nying ken tông, tsih-yiu mong Jing-ming sô dziao-læ cü-kwu, ziang Üô-leng ka. <sup>5</sup> Z-ka Kyi-toh yia m̆-teh Zi tʽe-tseng tso tsi-s-deo; z *Jing-ming* *sô* *tʽe-tseng-go*, teng Gyi wô, “Ng z Ngô-go Ng-ts, Ngô kyih-mih sang Ng:” \[S. 2. 7.\] <sup>6</sup> wa-yiu bih *u-sen* z-ka wô, “Ng üong-yün *tso* Tsi-s, tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.” \[S.110. 4.\]

<sup>4</sup> 還有箇貴重個職份嘸人敢當，只有蒙神明所召來主顧，像<u>亞倫</u>介。<sup>5</sup> 是介<u>基督</u>也嘸得自推尊做祭司頭；是神明所推尊個，等其話，「爾是我個兒子，我今末生爾。」\[S. 2. 7.\] <sup>6</sup> 還有別烏碎是介話，「爾永遠做祭司，照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次。」 \[S.110. 4.\]

<sup>7</sup> Gyi dzing we nyüoh-sing go z-ʽeo, tsʽæn-nyiæn gyiu-kʽeng, ngæn-li beh-cʽih do-do sing-hyiang eo keh-we neng-keo kyiu Gyi min-diao si go Cü-kwu; <sup>\[1\]</sup>we-leh Gyi tseng-kying pʽô-gyü-go sing, *Jing-ming* ziu tʽing Gyi: <sup>8</sup> se-tsih? z-go Ng-ts, Gyi neng-keo i-jing, yia z dzong kwʽu-næn li-hyiang jih-lin-ko-læ-go. <sup>9</sup> Tao-leh djün-be, *ziu* tso væn-pah i-jing Gyi cü-kwu üong-yün teh-kyiu-go Cü: <sup>10</sup> z Jing-ming sô tsʽing-hwu z Tsi-s-deo “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.”

<sup>7</sup> 其成爲肉身個時候，懺唸求懇，眼淚浡出大大聲響謳箇位能夠救其免掉死個主顧；爲了其尊敬怕懼個心，神明就聽其<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>8</sup> 雖即是個兒子，其能夠依順，也是從苦難裏向習練過來個。<sup>9</sup> 到了全備，就做凡百依順其主顧永遠得救個主。<sup>10</sup> 是神明所稱呼是祭司頭「照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次。」

Mk. 14. 33. Iʽe. 12. 27. 或話，或話，神明就聽其，使得其脫出怕懼個心。或，神明就聽其，救其免掉其所怕懼個事幹。看 Mt. 26. 37. Mk. 14. 33. Iʽe. 12. 27.（原註）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> KÔNG-TAO Gyi, ah-lah yiu hyü-to næn ka-shih go shih-wô, ing-we ng-lah tʽing-ts z mông-vu-deo-jü-de. <sup>13</sup> Ziah leng z-ʽeo-go *dziang-kyiu*, ng-lah *nyün* kæ tso sin-sang; næn-kæn wa kyʽiah-leh pô Jing-ming tsiao-shü-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li tsæ kao ng; ng-lah yia kyʽiah-leh *kyʽüoh* na, væn-zih z kyʽüoh-feh-læ-de. <sup>13</sup> Væn-pah kyʽüoh-na-go cü-kwu feh tʽong-dah kong-yi-go dao-li; gyi z-go siao-nying.” <sup>14</sup> Tsih-yiu <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzing-nying-tsiang-da-go hao *kyʽüoh* væn-zih; gyi kyin-sih we-leh <sup>\[2\]</sup>jih-lin neng-keo-bin-pih hao-tæ.

<sup>11</sup> 講到其，阿拉有許多難解說個說話，因爲爾拉聽仔是茫無頭緒了。<sup>12</sup> 若論時候個長久，爾拉原該做先生；難間還卻勒把神明詔書個初學道理再教爾；爾拉也卻勒喫奶，飯食是喫弗來了。<sup>13</sup> 凡百喫奶個主顧弗通達公義個道理；其是個小人。<sup>14</sup> 只有成人長大個<sup>\[1\]</sup>好喫飯食；其見識爲了習練<sup>\[2\]</sup>能夠辨別好歹。

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, jih-kwæn. 或話，習慣。（原註）</sup>

### HI. VI.

Sô-yi <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzong Kyi-toh-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li ah-lah yüong *dzin-dzin* tsing-ih tao djün-be-go di-bu: hao-vong tsæ tang ti-ts, *ziu-z* hwe-kæ *gyi* si-go ʽang-we, siang-sing Jing-ming, <sup>2</sup> *ling-ziu* keh-sing si-li, en-siu-go *li-tsih*, si-nying tsæ weh, üong-yün pʽun-tön, keh-sing dao-li. <sup>3</sup> Ziah-z Jing-ming kʽeng ing-hyü, ah-lah pih-ding z-ka tso.

所以從<u>基督</u>個初學道理阿拉用漸漸進益到全備個地步<sup>\[1\]</sup>。好甮再打底子，就是悔改其死個行爲，相信神明，<sup>2</sup> 領受箇星洗禮，按手個禮節，死人再活，永遠判斷，箇星道理。<sup>3</sup> 若是神明肯應許，阿拉必定是介做。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, ah-lah hao-vong kông Kyi-toh-go tsʽu-ʽôh dao-li; hao kyʽi kông keh djün-be-go dao-li. 或話，阿拉好甮講<u>基督</u>個初學道理；好去講箇全備個道理。（原註）</sup>

<sup>4</sup> Tʽông-jün yiu nying ih-tsao teh-djôh kwông-ming, zông-djôh keh tʽin-zông eng-s-go mi-dao, tsih-ziu Sing-Ling-go *ken-dong*, <sup>5</sup> zông-djôh Jing-ming-go hao-hao dao-li teng tsiang-læ z-shü-go neng-ken, <sup>6</sup> *ʽeo-deo* we shih-diao *keh-go* *dao-li*, tön feh neng-keo tsæ we-sing-cün-i ih-li tso-nying; *keh* *z* gyi-zi djong-sin ting-sah Jing-ming-go Ng-ts, ming-ming tsao-tʽah *Gyi*. <sup>7</sup> Pʽi-jü din-di peh-djông yiu yü ts-jing gyi; ziah fah-cʽih tʽu-tsʽæn, ʽeh-djôh <sup>\[1\]</sup>nong-fu-go yüong-dziang, z teh-djôh Jing-ming-go tô-hwô: <sup>8</sup> ziah sang-cʽih tsʽ-bang yia-tsʽao, *ziu* *iao* be kyʽi-diao, gying-wu tsiu-mô; gyi-go kwe-keng z iao me-diao.

<sup>4</sup> 倘然有人一遭得着光明，嚐着箇天上恩賜個味道，接受聖靈個感動，<sup>5</sup> 嚐着神明個好好道理等將來時世個能幹，<sup>6</sup> 後頭會失掉箇個道理，斷弗能夠再回心轉意一禮做人；箇是其自重先釘煞神明個兒子，明明糟蹋其。<sup>7</sup> 譬如田地不常有雨滋潤其；若發出土產，合着農夫個<sup>\[1\]</sup>用場，是得着神明個朵化。<sup>8</sup> 若生出刺棚野草，就要被棄掉，近乎咒罵；其個歸根是要𤈦掉。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kang-cong cü-nying-kô-go. 或話，耕種主人家個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>9</sup> Tsʽing-æ-go *hyüong-di*, ah-lah se-tsih ka kông, yia jih-we siang-sing ng-lah z kôh-lu-go, tsong hao teh-djôh kyiu. <sup>10</sup> Jing-ming tsæ-ve feh kong-dao, mông-kyi ng-lah sô tso-go z-ken, teng ng jing-æ-go lao-loh; ziu-z ng-lah we-leh Gyi-go ming-deo sô hyin-cʽih-go *jing-æ*, su-djông we kong-ing sing-du, *yin-dzæ* dzing-gyiu z-ka tso. <sup>11</sup> Ah-lah pô-feh-neng-keo ng-lah kôh-nying tu we hyin-cʽih tso-yiang nyih-sing, ih-dzih tao ti toh-jih siang-vông: <sup>12</sup> feh-kʽo yiæ-dæ, *iao* kʽen keh-sing we-leh siang-sing jing-næ we tsih-djôh sô ing-hyü cü-kwu-go yiang.

<sup>9</sup> 親愛個兄弟，阿拉雖即介講，也實惠相信爾拉是各路個，總好得着救。<sup>10</sup> 神明再𣍐弗公道，忘記爾拉所做個事幹，等爾仁愛個勞碌；就是爾拉爲了其個名頭所顯出個仁愛，素常會供應聖徒，現在仍舊是介做。<sup>11</sup> 阿拉巴弗能夠爾拉各人都會顯出做樣熱心，一直到抵篤實想望。<sup>12</sup> 弗可懈怠，要看箇星爲了相信忍耐會接着所應許主顧個樣。

<sup>13</sup> Jing-ming ing-hyü Üô-pah-lah-hen z-ʽeo, ing-we m-yiu keng-kô do-go hao ts-tin gyi vah-tsiu, ziu ts-tin Zi vah-tsiu, wô, <sup>14</sup> “Ngô tsong we do-nyiang coh-foh ng, do-nyiang cʽông-zing ng-go *ts-seng*:” \[Ts. 22. 16, 17.\] <sup>15</sup> z-ka *Üô-pah-lah-hen* ziu næ-sing teng-dæ, teh-djôh sô ing-hyü-go.

<sup>13</sup> 神明應許<u>亞伯拉罕</u>時候，因爲嘸有更加大個好指點其罰咒，就指點自罰咒，話，<sup>14</sup> 「我總會大樣祝福爾，大樣昌盛爾個子孫。」<sup>15</sup> 是介<u>亞伯拉罕</u>就耐心等待，得着所應許個。

<sup>16</sup> *Væn-pah* nying vah-tsiu, tsong z ts-tin do-jü *gyi-zi* go: vah-tsiu z kʽao-jih gyi *shih-wô*, ʽeo-deo m-kao hao cün-pin. <sup>17</sup> Næn-kæn Jing-ming pô-feh-neng-keo keh-sing tsih-djôh *Gyi* ing-hyü cü-kwu hao ming-ming hyiao-teh Gyi-go ts-i z keng-kæ-feh-læ, *sô-yi* <sup>\[1\]</sup>yüong vah-tsiu shih-wô tso bing-kyü: <sup>18</sup> z-ka, yiu liang-yiang keng-kæ-feh-læ-go z-ken, Jing-ming sô feh neng-keo zih-yin go; s-teh ah-lah dao-næn kyʽi tsih-ziu sô ts-tin ah-lah go siang-vông cü-kwu tu hao en-en-sing-sing. <sup>19</sup> Keh-go *siang-vông* kʽo-pi ling-weng-go mao, dziah-jih lao-kʽao, pʽao læ mun-tsiang li-hyiang: <sup>20</sup> keh-go di-fông Yiæ-su tso sin-fong tʽi ah-lah *sin* tseo-tsing-kyʽi, “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ” üong-yün tso Tsi-s-deo.

<sup>16</sup> 凡百人罰咒，總是指點大如其自個。罰咒是靠實其說話，後頭嘸告好轉變。<sup>17</sup> 難間神明巴弗能夠箇星接着其應許主顧好明明曉得其個旨意是更改弗來，所以用罰咒說話做憑據<sup>\[1\]</sup>。<sup>18</sup> 是介，有兩樣更改弗來個事幹，神明所弗能夠食言個；使得阿拉逃難去接受所指點阿拉個想望主顧都好安安心心。<sup>19</sup> 箇個想望可比靈魂個錨，着實牢靠，拋來幔帳裏向。<sup>20</sup> 箇個地方<u>耶穌</u>做先鋒替阿拉先走進去，「照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次」永遠做祭司頭。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, Zi tso cong-pao vah-tsiu. 或話，自做中保罰咒。（原註）</sup>

### H. VII.

KEH Mah-kyi-si-teh, Sah-leng-go wông, ting kao Jing-ming-go tsi-s, zin-deo pʽong-djôh Üô-pah-lah-hen sah-ba-leh keh-sing koh-wông cün-læ, ziu coh-foh gyi; <sup>2</sup> Üô-pah-lah-hen pô ih-tsʽih-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih feng peh gyi; *Mah-kyi-si-teh-go* *ming-z*, di-ih, z ‘kong-yi wông’ ka ka-shih, wa-yiu *kyiao-leh* Sah-leng wông, ziu-z, ‘bing-en-go wông’; <sup>3</sup> gyi m̆-teh ah-tia, m̆-teh ah-nyiang, m̆-teh kô-pu, yia m̆-teh sang-nyih, yia m̆-teh si-gyi, z ziang Jing-ming Ng-ts ka;—— keh-go nying dziang-tʽong tso tsi-s.

箇<u>麥基洗德</u>，<u>撒冷</u>個王，頂高神明個祭司，前頭碰着<u>亞伯拉罕</u>殺敗了箇星國王轉來，就祝福其；<sup>2</sup> <u>亞伯拉罕</u>把一切個東西十股得一分撥其。<u>麥基洗德</u>個名字，第一，是「公義王」介解說，還有叫勒<u>撒冷</u>王，就是，「平安個王」；<sup>3</sup> 其嘸得阿爹，嘸得阿孃，嘸得家譜，也嘸得生日，也嘸得死期，是像神明兒子介；——箇個人長通做祭司。

<sup>4</sup> Ng-lah tsʽeng-tsʽeng-kʽen, tsu-tsong-deo Üô-pah-lah-hen we pô lo-liah-læ-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih peh gyi, keh-go *nying* z dza-nyiang tseng-kwe. <sup>5</sup> Næn-kæn Li-vi ts-seng tsih-ziu tsi-s tsih-veng go cü-kwu, keh-sing pah-sing se-tsih z gyi hyüong-di, cʽih-ü Üô-pah-lah-hen-go kweh-joh, jün-r gyi vong-ts tsiao lih-fah hao do pah-sing-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih: <sup>6</sup> dæn-z Mah-kyi-si-teh, z teng gyi-lah kôh <sup>\[1\]</sup>ts-pʽa go, gyi we do Üô-pah-lah-hen-go tong-si jih-kwu-teh-ih, yi coh-foh keh yiu ing-hyü go cü-kwu. <sup>7</sup> Keh ʽô-pe teh-djôh zông-pe-go coh-foh, z m-pæn-poh-go dao-li. <sup>8</sup> Wa-yiu, *dziu* dông-pin *kʽen-læ*, iao si go nying z do-kyü jih-kwu-teh-ih go; *dziu* keh-pin *kʽen-læ*, *keh* *do-kyü-go* *cü-kwu* yiu te-tsing gyi z weh-go. <sup>9</sup> Ping-tsʽia Li-vi, *se-tsih* z do-kyü jih-kwu-teh-ih go cü-kwu, yia hao wô gyi dzong Üô-pah-lah-hen-go siu-li yi-kying yiu jih-kwu-teh-ih do-cʽih-de: <sup>10</sup> ing-we Mah-kyi-si-teh nying-tsih Üô-pah-lah-hen z-ʽeo, Li-vi wa læ gyi tsu-tsong-go sing-zông.

<sup>4</sup> 爾拉忖忖看，祖宗頭<u>亞伯拉罕</u>會把擄掠來個東西十股得一撥其，箇個人是咋樣尊貴。<sup>5</sup> 難間<u>利未</u>子孫接受祭司職份個主顧，箇星百姓雖即是其兄弟，出於<u>亞伯拉罕</u>個骨肉，然而其奉旨照律法好馱百姓個東西十股得一。<sup>6</sup> 但是<u>麥基洗德</u>，是等其拉各支派<sup>\[1\]</sup>個，其會馱<u>亞伯拉罕</u>個東西十股得一，又祝福箇有應許個主顧。<sup>7</sup> 箇下輩得着上輩個祝福，是嘸扳駁個道理。<sup>8</sup> 還有，就蕩邊看來，要死個人是馱歸十股得一個；就箇邊看來，箇馱歸個主顧有對證其是活個。<sup>9</sup> 並且<u>利未</u>，雖即是馱歸十股得一個主顧，也好話其從<u>亞伯拉罕</u>個手裏已經有十股得一馱出了。<sup>10</sup> 因爲<u>麥基洗德</u>迎接<u>亞伯拉罕</u>時候，<u>利未</u>還來其祖宗個身上。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kô-pu. 或話，家譜。（原註）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Li-vi tsi-s-go tsih-veng ziah neng-keo s-teh *nying* djün-be, (pah-sing z yi-kying læ gyi joh-ʽô teh-djôh lih-fah,) ka-ni ʽo-yüong bih-go tsi-s “tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ” ka cʽih-læ, feh eo gyi tsiao. Üô-leng-go pæn-tsʽ ni? <sup>12</sup> Tsi-s-go tsih-veng kyi-jün keng-kæ, lih-fah z-jün yia iao keng-kæ. <sup>13</sup> Keh-go shih-wô sô ts-tin-go z joh-ü bih-go ts-pʽa, bing m-yiu nying z-dzih tsi-dæn ko: <sup>14</sup> ah-lah-go Cü z ming-ming cʽih-ü Yiu-da; keh-go ts-pʽa Mo-si bing m-yiu kông-djôh tsi-s tsih-veng ko.

<sup>11</sup> <u>利未</u>祭司個職份若能夠使得人全備，（百姓是已經來其屬下得着律法，）介呢何用別個祭司「照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次」介出來，弗謳其照<u>亞倫</u>個班次呢？<sup>12</sup> 祭司個職份既然更改，律法自然也要更改。<sup>13</sup> 箇個說話所指點個是屬於別個支派，並嘸有人侍值祭壇過。<sup>14</sup> 阿拉個主是明明出於<u>猶大</u>；箇個支派<u>摩西</u>並嘸有講着祭司職份過。

<sup>15</sup> Keh-go z-ken z keng-kô hyin-jün; ing-we yiu bih-go Tsi-s ziang Mah-kyi-si-teh ka iao cʽih-læ, <sup>16</sup> Gyi feh-z tsiao nyüoh-sing ʽao-ling-go lih-fah, z tsiao m-gyüong-dzing weh-ming-go neng-ken sô shih-lih-go: <sup>17</sup> ing-we *Jing-ming* te-tsing *Gyi*, wô, “Ng üong-yün tso Tsi-s tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ.” \[S. 110. 4.\] <sup>18</sup> Keh zin-deo-go ʽao-ling, we-leh gyi no-ziah vu-yüong, z fi-diao-de; <sup>19</sup> (lih-fah ih-ngæn feh neng-keo s-teh nying djün-be;) <sup>\[1\]</sup>næn-kæn tsæ shih-cʽih keng-kô hao-go siang-vông, s-teh ah-lah kʽo-yi tsʽing-gying Jing-ming.

<sup>15</sup> 箇個事幹是更加顯然；因爲有別個祭司像<u>麥基洗德</u>介要出來，<sup>16</sup> 其弗是照肉身號令個律法，是照嘸窮盡活命個能幹所設立個。<sup>17</sup> 因爲神明對證其，話，「爾永遠做祭司照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次。」<sup>18</sup> 箇前頭個號令，爲了其懦弱無用，是廢掉了。<sup>19</sup> （律法一眼弗能夠使得人全備；）難間再說出更加好個想望，使得阿拉可以親近神明<sup>\[1\]</sup>。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, *lih-fah* peh-ko s-teh keh keng-kô hao-go siang-vông læ; *kʽen* Ky. 3. 24: *ʽôh* *wô*, tsih-yiu sô shih-lih keh keng-kô hao-go siang-vông *we* *s-teh* *nying* *djün-be*. 或話，律法不過使得箇更加好個想望來；看 Ky. 3. 24。或話，只有所設立箇更加好個想望會使得人全備。（原註）</sup>

<sup>20</sup> Tsæ-wô, *shih-lih* *Yiæ-su* feh-z m̆-teh vah-tsiu;—— <sup>21</sup> shih-lih gyi-lah tso tsi-s z m̆-teh vah-tsiu; tsih-yiu *Jing-ming* *shih-lih* Yiæ-su, vah-tsiu teng Gyi wô, “CÜ yi-kying vah-tsiu, tsæ-ve fæn-hwe; Ng üong-yün tso Tsi-s tsiao Mah-kyi-si-teh-go pæn-tsʽ:”—— <sup>22</sup> z-ka Yiæ-su *yia* z keng hao iah-go Pao-nying.

<sup>20</sup> 再話，設立<u>耶穌</u>弗是嘸得罰咒；——<sup>21</sup> 設立其拉做祭司是嘸得罰咒；只有神明設立<u>耶穌</u>，罰咒等其話，「主已經罰咒，再𣍐反悔；爾永遠做祭司照<u>麥基洗德</u>個班次。」——<sup>22</sup> 是介<u>耶穌</u>也是更好約個保人。

<sup>23</sup> Wa-yiu gyi-lah, we-leh iao si, feh neng-keo dziang læ-tong; sô-yi yiu hyü-to tsi-s: <sup>24</sup> tsih-yiu Yiæ-su, we-leh Gyi üong-yün læ-tong, z yiu <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-ziu-feh-læ-go tsi-s tsih-veng. <sup>25</sup> Sô-yi cong-ü neng-keo kyiu keh-sing kʽao-djôh Gyi tseo-long Jing-ming u-sen læ-go cü-kwu; ing-we Gyi z-djông weh-tong, hao tʽi gyi-lah dæ-gyiu.

<sup>23</sup> 還有其拉，爲了要死，弗能夠長來東；所以有許多祭司。<sup>24</sup> 只有<u>耶穌</u>，爲了其永遠來東，是有傳授弗來個<sup>\[1\]</sup>祭司職份。<sup>25</sup> 所以終於能夠救箇星靠着其走攏神明烏碎來個主顧；因爲其時常活東，好替其拉代求。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, feh we ko-kyʽi go. 或話，弗會過去個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>26</sup> Keh-cong-ka Tsi-s-deo kʽeo-kʽeo teng ah-lah siang-nyi-go; *Gyi* *z* sing-jün, lao-jih, m-yüô-tin, li-yün ze-nying, yi-kying kao-jü keh-sing tʽin go; <sup>27</sup> Gyi feh ziang *bih-go* tsi-s-deo, sin we-leh zi-go ze, ʽeo we-leh pah-sing-go *ze*, pih iao me-nyih hyin tsi-veh: Gyi z hyin-zông Zi-go *kyi-sing* tso joh-ze-go tsi-veh, tsih-siao ih-tsao *ziu* *keo-de*. <sup>28</sup> Keh lih-fah z shih-lih no-ziah-go nying tso tsi-s-deo: tsih-yiu lih-fah ts-ʽeo sô vah-tsiu shih-wô z *shih-lih* *keh-go* Ng-ts; Gyi yi-kying üong-yün <sup>\[1\]</sup>djün-be-de.

<sup>26</sup> 箇種介祭司頭扣扣等阿拉相宜個；其是聖善，老實，嘸瑕點，離遠罪人，已經高如箇星天個。<sup>27</sup> 其弗像別個祭司頭，先爲了自個罪，後爲了百姓個罪，必要每日獻祭物。其是獻上自個肌身做贖罪個祭物，只消一遭就夠了。<sup>28</sup> 箇律法是設立懦弱個人做祭司頭。只有律法之後所罰咒說話是設立箇個兒子；其已經永遠全備了<sup>\[1\]</sup>。

<sup>\[1\] *Yiu-teh* *fæn*, tseng-kyʽi-liao. 有得翻，尊起了。（原註）</sup>

### H. VIII.

NÆN-KÆN sô kông-go do-kwe-mo z ah-lah yiu ka-go Tsi-s-deo, yi-kying zo læ tʽin-zông, w̆e-nyin *Cü*-go zo-we jing-siu-pin; <sup>2</sup> tso keh sing-jün <sup>\[1\]</sup>*di-fông* teng tsing tsiang-bong li-hyiang go Bæn-z-nying: keh-go tsiang-bong z Cü sô tah, feh-z nying *tah-go*.

難間所講個大規模是阿拉有介個祭司頭，已經坐來天上，威嚴主個座位順手邊；<sup>2</sup> 做箇聖善地方<sup>\[1\]</sup>等真帳篷裏向個辦事人。箇個帳篷是主所搭，弗是人搭個。

<sup>\[1\] *Di-fông*; *yiu-teh* *wô*, *tong-si*. 地方；有得話，東西。（原註）</sup>

<sup>3</sup> Væn-pah tsi-s-deo z we-leh hyin li-veh teng tsi-z ka shih-lih; keh-lah keh-we *Tsi-s-deo* yia pih yiu soh-go *tsi-veh* hao hyin. <sup>4</sup> Gyi ziah læ di-ʽô læ-tong, m-neh hao sön tsi-s, ing-we keh-sing tsiao lih-fah hyin li-veh go tsi-s læ-tong: <sup>5</sup> gyi-lah z læ keh tʽin-zông *tong-si-go* siang-mao teng ing-tsong li-hyiang vong-z *Jing-ming*; ziang Mo-si tang-tsiang zao tsiang-bong z-ʽeo teh-djôh meh-z, wô, “Ng yüong siao-sing, væn-pah tong-si tu iao tsiao sæn-zông sô ts-tin ng go yiang-shih ka tso.” \[C. 25. 4O.\] <sup>6</sup> Næn-kæn *Yiæ-su* yi-kying teh-djôh keng kwe-djong-go tsih-veng, ing-we Gyi tso keng hao iah-go Cong-pao, z kʽao-djôh keng hao ing-hyü sô shih-lih-go.

<sup>3</sup> 凡百祭司頭是爲了獻禮物等祭祀介設立；箇拉箇位祭司頭也必有啥個祭物好獻。<sup>4</sup> 其若來地下來東，嘸得好算祭司，因爲箇星照律法獻禮物個祭司來東。<sup>5</sup> 其拉是來箇天上東西個相貌等影蹤裏向奉侍神明；像<u>摩西</u>打賬造帳篷時候得着默示，話，「爾用小心，凡百東西都要照山上所指點爾個樣式介做。」\[C. 25. 4O.\] <sup>6</sup> 難間<u>耶穌</u>已經得着更貴重個職份，因爲其做更好約個中保，是靠着更好應許所設立個。

<sup>7</sup> Keh zin-deo-go *iah* ziah-z m-kao hao pʽi-bing, hao-vong tsæ tang-sön ʽeo-deo-go *iah*. <sup>8</sup> Næn-kæn Jing-ming pʽi-bing gyi-lah, wô, “CÜ z-ka wô, ‘Nyih-ts læ-gyi, Ngô teng Yi-seh-lih keh-kô lin Yiu-da keh-kô iao shih-lih ih-go sing iah; <sup>9</sup> *keh* feh ziang Ngô teng gyi-lah tsu-tsong sô lih-go iah, *ziu-z* te gyi siu iao ling gyi cʽih Yiæ-gyih z-ʽeo *sô* *shih-lih-go*; ing-we gyi-lah feh siu-djü Ngô-go iah, Ngô yia feh kyʽi tsʽæ gyi:’ *keh* *z* CÜ *sô* kông-go. <sup>10</sup> CÜ *yi* wô, ‘Ko-leh keh-sing nyih-ts Ngô teng Yi-seh-lih keh-kô iao lih go iah, ziu-z, pô Ngô-go lih-fah <sup>\[1\]</sup>fông læ gyi hyüong-cong, sia læ gyi sing-li: Ngô we tso gyi-go Jing-ming, gyi we tso Ngô-go pah-sing: <sup>11</sup> hao-vong ling-kyü kao gyi ling-kyü, hyüong-di *kao* gyi hyüong-di, wô, Ng yüong sih-teh CÜ; ing-we væn-pah; feh-leng tseng-kwe pe-zin, tu pih-ding nying-djôh Ngô: <sup>12</sup> ing-we Ngô we kwʽun-shü gyi-go fi-li, feh tsæ kyi-nyiæn gyi-go ze-ôh teng gyi-go ko-væn.’” \[Yl. 31. 31–34.\]

<sup>7</sup> 箇前頭個約若是嘸告好批評，好甮再打算後頭個約。<sup>8</sup> 難間神明批評其拉，話，「主是介話，『日子來其，我等<u>以色列</u>箇家連<u>猶大</u>箇家要設立一個新約。<sup>9</sup> 箇弗像我等其拉祖宗所立個約，就是對其手要領其出<u>埃及</u>時候所設立個；因爲其拉弗守住我個約，我也弗去睬其。』箇是主所講個。<sup>10</sup> 主又話，『過了箇星日子我等<u>以色列</u>箇家要立個約，就是，把我個律法放來其胸中<sup>\[1\]</sup>，寫來其心裏。我會做其個神明，其會做我個百姓。<sup>11</sup> 好甮鄰居教其鄰居，兄弟教其兄弟，話，爾用識得主；因爲凡百，弗論尊貴卑賤，都必定認着我。<sup>12</sup> 因爲我會寬恕其個非禮，弗再記念其個罪惡等其個過犯。』」 \[Yl. 31. 31–34.\]

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng*, s-peh gyi. 本文，賜撥其。（原註）</sup>

<sup>13</sup> Gyi kyi-jün wô *z* “sing *iah*,” ziu s-teh keh zin-deo-go *iah* we gyiu-go: væn-pah gyiu-go sæ-lôh-kyʽi *ziu* gying-wu fi-diao-de.

<sup>12</sup> 其既然話是「新約」，就使得箇前頭個約爲舊個。凡百舊個衰落去就近乎廢掉了。

### H. IX.

Keh zin-deo-go *iah* yia yiu voh-z *Jing-ming* go <sup>\[1\]</sup>li-tsih, teng joh-ü shü-kæn-zông sing-jün-go *di-fông*. <sup>2</sup> Yiu zin-pun-gyüih tsiang-bong be-hao-kæn; li-hyiang yiu teng-dæ, en-coh, teng pa-kæn-go ping: keh tsʽing we “sing-jün *di-fông*.” <sup>3</sup> Wa-yiu di-nyi-dao mun-tsiang ʽeo-go tsiang-bong, tsʽing we “ting sing-jün *di-fông*:” <sup>4</sup> yiu tin-hyiang-go kying-lu, teng dön-kyʽün tʽiah-kying-go iah-siang; *iah-siang* li-hyiang *yiu* tsi mô-nô go kying-bing, Üô-leng tsʽiu-ngô-go bông, teng *lih*-iah-go pe-ba; <sup>5</sup> zông-deo *yiu* yüong-wô-go Kyi-lu-ping tæ-djü eng-zo: *ngô* yin-dzæ tsʽia vong dziang-si kông gyi.

箇前頭個約也有服侍神明個禮節<sup>\[1\]</sup>，等屬於世間上聖善個地方。<sup>2</sup> 有前半橛帳篷備好間；裏向有燈臺，案桌，等擺間個餅。箇稱爲「聖善地方」。<sup>3</sup> 還有第二道幔帳後個帳篷，稱爲「頂聖善地方」。<sup>4</sup> 有點香個經爐，等團圈貼金個約箱；約箱裏向有𣥼嗎嗱個金瓶，<u>亞倫</u>抽芽個棒，等立約個碑牌。<sup>5</sup> 上頭有榮華個<u>基路冰</u>戴住恩座。我現在且甮詳細講其。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kwe-kyü. 或話，規矩。（原註）</sup>

<sup>6</sup> Keh-sing tong-si kyi-jün z-ka zi-be, *cong* tsi-s dziang-tʽong tseo-tsing zin-pun-gyüih tsiang-bong kyʽi voh-z *Jing-ming*: <sup>7</sup> ʽeo-pun-gyüih tsih-yiu tsi-s-deo ih-nyin ih-tsao hao tseo-tsing; yia pih iao *do-leh* hyüih, we-leh gyi-zi teng pah-sing sô feh hyiao-teh ka væn-ko-go ze-kyʽin hyin-zông-kyʽi. <sup>8</sup> Sing-Ling *z-ka* ming-ming ts-tin *ah-lah*, zin-deo-go tsiang-bong wa lih-tong, keh sing-jün *di-fông-go* lu z feh-zing kʽæ-tʽong ming-bah. <sup>9</sup> Keh-go *tsiang-bong* tông-z tso pi-fông; keh z-ʽeo læ-tih hyin li-veh teng tsi-z, kông-tao liang-sing-zông feh neng-keo s-teh voh-z-go cü-kwu djün-be; <sup>10</sup> peh-ko z kyʽüoh-kyʽüoh,” hah-hah, teng kôh-yiang gyiang-nyüoh-go kwe-kyü, keh-sing nyüoh-sing-go <sup>\[1\]</sup>li-tsih; z *tæn-tsih* shih-lih tao kyʽi-gyiu-wun-sing-go nyih-ts.

<sup>6</sup> 箇星東西既然是介齊備，衆祭司長通走進前半橛帳篷去服侍神明。<sup>7</sup> 後半橛只有祭司頭一年一遭好走進；也必要馱勒血，爲了其自等百姓所弗曉得介犯過個罪愆獻上去。<sup>8</sup> 聖靈是介明明指點阿拉，前頭個帳篷還立東，箇聖善地方個路是弗曾開通明白。<sup>9</sup> 箇個帳篷當是做比方。箇時候來的獻禮物等祭祀，講到良心上弗能夠使得服侍個主顧全備。<sup>10</sup> 不過是喫喫，喝喝，等各樣滰肉個規矩，箇星肉身個禮節<sup>\[1\]</sup>；是單只設立到棄舊換新個日子。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kwe-kyü. 或話，規矩。（原註）</sup>

<sup>11</sup> Næn-kæn Kyi-toh læ, tso tsiang-læ hao-cʽü-go Tsi-s-deo, z kying-ko keng do keng djün-be-go tsiang-bong, feh-z *nying* siu sô zao, Ziu-z wô, feh joh-ü shü-kæn-zông; <sup>12</sup> yia feh kʽao-djôh yüong-sæn-yiang teng siao-ngeo-go hyüih, z kʽao-djôh Zi-go hyüih tsih-siao ih-tsao tseo-tsing keh sing-jün *di-fông*, tso-dzing üong-yün cʽü-joh-go tsi-veh. <sup>13</sup> Ziah-z keh-sing yüong-ngeo yüong-sæn-yiang-go hyüih, teng tsʽ-ngeo-go hwe tsah læ u-w̆e cü-kwu-go sing-zông, wa neng-keo tseng-kyʽi gyi, s-teh nyüoh-sing we kyih-zing; <sup>14</sup> ʽo-hwông Kyi-toh, kʽao-djôh üong-yün-go *Sing*-Ling hyin-zông Zi <sup>\[1\]</sup>m-yüô-tin-go *kyi-sing* peh Jing-ming, Gyi-go hyüih pih *neng-keo* kyih-zing ng-lah liang-sing kyʽi-diao keh-sing si-go ʽang-we, hao kyʽi voh-z weh-go Jing-ming!

<sup>11</sup> 難間<u>基督</u>來，做將來好處個祭司頭，是經過更大更全備個帳篷，弗是人手所造，就是話，弗屬於世間上；<sup>12</sup> 也弗靠着雄山羊等小牛個血，是靠着自個血只消一遭走進箇聖善地方，做成永遠取贖個祭物。<sup>13</sup> 若是箇星雄牛雄山羊個血，等雌牛個灰扎來污穢主顧個身上，還能夠尊起其，使得肉身會潔淨；<sup>14</sup> 何況<u>基督</u>，靠着永遠個聖靈獻上自嘸瑕點個<sup>\[1\]</sup>肌身撥神明，其個血必能夠潔淨爾拉良心棄掉箇星死個行爲，好去服侍活個神明！

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, m-pʽi-bing-go. 或話，嘸批評個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>15</sup> Gyi we-leh ka z tso sing iah-go Cong-pao, iao si-ts cʽü-joh væn zin iah go ze, s-teh keh-sing be dziao-læ cü-kwu hao teh-djôh sô ing-hyü üong-yün-go tsʽæn-nyih. <sup>16</sup> (Væn-pah *yi-coh-go* iah pih iao <sup>\[1\]</sup>teng-dæ coh-tʽôh-go cü-kwu si: <sup>17</sup> ing-we *yi-coh-go* iah z læ si-ʽeo yiu gying-dao; ziah-z coh-tʽôh-go cü-kwu wa weh-tong, z m-lih-go.) <sup>13</sup> Keh-lah zin-deo-go *iah*, yia pih iao yüong-djôh hyüih <sup>\[2\]</sup>shih-lih gyi. <sup>19</sup> Mo-si kyi-jün tsiao lih-fah kông ih-tsʽih-go feng-fu peh cong pah-sing tʽing, *ziu* tsiang siao-ngeo teng yüong-sæn-yiang-go hyüih, yi pô shü teng <sup>\[3\]</sup>do-ʽong-go yiang-mao teng ngeo-sih-tsʽao, tsah-tsah keh-peng *iah*-shü lin cong pah-sing, wô, <sup>20</sup> “Keh z Jing-ming sô feng-fu ng-lah iah go hyüih.” \[C. 24. 8.\] <sup>21</sup> Yi yüong hyüih yia tsah-tsah tsiang-bong teng ih-tsʽih kong-vong *Jing-ming* go kyʽi-ming. <sup>22</sup> Ping-tsʽia tsʽô-feh-to long-tsong tong-si tsiao lih-fah iao yüong hyüih kyih-zing gyi: ziah feh-z liu-cʽih hyüih, m-kao hao sô-min.

<sup>15</sup> 其爲了介是做新約個中保，要死仔取贖犯前約個罪，使得箇星被召來主顧好得着所應許永遠個產業。<sup>16</sup> （凡百遺囑個約必要等待<sup>\[1\]</sup>囑託個主顧死。<sup>17</sup> 因爲遺囑個約是來死後有勁道；若是囑託個主顧還活東，是嘸力個。）<sup>18</sup> 箇拉前頭個約，也必要用着血設立其<sup>\[2\]</sup>。<sup>19</sup> <u>摩西</u>既然照律法講一切個吩咐撥衆百姓聽，就將小牛等雄山羊個血，又把水等大紅個<sup>\[3\]</sup>羊毛等牛膝草，扎扎箇本約書連衆百姓，話，<sup>20</sup> 「箇是神明所吩咐爾拉約個血。」\[C. 24. 8.\] <sup>21</sup> 又用血也扎扎帳篷等一切供奉神明個器皿。<sup>22</sup> 並且差弗多攏總東西照律法要用血潔淨其。若弗是流出血，嘸告好赦免。

<sup>\[3\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, ts-ʽong-go. 或話，紫紅個。（原註）</sup>

### H. X.

Lih-fah *peh-ko* yiu tsiang-læ hao-cʽü-go ing-tsong, m̆-teh keh-sing tong-si-go jih-we ying-ziang, sô-yi ih-ngæn feh neng-keo we-leh me-nyin dziang-tʽong sô hyin-zông-go tso-yiang tsi-veh s-teh keh-sing tseo-long-go cü-kwu djün-be.” <sup>2</sup> Ziah *neng-keo* z-ka, keh-sing voh-z-go cü-kwu ih-tsao long kyih-zing, gyi-go liang-sing yi-ʽeo ih-ngæn feh we kôh-djôh ze, keh hyin-tsi-go z-ken <sup>\[1\]</sup>kyʽi feh hao hyih-tsôh? <sup>3</sup> Jün-r keh-sing tsi-veh z *deh-we* nyin-nyin læ-tih di-sing *gyi-go* ze. <sup>4</sup> Keh-sing yüong-ngeo yüong-sæn-yiang-go hyüih tön feh neng-keo djü-diao ze.

律法不過有將來好處個影蹤，嘸得箇星東西個實惠形象，所以一眼弗能夠爲了每年長通所獻上個做樣祭物使得箇星走攏個主顧全備。<sup>2</sup> 若能夠是介，箇星服侍個主顧一遭攏潔淨，其個良心以後一眼弗會覺着罪，箇獻祭個事幹豈弗好歇作<sup>\[1\]</sup>？<sup>3</sup> 然而箇星祭物是特爲年年來的提醒其個罪。<sup>4</sup> 箇星雄牛雄山羊個血斷弗能夠除掉罪。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, pih-ding we hyih-tsôh. 或話，必定會歇作。（原註）</sup>

<sup>5</sup> Keh-lah *Kyi-toh* tseo-tsing shü-kæn-zông læ z-ʽeo yiu ka wô, “Tsi-z teng foh-li Ng feh iao-go; z be-hao ih-go kyi-sing peh Ngô: <sup>6</sup> siao-hwô-go tsi-veh teng joh-ze-go *tsi-veh* Ng feh hwun-hyi. <sup>7</sup> Ngô ziu wô, “Tsʽia-kʽen, Ngô læ-tong; iao tso-dzing Ng Jing-ming go ts-i: *ziang* kying-kyün-li *sô* sia-tih ts-tin Ngô go.” \[S. 4. 68.\] <sup>8</sup> Zông-veng wô, “Tsi-z, foh-li, siao-hwô-go tsi-veh, teng joh-ze-go *tsi-veh*,” (ziu-z tsiao lih-fah sô hyin-zông-go,) “Ng tu feh iao, feh hwun-hyi:” <sup>9</sup> ʽeo-deo wô, “Tsʽia-kʽen, Ngô læ-tong; iao tso-dzing Ng Jing-ming go ts-i.” *Keh* *z* djü-diao gyi zin-deo, iao shih-lih gyi ʽeo-deo go. <sup>10</sup> We-leh keh-go ts-i, kʽao-djôh Yiæ-su Kyi-toh-go kyi-sing tsih-siao ih-tsao hyin-zông, ah-lah z tseng-kyʽi-liao-go.

<sup>5</sup> 箇拉<u>基督</u>走進世間上來時候有介話，「祭祀等福禮爾弗要個；是備好一個肌身撥我。<sup>6</sup> 燒化個祭物等贖罪個祭物爾弗歡喜。<sup>7</sup> 我就話，『且看，我來東；要做成爾神明個旨意。』像經卷裏所寫的指點我個。」\[S. 4. 68.\] <sup>8</sup> 上文話，「祭祀，福禮，燒化個祭物，等贖罪個祭物，」（就是照律法所獻上個，）「爾都弗要，弗歡喜。」<sup>9</sup> 後頭話，「且看，我來東；要做成爾神明個旨意。」箇是除掉其前頭，要設立其後頭個。<sup>10</sup> 爲了箇個旨意，靠着<u>耶穌</u> <u>基督</u>個肌身只消一遭獻上，阿拉是尊起了個。

<sup>11</sup> Væn-pah tsi-s me-nyih lih-ts kong-vong *Jing-ming*, le-tsʽ hyin-zông tso-yiang tsi-veh, z ih-ngæn feh neng-keo tʽeh-diao ze: <sup>12</sup> tsih-yiu keh-we *Kyi-toh* hyin-zông ih-go joh-ze tsi-veh, dziang-tʽong zo læ Jing-ming-go jing-siu-pin; <sup>13</sup> yi-ʽeo tæn-teng Gyi dziu-dih tso Gyi-go dah-kyiah-teng. <sup>14</sup> Gyi hyin-zông ih-go *tsi-veh*, we s-teh keh-sing be tseng-kyʽi-go cü-kwu üong-yün djün-be.

<sup>11</sup> 凡百祭司每日立仔供奉神明，屢次獻上做樣祭物，是一眼弗能夠脫掉罪。<sup>12</sup> 只有箇位<u>基督</u>獻上一個贖罪祭物，長通坐來神明個順手邊；<sup>13</sup> 以後單等其仇敵做其個踏腳凳。<sup>14</sup> 其獻上一個祭物，會使得箇星被尊起個主顧永遠全備。

<sup>15</sup> Keh-we Sing-Ling yia teng ah-lah tso te-tsing: Gyi yü-sin sô kông, <sup>16</sup> “CÜ z-ka wô, Ko-leh keh-sing nyih-ts Ngô teng gyi-lah iao lih-go iah, ziu-z, pô Ngô-go lih-fah fông læ gyi sing-li, sia læ gyi hyüong-cong ; <sup>17</sup> *ping-tsʽia* *wô*, “Ngô feh tsæ kyi-nyiæn gyi-go ze-ôh teng gyi-go ko-væn.” \[Yl. 31. 33, 34.\] <sup>18</sup> Ze-ôh teng ko-væn *yi-kying* sô-diao, hao-vong tsæ hyin joh-ze-go *tsi-veh*.

<sup>15</sup> 箇位聖靈也等阿拉做對證。其預先所講，<sup>16</sup> 「主是介話，『過了箇星日子我等其拉要立個約，就是，把我個律法放來其心裏，寫來其胸中；』」<sup>17</sup> 並且話，「『我弗再記念其個罪惡等其個過犯。』」\[Yl. 31. 33, 34.\] <sup>18</sup> 罪惡等過犯已經赦掉，好甮再獻贖罪個祭物。

<sup>19</sup> KA-NI, hyüong-di, ah-lah kʽao-djôh Yiæ-su-go hyüih kʽo-yi fông-tæn tseo-tsing keh sing-jün *di-fông*; <sup>20</sup> (ziu-z Gyi we-leh ah-lah sô sing-kʽæ keh-da tsʽoh-tsæn-sing-go weh-lu, dzong keh-dao mun-tsiang, ziu-z Gyi nyüoh-sing, *ka* *ko-læ-go*: ) <sup>21</sup> ping-tsʽia **yiu** ih-go do Tsi-s djü Jing-ming-go ih-kô,—— <sup>22</sup> *sô-yi* ah-lah, be *hyüih* tsah læ sing-li kyʽi-diao liang-sing sô *tsah-vah-go* ôh, yia be tsʽing shü gyiang-*ken-zing* kyi-sing, kæ yüong dzing-jih-go sing-dziang toh-jih-go siang-sing kyʽi tseo-long *Jing-ming-go* *u-sen*.

<sup>19</sup> 介呢，兄弟，阿拉靠着<u>耶穌</u>個血可以放膽走進箇聖善地方；<sup>20</sup> （就是其爲了阿拉所新開箇埭簇斬新個活路，從箇道幔帳，就是其肉身，介過來個。）<sup>21</sup> 並且有一個大祭司治神明個一家，——<sup>22</sup> 所以阿拉，被血扎來心裏棄掉良心所責罰個惡，也被清水滰乾淨肌身，該用誠實個心腸篤實個相信去走攏神明個烏碎。

<sup>23</sup> Ah-lah sô siang-vông-go *foh-kyʽi*, kæ lih-ding cü-i kyʽi tsiao-jing gyi; feh-kʽo yiao-dong: ing-we ing-hyü *ah-lah* go Cü-kwu z sing-jih-go. <sup>24</sup> Ping-tsʽia kæ dô-kô kwu-djôh, *dô-kô* min-li, dzeng jing-æ kyʽi ʽang-jün. <sup>25</sup> *Kyiao-we-li* feh-kʽo feh jü-long, ziang yiu-teh-go nying ka; z iao *siang*-kyʽün: kʽen-kyin keh-go nyih-ts gying-de, kæ yü-kô *z-ka* tso.

<sup>23</sup> 阿拉所想望個福氣，該立定主意去招認其；弗可搖動。因爲應許阿拉個主顧是信實個。<sup>24</sup> 並且該大家顧着，大家勉勵，存仁愛去行善。<sup>25</sup> 教會裏弗可弗聚攏，像有得個人介；是要相勸。看見箇個日子近了，該愈加是介做。

<sup>26</sup> Ah-lah sih-teh tsing dao-li ts-ʽeo ziah-z kwu-i kyʽi væn-ze, tön feh neng-keo tsæ yiu joh-ze-go tsi-veh; <sup>27</sup> tsih-yiu gwah-gwah-teo teng-dæ sing-pʽun, teng tsiang-læ iao kyʽüoh-meh dziu-dih *ziang* *mang*-ho *ka* ô-wông. <sup>28</sup> Væn-pah kyʽi-diao Mo-si lih-fah go cü-kwu, yiu liang-sæn-go nying tso te-tsing, *pih* *iao* si, feh æ-lin *gyi*: <sup>29</sup> ʽo-hwông dzin-dah Jing-ming-go Ng-ts, sön sô tseng-kyʽi gyi keh *lih*-iah-go hyüih z feh. kyih-zing, ping-tsʽia tsao-tʽah keh eng-we-go *Sing*-Ling, ng tsʽeng keh-go *nying* feh kæ ziu keng-kô djong-go ying-vah ma? <sup>30</sup> Ah-lah hyiao-teh jü yiu *ka* kông, “Cü wô, ‘Sing-ün dzæ Ngô, Ngô we wæn-pao:’” \[Sm. 32. 35.\] wa-yiu, “CÜ we sing-pʽun Gyi-go pah-sing.” \[Sm. 32. 36.\] <sup>31</sup> Lôh læ weh Jing-ming-go siu-li, tsing-tsing kʽo pʽô.

<sup>26</sup> 阿拉識得真道理之後若是故意去犯罪，斷弗能夠再有贖罪個祭物；<sup>27</sup> 只有刮刮抖等待審判，等將來要喫沒仇敵像猛火介𢛟惶。<sup>28</sup> 凡百棄掉<u>摩西</u>律法個主顧，有兩三個人做對證，必要死，弗哀憐其。<sup>29</sup> 何況踐踏神明個兒子，算所尊起其箇立約個血是弗潔淨，並且糟蹋箇恩惠個聖靈，爾忖箇個人弗該受更加重個刑罰嗎？<sup>30</sup> 阿拉曉得誰有介講，「主話，『伸冤在我，我會還報。』」\[Sm. 32. 35.\] 還有，「主會審判其個百姓。」\[Sm. 32. 36.\] <sup>31</sup> 落來活神明個手裏，真正可怕。

<sup>32</sup> Ng-lah kæ tsʽeng-tao zin-deo-go nyih-ts, ng-lah teh-djôh kwông-ming ts-ʽeo ziu we jing-næ hyü-to næn-deo, *tsing-ziang* pi-vu *ka*: <sup>33</sup> fi-dæn læ cong-nying-go ngæn-zin be zôh-mô, ziu kwʽeng-kwʽu; ziu-z bih-nying tsao-djôh keh-cü-ka *næn-deo*, ng-lah yia we dô-kô yiu-veng. <sup>34</sup> Ing-we ng-lah z tʽi-liang æ-lin ngô-go lin-diao; *nying-kô* deh ng-go kô-kyi, ng-lah we hwun-hwun-hyi-hyi; <sup>\[1\]</sup>ing-we hyiao-teh ng-lah zi læ tʽin-zông yiu keng-kô hao, dziang læ-tong-go kô-kyi.

<sup>32</sup> 爾拉該忖到前頭個日子，爾拉得着光明之後就會忍耐許多難頭，正像比武介。<sup>33</sup> 非但來「衆人個眼前被𧮙罵，受困苦；就是別人遭着箇株介難頭，爾拉也會大家有份。<sup>34</sup> 因爲爾拉是體諒哀憐我個鏈條；人家奪爾個家計，爾拉會歡歡喜喜；因爲曉得爾拉自<sup>\[1\]</sup>來天上有更加好，長來東個家計。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, zi hyiao-teh. 或話，自曉得。（原註）</sup>

<sup>35</sup> Ka-ni, feh-kʽo sông-diao ng-go tæn-liang; gyi tsong we teh-djôh do-do sông-s. “Ng-lah pih iao jing-næ; s-teh ng-lah, tso-dzing Jing-ming-go ts-i, hao teh-djôh keh-go ing-hyü. Tsæ z ih-zông-zông, “keh-we iao læ go Cü-kwu ziu tao, feh we tæn-kôh. <sup>38</sup> Tsing-dzih nying z dzong siang-sing hao weh: ziah-z tao-tʽe-go cü-kwu, Ngô-go sing tsong feh hwun-hyi gyi.” \[Hp. 2. 3,4, LXX.\] <sup>39</sup> Tsih-yiu ah-lah feh-z tao-tʽe iao mih-vông go *nying*, z siang-sing hao kyiu *zi* weh-ling go.

<sup>35</sup> 介呢，弗可喪掉爾個膽量；其總會得着大大賞賜。<sup>36</sup> 爾拉必要忍耐；使得爾拉，做成神明個旨意，好得着箇個應許。<sup>37</sup> 再是一晌晌，箇位要來個主顧就到，弗會耽擱。<sup>38</sup> 「正直人是從相信好活；若是倒退個主顧，我個心總弗歡喜其。」\[Hp. 2. 3,4, LXX.\] <sup>39</sup> 只有阿拉弗是倒退要滅亡個人，是相信好救自活靈個。

### H. XI.

NÆN-KÆN siang-sing, ziu-z sô siang-vông *tong-si*-go <sup>\[1\]</sup>dziah-lôh; sô m-neh kʽen-kyin tong-si-go bing-kyü. <sup>2</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, keh-sing tsiang-lao z teh-djôh hao te-tsing.

難間相信，就是所想望東西個着落<sup>\[1\]</sup>；所嘸得看見東西個憑據。<sup>2</sup> 爲了有相信，箇星長老是得着好對證。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kyʽüôh-jih. 或話，確實。（原註）</sup>

<sup>3</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, ah-lah hyiao-teh tʽin-di z kʽao-djôh Jing-ming-go ʽao-ling sô zao-dzing; gyi kʽen-leh-kyin-go tong-si feh-z dzong yi-kying hyin-cʽih-tong ka læ-go.

<sup>3</sup> 爲了有相信，阿拉曉得天地是靠着神明個號令所造成；其看勒見個東西弗是從已經顯出東介來個。

<sup>4</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Üô-pah hyin tsi-veh peh Jing-ming pi Kæ-ing yü-kô hao: kʽao-djôh keh-go *siang-sing* *Üô-pah* yiu te-tsing gyi z tsing-dzih-go, ing-we, Jing-ming te-tsing gyi li-veh: sô-yi gyi se-tsih si, dzing-gyiu <sup>\[1\]</sup>læ-tih kông shih-wô.

<sup>4</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>亞伯</u>獻祭物撥神明比<u>該隱</u>愈加好。靠着箇個相信<u>亞伯</u>有對證其是正直個，因爲，神明對證其禮物。所以其雖即死，仍舊來的講說話<sup>\[1\]</sup>。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, nying-kô kông-tao gyi-go z-ken. 或話，人家講到其個事幹。（原註）</sup>

<sup>5</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Yi-nôh be siu-leh-kyʽi, m-neh kʽen-kyin si; “ing-we Jing-ming yi-kying siu-leh-kyʽi, sô-yi *cong-ü* zing gyi feh-djôh:” \[Ts. 5. 24.\] feh-zing siu-kyʽi ts-zin, yiu te-tsing gyi z peh Jing-ming hwun-hyi. <sup>6</sup> M̆-teh siang-sing, feh neng-keo peh *Gyi* hwun-hyi: ing-we tseo-long Jing-ming u-sen læ-go cü-kwu pih iao siang-sing *Gyi* z yiu-go, yia we sông-s keh-sing gyin-sing gyiu Gyi go nying.

<sup>5</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>以諾</u>被收勒去，嘸得看見死；「因爲神明已經收勒去，所以終於尋其弗着。」\[Ts. 5. 24.\] 弗曾收去之前，有對證其是撥神明歡喜。<sup>6</sup> 嘸得相信，弗能夠撥其歡喜。因爲走攏神明烏碎來個主顧必要相信其是有個，也會賞賜箇星虔心求其個人。

<sup>7</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Nô-üô teh-djôh meh-z ts-tin feh-zing kʽen-kyin-go z-ken, <sup>\[1\]</sup>sing-li pʽô-gyü, ziu be-bæn ih-tsah kyiu zi *ʽeh-kô* go fông-jün; sô-yi ding shü-kæn-zông-go ze, yi tsih-djôh keh dzong siang-sing ka læ-go kong-yi tso tsʽæn-nyih.

<sup>7</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>挪亞</u>得着默示指點弗曾看見個事幹，心裏怕懼<sup>\[1\]</sup>，就備辦一隻救自合家個方船。所以定世間上個罪，又接着箇從相信介來個公義做產業。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, dzeng tseng-kying pʽô-gyü-go sing; *ʽôh*, siao-sing kwu-djôh. 或話，存尊敬怕懼個心；或，小心顧着。（原註）</sup>

<sup>8</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Üô-pah-lah-hen be dziao-læ ziu vong-ts tseo-cʽih tao gyi tsiang-læ iao teh-djôh tso tsʽæn-nyih go u-sen; gyi tseo-cʽih *z-ʽeo* feh hyiao-teh tao ʽah-li kyʽi. <sup>9</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, ziu tæn-kôh læ sô ing-hyü-go di-fông, tsing-ziang bih-nying-go *di-fông* ka; teng Yi-sah Yüô-kôh tu deng læ tsiang-bong li-hyiang: (*ziu-z* *teng* *gyi* dô-kô tsih-djôh keh ing-hyü go cü-kwu: ) <sup>10</sup> gyi z teng-dæ keh-zo yiu keng-kyi go dzing-ts, Jing-ming sô kyin-lih, sô tsʽông-zao-go.

<sup>8</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>亞伯拉罕</u>被召來就奉旨走出到其將來要得着做產業個烏碎；其走出時候弗曉得到何裏去。<sup>9</sup> 爲了有相信，就耽擱來所應許個地方，正像別人個地方介；等<u>以撒</u> <u>雅各</u>都庉來帳篷裏向。（就是等其大家接着箇應許個主顧。）<sup>10</sup> 其是等待箇座有根基個城子，神明所建立，所創造個。

<sup>11</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Sah-lah zi, z-ʽeo yi-kying ko-deo, yia neng-keo dzing-yüing sang *ng-ts*, ing-we sön keh ing-hyü *gyi* Cü-kwu z sing-jih-go. <sup>12</sup> Keh-lah dzong ih-go hyüih-kyʽi yi-kying kwʽu-sao-go nying we sang-tsʽæn ziang tʽin-li-go sing-siu ka to, ziang hæ-du-go sô-nyi ka su-feh-pin.

<sup>11</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>撒拉</u>自，時候已經過頭，也能夠成孕生兒子，因爲算箇應許其主顧是信實個。<sup>12</sup> 箇拉從一個血氣已經枯燥個人會生產像天裏個星宿介多，像海塗個沙泥介數弗遍。

<sup>13</sup> Keh-sing tu <sup>\[1\]</sup>dzeng siang-sing-go sing, si-de; feh-zing teh-djôh keh-sing ing-hyü, tæn-tsih yün-yün kʽen-kyin gyi,: toh-jih siang-sing *gyi*, ih-sing kyʽi-mo gyi, zi wô læ shü-kæn-zông z nga-læ-nying, z cʽih-meng-nying. <sup>14</sup> Ka kông-go cü-kwu z ming-ming wô gyi læ-tih zing peng-koh. <sup>15</sup> Ziah-z kwô-nyiæn sô tseo-cʽih-go *di-fông*, tsong yiu kyi-we hao kyü-kyʽi: <sup>16</sup> næn-kæn z kyʽi-mo keng hao-go *koh*, ziu-z tʽin-*koh*: sô-yi Jing-ming tsʽing we gyi-lah-go Jing-ming, m-kao wông-kʽong; z yi-kying teng gyi-lah be-bæn ih-zo dzing-ts.

<sup>13</sup> 箇星都存相信個心<sup>\[1\]</sup>，死了；弗曾得着箇星應許，單只遠遠看見其，篤實相信其，一心企慕其，自話來世間上是外來人，是出門人。<sup>14</sup> 介講個主顧是明明話其來的尋本國。<sup>15</sup> 若是掛念所走出個地方，總有機會好歸去。<sup>16</sup> 難間是企慕更好個國，就是天國。所以神明稱爲其拉個神明，嘸告惶恐；是已經等其拉備辦一座城子。

<sup>\[1\] *Peng-veng*, tsiao siang-sing ka. 本文，照相信介。（原註）</sup>

<sup>17, 18</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Üô-pah-lah-hen be s-lin z-ʽeo ziu hyin-zông Yi-sah: gyi z yi-kying tsih-ziu keh-sing ing-hyü, *Jing-ming* *yi* teng gyi wô, “Dzong Yi-sah ka læ-go hao sön ng-go ʽeo-dæ,” \[Ts. 21. 12.\] *næn-kæn* *wa* we hyin-zông gyi doh-yiang *ng-ts*. <sup>19</sup> *Gyi-zi* tsʽeng ding-tông Jing-ming neng-keo s-teh *gyi* dzong si-go cong-nyiang weh-cün-læ: ʽeo-deo tso-læ tsing-ziang dzong si-go cong-nyiang yi teh-tao-siu.

<sup>17, 18</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>亞伯拉罕</u>被試煉時候就獻上<u>以撒</u>。其是已經接受箇星應許，神明又等其話，「從<u>以撒</u>介來個好算爾個後代，」難間還會獻上其獨養兒子。<sup>19</sup> 其自忖停當神明能夠使得其從死個中央活轉來；後頭做來正像從死個中央又得到手。

<sup>20</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Yi-sah ts-tin tsiang-læ z-ken coh-foh Yüô-kôh teng Yi-sao.

<sup>20</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>以撒</u>指點將來事幹祝福<u>雅各</u>等以掃。

<sup>21</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Yüô-kôh ling-si z-ʽeo coh-foh Iah-seh liang-go ng-ts; ping-tsʽia “tông-leh æ-dziang pa-pa *Jing-ming*.” \[Ts. 47. 31, LXX.\]

<sup>21</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>雅各</u>臨死時候祝福<u>約瑟</u>兩個兒子；並且「擋勒哀杖拜拜神明。」\[Ts. 47. 31, LXX.\]

<sup>22</sup> We-leh yiu siang-sing, Iah-seh ling-cong z-ʽeo <sup>\[1\]</sup>kông-tao Yi-seh-lih nying cʽih *Yiæ-gyih* go z-ken, yia ts-tin zi-go s-kweh tʽôh-fu *gyi*.

<sup>22</sup> 爲了有相信，<u>約瑟</u>臨終時候講到<sup>\[1\]</sup><u>以色列</u>人出<u>埃及</u>個事幹，也指點自個屍骨託付其。

<sup>39</sup> Keh-sing nying se-tsih kʽao-djôh siang-sing tu yiu hao te-tsing, *tsih-z* m-neh teh-djôh keh-go ing-hyü: <sup>40</sup> ing-we Jing-ming z we-leh ah-lah yü-sin be-bæn ih-yiang keng hao-go z-ken; s-teh gyi-lah, ziah-z m̆-teh ah-lah dong-de læ-tong, feh neng-keo djün-be.

### H. XII.

Sô-yi ah-lah kyi-jün yiu ka hyü-to kyin-tsing *ziang* yüing *ka* dön-kyʽün we-djü, ziu kæ sia-diao ih-tsʽih-go djong-tæn, teng yüong-yi dzin-ziao go ze-nyih; sô ts-ying ah-lah go lu-dzing, kæ næ-sing peng-zông-kyʽi; <sup>2</sup> *kæ* *z-djông* nyiang-vông Yiæ-su, *ziu-z* keh-we <sup>\[1\]</sup>shih-lih wun-djün *ah-lah-go* siang-sing Cü-kwu: Gyi, we-leh sô ts-tin Gyi go kwʽa-lôh, we jing-næ jih-z-kô-go *kwʽu-deo*, feh kwu tao-me; *yin-dzæ* zo læ Jing-ming zo-we-go jing-siu-pin. <sup>3</sup> Gyi yi-kying jing-næ ze-nying keh-cü-ka w̆ang-dzin, ng yüong s-tsʽeng Gyi; sæn-leh ng-lah bi-gyün, sing-li tʽeh-lih.

所以阿拉既然有介許多見證像雲介團圈圍住，就該卸掉一切個重擔，等容易纏擾個罪孽；所指引阿拉個路程，該耐心奔上去；<sup>2</sup> 該時常仰望<u>耶穌</u>，就是箇位設立<sup>\[1\]</sup>完全阿拉個相信主顧。其，爲了所指點其個快樂，會忍耐十字架個苦頭，弗顧倒霉；現在坐來神明座位個順手邊。<sup>3</sup> 其已經忍耐罪人箇株介橫纏，爾用思忖其；省勒爾拉疲倦，心裏脫力。

<sup>\[1\] Shih-lih: *ʽôh* *wô*, ling-deo; *ʽôh*, tso Keng-nyün, Sd. 3. 15; *ʽôh*, tso Kyüing-wông, Sd. 5. 31. H. 2. 10. 設立：或話，零頭；或，做根源，Sd. 3. 15；或，做君王，Sd. 5. 31. H. 2. 10。（原註）</sup>

<sup>4</sup> Ng-lah ti-tông ze-ôh, wa m̆-teh tsang tao hyüih-cʽih: <sup>5</sup> ping-tsʽia z yi-kying mông-kyi sô kyʽün ng-lah, ziang kyʽün ng-ts ka, *ziu-z* *wô*, “Ngô-go ng-ts, Cü tsah-be ng, feh-kʽo kyʽing-yi hweh-liah; Cü pʽi-bing ng, feh-kʽo tʽeh-lih: <sup>6</sup> ing-we Cü sô æ-sih-go, *tsong* tsah-be gyi; væn-pah sô siu-liu-go ng-ts, tsong we tang gyi.” \[Ty. 3. 11, 12, LXX.\] <sup>7</sup> Ng-lah ziah næ-sing ziu tsah-be, z Jing-ming dæ ng ziang ng-ts ka: ʽah-li ih-go ng-ts, ah-tia feh tsah-be gyi ma? <sup>8</sup> Næn-kæn cong *ng-ts* tu be tsah-be; ng-lah ziah feh be tsah-be, ziu feh-z ng-ts, z-go yia-cong.

<sup>4</sup> 爾拉抵擋罪惡，還嘸得爭到血出。<sup>5</sup> 並且是已經忘記所勸爾拉，像勸兒子介，就是話，「我個兒子，主責備爾，弗可輕易忽略；主批評爾，弗可脫力。<sup>6</sup> 因爲主所愛惜個，總責備其；凡百所收留個兒子，總會打其。」\[Ty. 3. 11, 12, LXX.\] <sup>7</sup> 爾拉若耐心受責備，是神明待爾像兒子介。何裏一個兒子，阿爹弗責備其嗎？<sup>8</sup> 難間衆兒子都被責備；爾拉若弗被責備，就弗是兒子，是個野種。

<sup>9</sup> Tsæ-wô, yiu nyüoh-sing-go ah-tia tsah-be ah-lah, jông-tsʽia we kying-djong gyi: ʽo-hwông ling-sing-go Ah-tia, feh kæ keng-kô voh Gyi, hao teh-djôh weh-ming ma? <sup>10</sup> Keh-sing *nyüoh-sing-go* *ah-tia* tsiao zi-go i-s dzæn-z tsah-be ah-lah; dæn-z keh-we *ling-sing-go* *Ah-tia* z we-leh *ah-lah-go* ih-cʽü, iao ah-lah dô-kô teh-djôh Gyi-go sing-jün. <sup>11</sup> Ts-ü væn-pah-go tsah-be, be tsah-be z-ʽeo hao-ziang m-neh kwʽa-lôh, tsih-yiu iu-meng; ʽeo-deo z we kyih ʽo-bing kong-yi-go ko-ts peh gyi sô lin-lin cü-kwu.

<sup>9</sup> 再話，有肉身個阿爹責備阿拉，尚且會敬重其。何況靈性個阿爹，弗該更加服其，好得着活命嗎？<sup>10</sup> 箇星肉身個阿爹照自個意思暫時責備阿拉。但是箇位靈性個阿爹是爲了阿拉個益處，要阿拉大家得着其個聖善。<sup>11</sup> 至於凡百個責備，被責備時候好像嘸得快樂，只有憂悶；後頭是會結和平公義個果子撥其所練練主顧。

<sup>12</sup> Sô-yi “m-lih-go siu, nyün-ziah-go kyiah-kʽo-deo, yüong teng gyi kyʽi-læ:” \[Y. 35. 3.\] <sup>13</sup> yüong <sup>\[1\]</sup>pʽu-dzih *gyi* lu peh ng-lah-go kyiah *hao* *tseo*,” \[Ty. 4. 26, LXX.\] s-teh keh kwa-kyiah-go feh we tseo-tsʽo, fæn-cün neng-keo i-hao. <sup>14</sup> Yüong zông-kying kyʽi ʽo-moh cong-nying; *yüong* *ken-kying* *kyʽi* *tse-gyiu* sing-jün; ziah feh-z sing-jün, m-nying neng-keo kyin Cü. <sup>15</sup> Yüong *tông-sing* kwu-djôh, kʽong-pʽô yiu nying <sup>\[2\]</sup>tsʽo-lôh, feh neng-keo teh-djôh Jing-ming-go eng-we: kʽong-pʽô yiu ôh-doh-go keng-cü tsʽiu-cʽih ngô læ, kao-ziao *ng-lah*, \[*Kʽen* Sm. 29. 18.\] s-teh hyü-to nying be gyi ta-le: <sup>16</sup> kʽong-pʽô? yiu soh-go ying-lön cü-kwu, ʽôh-tsia vông-tsôh-vông-we-go, ziang Yi-sao ka; gyi we-leh ih-tsʽæn væn m-diao zi tsiang-ts-go pe-veng. <sup>17</sup> Ng-lah yia hyiao-teh gyi ʽeo-deo iao-siang tsih-djôh *tsiang-ts-go* coh-foh, z be kyʽi-diao: gyi se-tsih ngæn-li beh-cʽih iao gyiu <sup>\[3\]</sup>w̆æn-we-ts cʽü, cong-ü feh neng-keo.

<sup>12</sup> 所以「嘸力個手，軟弱個腳䯊頭，用敦其起來。」\[Y. 35. 3.\] <sup>13</sup> 用鋪直<sup>\[1\]</sup>其路撥爾拉個腳好走」\[Ty. 4. 26, LXX.\]，使得箇拐腳個弗會走錯，反轉能夠醫好。<sup>14</sup> 用上緊去和睦衆人；用趕緊去追求聖善；若弗是聖善，嘸人能夠見主。<sup>15</sup> 用當心顧着，恐怕有人錯落，弗能夠得着<sup>\[2\]</sup>神明個恩惠。恐怕有惡毒個根株抽出芽來，攪擾爾拉，\[看 Sm. 29. 18.\] 使得許多人被其帶累。<sup>16</sup> 恐怕有啥個淫亂主顧，或者妄作妄爲個，像以掃介；其爲了一餐飯嘸掉自長子個輩份。<sup>17</sup> 爾拉也曉得其後頭要想接着長子個祝福，是被棄掉。其雖即眼淚浡出要求挽回之處<sup>\[3\]</sup>，終於弗能夠。

<sup>25</sup> Yüong kying-jing feh-kʽo kyʽi-diao keh-we læ-tih *kông-go*. Ing-we læ di-ʽô kông-go, keh-sing kyʽi-diao Gyi cü-kwu jông-tsʽia bi-feh-ko; ʽo-hwông dzong tʽin-zông *kông-go*, ah-lah *ziah* feh kyʽi tsʽæ Gyi, *dza* *neng-keo* *bi-leh-ko* *ni*? <sup>26</sup> Keh-go z-ʽeo Gyi-go sing-ing cing-dong di: næn-kæn yiu ing-hyü, wô, “Tsæ z ih-tsao, Ngô fi-dæn cing-dong di, yia *cing-dong* tʽin.” \[*Kʽen* Hk. 2. 6.\] <sup>27</sup> Keh-kyü “tsæ z ih-tsao go *shih-wô* z ming-ming ts-tin keh-sing <sup>\[1\]</sup>be cing-dong-go tong-si, (kyi-jün z zao-cʽih-læ-go,) pih iao keng-kæ; s-teh keh-sing feh be cing-dong-go hao dziang læ-tong. <sup>28</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah kyi-jün tsih-ziu ih-go feh-kʽo cing-dong-go koh-veng, kæ <sup>\[2\]</sup>dzeng ken-eng-go sing; z-ka bao kong-kying pʽô-gyü kyʽi voh-z Jing-ming, peh Gyi hwun-hyi: <sup>29</sup> ing-we ah-lah-go Jing-ming z “*ziang* læ-tih dziah-go *mang*-ho *ka*.” \[Sm. 4. 24: 9. 3.\]

<sup>25</sup> 用謹慎弗可棄掉箇位來的講個。因爲來地下講個，箇星棄掉其主顧尚且避弗過；何況從天上講個，阿拉若弗去睬其，咋能夠避勒過呢？<sup>26</sup> 箇個時候其個聲音震動地。難間有應許，話，「再是一遭，我非但震動地，也震動天。」\[看 Hk. 2. 6.\] <sup>27</sup> 箇句「再是一遭」個說話是明明指點箇星被震動個<sup>\[1\]</sup>東西，（既然是造出來個，）必要更改；使得箇星弗被震動個好長來東。<sup>28</sup> 所以阿拉既然接受一個弗可震動個國份，該存感恩個心<sup>\[2\]</sup>；是介抱恭敬怕懼去服侍神明，撥其歡喜。<sup>29</sup> 因爲阿拉個神明是「像來的着個猛火介。」\[Sm. 4. 24: 9. 3.\]

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, kʽo-yi cing-dong-go. 或話，可以震動個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>\[2\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, yiu eng-we; *ʽôh*, pô eng-we siu-leh-lao. 或話，有恩惠；或，把恩惠守勒牢。（原註）</sup>

### H. XIII.

KÆ z-djông tsʽing-æ hyüong-di. <sup>2</sup> Iao ing-gying tsih-dæ cʽih-meng-nying, feh-kʽo mông-kyi: ing-we *zin-deo* yiu nying z-ka tso-fah, feh-cü-feh-kôh we tsih-dæ tʽin-s. <sup>3</sup> Yüong kyi-nyiæn keh-sing be lin-diao so-djü-kæn-go, ziang ng-zi *teng* *gyi* dô-kô so-djü ka: *kyi-nyiæn* keh-sing ziu-næn-go, ing-we hyiao-teh ng-zi yia yiu *ziu-næn-go* kyi-sing. <sup>4</sup> Hweng-ing z-ken, cong-*nying* tu kæ kʽen-djong; gyi min-zông yia *kæ* m-yüô-tin-go: ziah-z ying-lön teng kæn-ying-go cü-kwu, Jing-ming we pʽun-tön gyi. <sup>5</sup> Tso-nying feh-kʽo dzeng tʽen-dzæ-go sing; sô yiu-go tong-si, kæ cü-tsoh; ing-we Jing-ming yiu ka wô, “Ngô feh we li-kʽæ ng, yia feh we kyʽi-diao ng:” \[Is. l. 5.\] <sup>6</sup> sô-yi ah-lah hao fông-tæn wô, “CÜ z ngô-go pông-siu; ngô feh pʽô nying-kô dza dæ ngô.” \[S. 118. 6.\]

該時常親愛兄弟。<sup>2</sup> 要殷勤接待出門人，弗可忘記。因爲前頭有人是介做法，弗知弗覺會接待天使。<sup>3</sup> 用記念箇星被鏈條鎖住間個，像爾自等其大家鎖住介。記念箇星受難個，因爲曉得爾自也有受難個肌身。<sup>4</sup> 婚姻事幹，衆人都該看重；其眠牀也該嘸瑕點個。若是淫亂等姦淫個主顧，神明會判斷其。<sup>5</sup> 做人弗可存貪財個心；所有個東西，該知足。因爲神明有介話，「我弗會離開爾，也弗會棄掉爾。」\[Is. l. 5.\] <sup>6</sup> 所以阿拉好放膽話，「主是我個幫手；我弗怕人家咋待我。」\[S. 118. 6.\]

<sup>7</sup> Keh-sing yi-zin <sup>\[1\]</sup>ying-dao ng, djün Jing-ming-go dao-li peh ng tʽing go cü-kwu, yüong kyi-nyiæn gyi: ng-lah ts-si kʽen gyi ʽang-i-dong-zing-go kwe-keng, yüong ʽôh gyi siang-sing-go yiang.

<sup>7</sup> 箇星以前引導爾<sup>\[1\]</sup>，傳神明個道理撥爾聽個主顧，用記念其。爾拉仔細看其行意動靜個歸根，用學其相信個樣。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, ng-lah li-hyiang tso we-deo-we-nao go. 或話，爾拉裏向做爲頭爲腦個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>8</sup> Zin-deo, næn-kæn, tao shü-shü-dæ-dæ, Yiæ-su Kyi-toh z üong feh keng-kæ. <sup>9</sup> M-nao be keh-sing yiu-ho ʽao-su zia-kyiao yiao-dong: sing-li kʽao-djôh eng-we neng-keo kyin-kwu, z hao-go; feh-*kʽo* kʽao-djôh kyʽüoh-zih; ing-we liu-sing kyʽüoh-zih go cü-kwu z vu-ih-go.

<sup>8</sup> 前頭，難間，到世世代代，<u>耶穌</u> <u>基督</u>是永弗更改。<sup>9</sup> 嘸得好被箇星有夥號數邪教搖動。心裏靠着恩惠能夠堅固，是好個；弗可靠着喫食。因爲留心喫食個主顧是無益個。

<sup>10</sup> Ah-lah yiu ih-go tsi-dæn, gyi-go *li-veh* keh-sing tsiang-bong-li vong-z-go cü-kwu feh neng-keo kyʽüoh gyi. <sup>11</sup> Keh-sing sang-kʽeo gyi-go hyüih z tsi-s-deo sô ta-tsing sing-jün di-fông iao joh *nying-go* ze, gyi-go sing-tʽi z læ ying-za-nga be *ho* me-diao: <sup>12</sup> sô-yi Yiæ-su, iao pô Zi-go hyüih tseng-kyʽi pah-sing, yia læ dzing-meng-nga ziu kwʽu. <sup>13</sup> Ka-ni, ah-lah *yia* kæ tseo-cʽih ying-za-nga tao Gyi u-sen kyʽi, *dzing-nyün* tæn-tông Gyi-go zôh-mô. <sup>14</sup> Ah-lah dông-deo m̆-teh dziang læ-tong-go dzing-ts, z tæn gyiu keh-zo tsiang-læ-go.

<sup>10</sup> 阿拉有一個祭壇，其個禮物箇星帳篷裏奉侍個主顧弗能夠喫其。<sup>11</sup> 箇星牲口其個血是祭司頭所帶進聖善地方要贖人個罪，其個身體是來營寨外被火𤈦掉。<sup>12</sup> 所以<u>耶穌</u>，要把自個血尊起百姓，也來城門外受苦。<sup>13</sup> 介呢，阿拉也該走出營寨外到其烏碎去，情願擔當其個𧮙罵。<sup>14</sup> 阿拉蕩頭嘸得長來東個城子，是單求箇座將來個。

<sup>15</sup> Sô-yi ah-lah kæ kʽao-djôh Yiæ-su pô tsæn-me-go tsi-veh z-kʽeh hyin-zông peh Jing-ming, ziu-z *ah-lah* kʽeo-li ken-zia Gyi ming-deo go ko-ts. <sup>16</sup> Ping-tsʽia kyʽi ʽang hao-z tsiu-tsi *nying-kô*, feh-kʽo mông-kyi: keh-cong-ka tsi-veh z Jing-ming sô hwun-hyi-go.

<sup>15</sup> 所以阿拉該靠着<u>耶穌</u>把讚美個祭物時刻獻上撥神明，就是阿拉口裏感謝其名頭個果子。<sup>16</sup> 並且去行好事周濟人家，弗可忘記。箇種介祭物是神明所歡喜個。

<sup>17</sup> Keh-sing <sup>\[1\]</sup>ying-dao ng go cü-kwu, z kying-sing kwu-djôh ng-go ling-weng, tsing-ziang tsiang-læ iao kyʽi kao-dæ tsiang-moh ka, ng yüong i-jing gyi, deo-voh gyi; s-teh gyi hao hwun-hwun-hyi-hyi tso keh-go z-ken, hao-vong ʽæ-sing-tʽæn-kyʽi: *ziah* *feh-z* *ka*, z ü ng-lah vu-ih.

<sup>17</sup> 箇星引導爾個主顧<sup>\[1\]</sup>，是警醒顧着爾個靈魂，正像將來要去交代賬目介，爾用依順其，投伏其；使得其好歡歡喜喜做箇個事幹，好甮嗐聲嘆氣。若弗是介，是於爾拉無益。

<sup>\[1\] *ʽÔh* *wô*, ng-lah li-hyiang tso we-deo-we-nao go. 或話，爾拉裏向做爲頭爲腦個。（原註）</sup>

<sup>16</sup> Yüong tʽi ah-lah tao-kao: ing-we ah-lah tsʽeng-tsʽeng z dzeng-hao-go liang-sing, pô-feh-neng-keo væn-pah z-ken jing-kwe-dao-kyü tso-nying. <sup>19</sup> Ngô yü-kô hyüih-sing væn ng tao-kao, s-teh ngô hao soh-soh tsæ tao ng-lah-go u-sen læ.

<sup>18</sup> 用替阿拉禱告。因爲阿拉忖忖是存好個良心，巴弗能夠凡百事幹循規蹈矩做人。<sup>19</sup> 我愈加血心煩爾禱告，使得我好速速再到爾拉個烏碎來。

<sup>20</sup> Næn-kæn keh-we bing-en-go Jing-ming, z pô ah-lah Cü Yiæ-su keh-we *kʽen*-yiang-*go* do Moh-s, tsiao üong iah-go hyüih, dzong si-go cong-nyiang ta-zông-læ,—— <sup>21</sup> dæn-nyün Gyi s-teh ng-lah *teh-kyʽi* wun-djün *kyʽi* ʽang ih-tsʽih-go jün-z, neng-keo tso-dzing Gyi-go ts-i, kʽao-djôh Yiæ-su Kyi-toh pô Gyi-zi sô hwun-hyi-go *sing-siang* dzeng læ ng-lah *sing*-li. Dæn-nyün yüong-wô shü-shü-dæ-dæ kwe peh Gyi. Üô-meng.

<sup>20</sup> 難間箇位平安個神明，是把阿拉主<u>耶穌</u>箇位看羊個大牧師，照永約個血，從死個中央帶上來，——<sup>21</sup> 但願其使得爾拉德氣完全去行一切個善事，能夠做成其個旨意，靠着<u>耶穌</u> <u>基督</u>把其自所歡喜個心想存來爾拉心裏。但願榮華世世代代歸撥其。亞們。

<sup>22</sup> Hyüong-di, ngô z liah-liah sia liang-kyü peh ng; tsʽing ng-lah næ-sing tʽing ngô kyʽün-min-go shih-wô. <sup>23</sup> Ng hao hyiao-teh hyüong-di Di-mo-tʽa yi-kying fông-cʽih: gyi ziah soh-tsih ziu tao, ngô we teng gyi dong-de *læ* kyin ng-lah. <sup>24</sup> Væn ng mông-mông cong-we ying-dao ng go cü-kwu teng cong sing-du. Keh-sing Yi-da-li *hyüong-di* tu mông-mông ng. <sup>25</sup> Dæn-nyün eng-we ling-djôh ng-lah cong-nying. Üô-meng.

<sup>22</sup> 兄弟，我是略略寫兩句撥爾；請爾拉耐心聽我勸勉個說話。<sup>23</sup> 爾好曉得兄弟<u>提摩太</u>已經放出。其若速即就到，我會等其同隊來見爾拉。<sup>24</sup> 煩爾望望衆位引導爾個主顧等衆聖徒。箇星<u>以大利</u>兄弟都望望爾。<sup>25</sup> 但願恩惠臨着爾拉衆人。亞們。
